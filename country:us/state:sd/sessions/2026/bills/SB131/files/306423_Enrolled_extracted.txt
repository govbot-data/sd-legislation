AN ACT
ENTITLED An Act to revise the season in which a nonprofit organization may host a
special pheasant hunt for disabled veterans and Purple Heart recipients.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
Section 1. That § 41-11-5.5 be AMENDED:
41-11-5.5. A nonprofit organization may host a special pheasant hunt in which
disabled veterans and Purple Heart recipients of the armed forces of the United States
may participate. The special pheasant hunt may be held during the private shooting
preserve season, pursuant to § 41-10-11. No fee may be charged to participants in the
hunt, by the state or the sponsoring organization.
The Game, Fish and Parks Commission shall promulgate rules, pursuant to chapter
1-26, to administer the special pheasant hunts authorized in this section.
For the purposes of this section, "disabled veteran" means a veteran of the armed
forces of the United States who:
(1) Has been adjudicated by the United States Department of Veterans Affairs as being
twenty percent or more disabled due to a service-connected disability; or
(2) Is designated by the United States Department of Veterans Affairs to receive
Special Monthly Compensation Level K pursuant to 38 U.S.C. § 1114 (January 1,
2026).
