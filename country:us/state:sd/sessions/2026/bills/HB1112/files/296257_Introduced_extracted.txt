26.62.10 101st Legislative Session 1112
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1112
Introduced by: Representative Mulally
1 An Act to modify the definition of an electronic smoking device.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That § 34-46-1 be AMENDED:
4 34-46-1. Terms used in this chapter mean:
5 (1) "Electronic smoking device," any e-cigarette, e-cigar, e-pipe, e-hookah, or vape
6 pen, containing or delivering nicotine or any other substance with nicotine intended
7 for human consumption that, which may be used by a person in any manner for
8 the purpose of inhaling vapor or aerosol from the product;
9 (2) "Proof of age," a driver's license, nondriver identification card, tribal identification
10 card, or other generally accepted means of identification that contains a picture of
11 the individual and appears on its face to be valid;
12 (3) "Sample," tobacco products distributed to members of the general public at no cost
13 for purposes of promoting the product;
14 (4) "Sampling," the distribution of samples to members of the general public in a public
15 place;
16 (5) "Self-service display," a display that contains cigarettes or smokeless tobacco, or
17 both, and is located in an area openly accessible to the merchant's consumers, and
18 from which such consumers can readily access cigarettes or smokeless tobacco, or
19 both, without the assistance of the merchant or an employee or agent of the
20 merchant. A display case that holds tobacco products behind locked doors does not
21 constitute a self-service display;
22 (6) "Smoke" or "Smoking," the act of inhaling, exhaling, burning, or carrying any
23 lighted or heated cigar, cigarette, pipe, hookah, or any other lighted or heated
24 tobacco or plant product intended for inhalation, whether natural or synthetic, in
25 any manner or in any form, including the use of an electronic smoking device which
26 creates an aerosol or vapor, in any manner or in any form;
