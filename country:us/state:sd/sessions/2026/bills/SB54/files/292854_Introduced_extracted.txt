26.554.9 101st Legislative Session 54
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 54
Introduced by: The Chair of the Committee on Retirement Laws at the request of the South Dakota Retirement
System
1 An Act to revise the conditions for in-service distributions from the South Dakota
2 deferred compensation plan.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 3-13-78 be AMENDED:
5 3-13-78. Deferrals may be distributed only if one of the following conditions has
6 occurred:
7 (1) Severance from employment with a participating employer;
8 (2) Death of the participant;
9 (3) An unforeseeable emergency, set forth in § 3-13-82;
10 (4) Requirements are satisfied for an in-service distribution, as set forth in § 3-13-
11 83;or
12 (5) AThe participant is called to perform qualified military service for a period in excess
13 of thirty days; or
14 (6) The participant elects to receive an in-service distribution pursuant to section 2 of
15 this Act.
16 Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 3-13:
17 Notwithstanding § 3-13-83, a participant who is at least fifty-nine and one-half
18 years old may elect to receive an in-service distribution from the plan in any form
19 permitted under § 3-13-80. The receipt of an in-service distribution pursuant to this
20 section does not preclude the participant from continuing to make deferrals of
21 compensation into the plan.
Underscores indicate new language.
