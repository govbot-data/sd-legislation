26.702.20 101st Legislative Session 212
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 212
Introduced by: Senator Deibert
1 An Act to establish the homeowner tax reduction fund.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 10-13:
4 There is created in the state treasury the homeowner tax reduction fund.
5 The Department of Revenue shall administer the fund. The purpose of the fund is
6 to provide a property tax rebate for owner-occupied single-family dwellings. Interest on
7 moneys credited to the fund must remain in the fund. Expenditures from the fund must
8 be budgeted through the general appropriation bill.
9 Moneys in the fund may not be transferred to the general fund.
