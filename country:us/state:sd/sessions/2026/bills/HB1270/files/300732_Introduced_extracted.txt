26.761.26 101st Legislative Session 1270
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1270
Introduced by: Representative Fitzgerald
1 An Act to provide an apprenticeship pathway to practice law in South Dakota.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to a NEW CHAPTER in title 16:
4 The Legislature hereby creates a legal apprenticeship pathway to licensure as an
5 attorney in the state of South Dakota.
6 Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to a NEW CHAPTER in title 16:
7 The Legislature hereby creates a legal apprenticeship program if the following
8 conditions are met:
9 (1) The applicant is a graduate of the University of South Dakota School of Law;
10 (2) The applicant is a resident of the state of South Dakota at the time of
11 commencement of the apprenticeship and completion; and
12 (3) The applicant successfully completes six hundred seventy-five hours of supervised
13 apprenticeship under a qualified supervising attorney within the state of South
14 Dakota.
15 A supervising attorney may include an attorney employed by or associated with a
16 government legal department, or any attorney engaged in the private practice of law.
17 Section 3. That a NEW SECTION be added to a NEW CHAPTER in title 16:
18 A supervising attorney shall:
19 (1) Be licensed and in good standing to practice law in this state; and
20 (2) Have engaged in the full-time practice of law for not less than seven years.
21 Section 4. That a NEW SECTION be added to a NEW CHAPTER in title 16:
22 The supervising attorney shall certify that:
