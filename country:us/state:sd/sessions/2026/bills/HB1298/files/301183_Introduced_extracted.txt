26.983.9 101st Legislative Session 1298
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1298
Introduced by: Representative Roby
1 An Act to prohibit the electronic publication of certain information about a judicial
2 officer or law enforcement officer and provide a penalty therefor.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 22-11:
5 No person may publish, by electronic means, the home address, date of birth, or
6 social security number of a judicial officer or law enforcement officer:
7 (1) Without the officer's consent; and
8 (2) With the intent to:
9 (a) Place the officer in reasonable fear of death or great bodily injury; or
10 (b) Cause the officer to reasonably fear for the officer's safety or the safety of
11 the officer's immediate family.
12 A violation of this section is a Class 6 felony.
