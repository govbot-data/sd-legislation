26.965.9 101st Legislative Session 1304
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1304
Introduced by: Representative Shubeck
1 An Act to allow subdivisions to limit the operation of drones at open-air events.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 50-15:
4 In accordance with § 50-15-10, a political subdivision may enact ordinances and
5 resolutions containing reasonable and necessary limitations on the operation of drones to
6 protect privacy and prevent trespass at:
7 (1) Parades;
8 (2) Outdoor festivals;
9 (3) The state fair;
10 (4) County fairs;
11 (5) Air shows;
12 (6) Sporting events; and
13 (7) Any other open-air event that draws large numbers of people.
