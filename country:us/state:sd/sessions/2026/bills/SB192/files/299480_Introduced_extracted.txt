1 An Act to modify the blight requirements for purposes of creating a tax increment
2 financing district.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 11-9-8 be AMENDED:
5 11-9-8. The resolution required by § 11-9-5 shall must contain the following
6 findings:
7 (1) Not less than twenty-five fifty percent, by area, of the real property within the
8 district is a blighted area or and not less than fifty percent, by area, of the real
9 property within the district will stimulate and develop the general economic welfare
10 and prosperity of the state through the promotion and advancement of industrial,
11 commercial, manufacturing, agricultural, or natural resources development; and
12 (2) The improvement of the area is likely to significantly enhance the value of
13 substantially all other real property in the district.
14 It is not necessary to identify the specific parcels meeting the criteria. No county
15 may create a district located, in whole or in part, within a municipality, unless the
16 governing body of the municipality has consented to creation of a district by resolution.
17 Section 2. That § 11-9-10 be AMENDED:
18 11-9-10. For the purposes of this chapter, the term "blighted area" means an area
19 that substantially impairs or arrests the sound growth of the political subdivision, inhibits
20 housing development, constitutes an economic or social liability, or is a danger in its
21 present condition and use to the health, safety, morals, or welfare of the public because
22 of:
23 (1) The presence of a substantial number of substandard, slum, deteriorated, or
24 deteriorating structures;
25 (2) A predominance of defective or inadequate street layouts;
