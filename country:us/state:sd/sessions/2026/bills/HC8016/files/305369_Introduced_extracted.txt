26.1028.11 101st Legislative Session HC8016
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Commemoration 8016
Introduced by: Representative Derby
1 A LEGISLATIVE COMMEMORATION honoring the South Dakota Health Care
2 Association on its seventy-fifth anniversary.
3 WHEREAS, the South Dakota Health Care Association has, for the past seventy-five years,
4 focused on providing assistance and long-term care services so that individuals can continue
5 living dignified and meaningful lives; and
6 WHEREAS, the South Dakota Health Care Association has educated countless individuals,
7 families, and community leaders over the years about planning for long-term care services in
8 order to better help elderly individuals and persons with disabilities lead productive and active
9 lives; and
10 WHEREAS, the South Dakota Health Care Association is committed to continuing this
11 mission for many decades and more generations hence; and
12 WHEREAS, the South Dakota Health Care Association's Board of Directors consists of
13 President Jason Hanssen, Estelline; Secretary Laura Karlson, Rapid City; Treasurer Kasey
14 Klapprodt, Watertown; Non-Proprietary Representatives Tyler Donahue, Rapid City, and
15 Deborah Herrboldt, Sioux Falls; Proprietary Representatives Celina Block, Rapid City, and
16 Megan Kleinsasser, Wessington; Assisted Living Representative Krista Beauchamp,
17 Watertown; and Immediate Past-President Tatiana Johnson, Rapid City;
18 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT COMMEMORATED, by the One Hundred First Legislature of the
19 State of South Dakota, that the South Dakota Health Care Association is congratulated for
20 seventy-five years of life-enhancing services to elderly individuals and persons with disabilities
21 in South Dakota.
