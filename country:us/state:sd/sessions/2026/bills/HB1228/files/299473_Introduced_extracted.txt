26.679.29 101st Legislative Session 1228
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1228
Introduced by: Representative Weems
1 An Act to provide a stipend to certain members of the National Guard who donate
2 an organ for the purpose of transplantation to another individual.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 33-7:
5 If a member of the National Guard donates the member's organ for the purpose of
6 transplantation to another individual, the Department of the Military must pay a stipend
7 to the member.
8 If the member has an operation recovery time of two weeks or less, as certified by
9 a licensed medical practitioner, the stipend must equal fifty percent of the member's
10 active-duty basic monthly pay. If the member has a recovery time of more than two
11 weeks, as certified by a licensed medical practitioner, the stipend must equal one hundred
12 fifty percent of the member's active-duty basic monthly pay.
13 To be eligible for the stipend, the member shall:
14 (1) Follow all procedures and protocols established by the National Guard regarding
15 the donation of an organ by a member;
16 (2) At least one month prior to the organ procurement operation, provide the
17 Department of the Military notice of the member's intent to donate the organ; and
18 (3) Undergo the organ procurement operation at a transplant hospital, as defined in
19 § 34-26-49.
20 A member qualifies for a stipend only if the member does not receive living donor
21 paid leave from the member's employer.
