AN ACT
ENTITLED An Act to eliminate an exclusion from the definition of an agricultural
equipment dealer.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
Section 1. That § 32-39-3 be AMENDED:
32-39-3. For the purposes of this chapter, a dealer is any person whose sales of
the manufacturer's equipment is equal to or exceeds thirty percent of the dealer's gross
agricultural equipment sales during the preceding calendar year.
