26.980.10 101st Legislative Session 1263
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1263
Introduced by: Representative Jamison
1 An Act to eliminate an exclusion from the definition of an agricultural equipment
2 dealer.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 32-39-3 be AMENDED:
5 32-39-3. For the purposes of this chapter, a dealer is any person whose sales of
6 the manufacturer's equipment is equal to or exceeds thirty percent of the dealer's gross
7 agricultural equipment sales during the preceding calendar year. The term, dealer, does
8 not include a dealer whose principal business is the sale of off-road construction
9 equipment.
