26.398.10 101st Legislative Session 1022
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1022
Introduced by: The Chair of the Committee on Judiciary at the request of the Department of Public Safety
1 An Act to revise and repeal provisions related to the crime victims' compensation
2 program.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 23A-28B-28 be AMENDED:
5 23A-28B-28. The department shall receive, review, and investigate, any
6 application claim for victims' compensation which has been that is properly filed. Following
7 such review and investigation, the department shall issue an order pursuant to § 23A-
8 28B-31 that includes:
9 (1) Findings of fact;
10 (2) The amount of compensation, if any, payable under the provisions of this chapter;
11 (3) The name of any person to whom compensation is payable;
12 (4) A notice that the claimant may request commission review of the order in
13 accordance with § 23A-28B-32; and
14 (5) Any other information that the department deems necessary.
15 The department shall file any such the order with the commission and shall serve
16 notice of such the order by mailing, emailing, or hand-delivering a copy to the claimant.
17 Section 2. That § 23A-28B-31 be REPEALED.
18 For all claims submitted under the provisions of this chapter, the department shall
19 enter an order which shall include:
20 (1) Findings of fact;
21 (2) The amount of compensation, if any, payable under the provisions of this chapter;
22 (3) The name of any person to whom such compensation is payable;
23 (4) A notice that the claimant may request commission review of the order in
24 accordance with § 23A-28B-32; and
25 (5) Any other information which the department deems necessary.
