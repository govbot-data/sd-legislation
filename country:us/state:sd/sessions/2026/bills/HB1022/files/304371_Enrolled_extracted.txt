26.398.11 101st Legislative Session 1022
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1022
ENROLLED
AN ACT
ENTITLED An Act to revise and repeal provisions related to the crime victims'
compensation program.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
Section 1. That § 23A-28B-28 be AMENDED:
23A-28B-28. The department shall receive, review, and investigate any claim for
victims' compensation that is properly filed. Following review and investigation, the
department shall issue an order that includes:
(1) Findings of fact;
(2) The amount of compensation, if any, payable under the provisions of this chapter;
(3) The name of any person to whom compensation is payable;
(4) A notice that the claimant may request commission review of the order in
accordance with § 23A-28B-32; and
(5) Any other information that the department deems necessary.
The department shall file the order with the commission and serve notice of the
order by mailing, emailing, or hand-delivering a copy to the claimant.
Section 2. That § 23A-28B-31 be REPEALED.
