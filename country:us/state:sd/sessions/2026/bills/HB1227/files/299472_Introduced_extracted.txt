26.680.9 101st Legislative Session 1227
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1227
Introduced by: Representative Weems
1 An Act to increase the annual fee required of an owner of an electric motor vehicle.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That § 32-5-188 be AMENDED:
4 32-5-188. In addition to the license fees paid pursuant to the provisions of this
5 chapter for a motor vehicle, the owner of an electric motor vehicle shall pay an annual fee
6 of fifty one hundred dollars. The fee shall must be paid at the time of annual registration
7 as otherwise provided in this chapter. However, the The license fee imposed by this section
8 does not apply to any motorcycle propelled by an electric motor, if the license fee is paid
9 pursuant to § 32-5-9.
10 For the purposes of this section, the term, "electric motor vehicle," means a
11 noncommercial motor vehicle that is propelled by an electric motor that draws electricity
12 from a battery that is capable of being recharged from an external source of electricity,
13 and that which may be legally operated on the interstate highways in this state and eligible
14 for registration pursuant to the laws of this state. The electric motor vehicle shall must
15 have been made by a manufacturer primarily for use on public streets, roads, and
16 highways and meets must meet National Highway Traffic Safety Administration standards
17 included provided in 49 C.F.R. § 571 as of (January 1, 2021).
18 The fees received pursuant to this section shall must be deposited in the state
19 highway fund.
