26.232.18 101st Legislative Session 1204
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1204
Introduced by: Representative Uhre‑Balk
1 An Act to create the South Dakota school construction fund, provide for the transfer
2 of moneys to the fund, and make an appropriation therefor.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 13-24:
5 There is created the South Dakota school construction loan fund, to be
6 administered by the Department of Education, for the purpose of making loans to school
7 districts to construct or expand a school building, as provided in section 2 of this Act.
8 Any interest earned on moneys in the fund must remain in the fund. Any repayment
9 of the principal amount of a loan must be deposited into the fund and used for making
10 new loans. Money in the fund designated for loans is continuously appropriated for the
11 purposes provided in section 2 of this Act.
12 The secretary of the department shall approve vouchers, and the state auditor shall
13 draw warrants to pay expenditures authorized from this fund.
14 Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 13-24:
15 The Department of Education may make loans from the South Dakota school
16 construction fund, created in section 1 of this Act, to a school district for the purpose of
17 constructing or expanding a school building. The amount of the loan may not exceed forty
18 percent of the total cost of the construction or expansion project. The department may
19 use up to one percent of the principal amount of a loan to offset the department's expenses
20 in administering the loan.
21 Any loan made by the department must be provided to the district at an interest
22 rate of zero percent. The terms of the loan may not require collateral from the district.
23 The terms of the loan must require that the payments be based on a straight and
24 level amortization of twenty years and be made annually over a term of twenty years,
25 with the first payment due one year from the date of the funding of the loan.

9 through twelfth grade educational program.
10 Section 3. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 13-24:
11 A school district is not eligible for a loan under section 2 of this Act if:
12 (1) The school district is eligible for a loan under § 11-15-8;
13 (2) The municipality in which the district is headquartered is less than ten miles from
14 a municipality in which another school district is headquartered; or
15 (3) The school district has a fall enrollment of fewer than two hundred students and is
16 not designated as a sparse school district, as defined in § 13-13-78.
