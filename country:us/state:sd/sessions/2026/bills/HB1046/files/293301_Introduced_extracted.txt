26.559.11 101st Legislative Session 1046
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1046
Introduced by: The Chair of the House Committee on Appropriations at the request of the Department of Corrections
1 An Act to authorize the Department of Corrections to demolish the Pierre Minimum
2 Center, to make an appropriation therefor, and to declare an emergency.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. The Department of Corrections may demolish the Pierre Minimum Center, Pierre,
5 South Dakota, and remove and dispose of fixtures and structures, abate any hazardous
6 materials, and take any action reasonably necessary to restore the site to grade.
7 Section 2. There is appropriated $682,825 from the general fund, to the Department of
8 Corrections, for the purposes described in section 1 of this Act.
9 Section 3. The project authorized in section 1 of this Act shall be under the general charge
10 and supervision of the Bureau of Human Resources and Administration, as provided in chapter
11 5-14.
12 Section 4. Any amounts appropriated in this Act not lawfully expended or obligated shall
13 revert in accordance with the procedures prescribed in chapter 4-8.
14 Section 5. The secretary of the Department of Corrections shall approve vouchers and the
15 state auditor shall draw warrants to pay expenditures authorized by this Act.
16 Section 6. Whereas, this Act is necessary for the support of the state government and its
17 existing public institutions, an emergency is hereby declared to exist, and this Act shall be in
18 full force and effect from and after its passage and approval.
