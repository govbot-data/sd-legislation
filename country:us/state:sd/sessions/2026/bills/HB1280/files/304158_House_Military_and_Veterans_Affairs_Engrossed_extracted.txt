2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1280
HOUSE MILITARY AND VETERANS AFFAIRS ENGROSSED
This bill has been extensively amended (hoghoused) and may no longer be
consistent with the original intention of the sponsor.
Introduced by: Representative Jorgenson
1 An Act to increase penalties for the operation of drones over certain facilities and
2 to provide for mitigation techniques and countermeasures.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 50-15-3 be AMENDED:
5 50-15-3. No person may operate a drone over the grounds of a prison, correctional
6 facility, jail, juvenile detention facility, or any military facility unless expressly authorized
7 by the administrator thereof. A violation of this section is a Class 1 misdemeanor 6 felony.
8 Law enforcement officers may utilize mitigation techniques and countermeasures,
9 authorized in accordance with section 2 of this Act, in response to a violation of this
10 section.
11 Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 50-15:
12 The Department of Public Safety may promulgate rules, in accordance with chapter
13 1-26, to:
14 (1) Identify the nature and scope of mitigation techniques and drone countermeasures
15 that may be utilized in response to a violation of § 50-15-3; and
16 (2) Develop and implement a training program for law enforcement officers in the
17 selection and use of mitigation techniques and drone countermeasures.
