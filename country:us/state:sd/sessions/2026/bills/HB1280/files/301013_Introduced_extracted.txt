26.956.11 101st Legislative Session 1280
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1280
Introduced by: Representative Jorgenson
1 An Act to prohibit drone surveillance of state military installations, to provide a
2 penalty therefor, and to authorize law enforcement to intercept or disable any
3 drone or small unmanned aircraft threatening public safety.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
5 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 50-15:
6 No person may use a drone, small unmanned aircraft, or small unmanned aircraft
7 system, as defined in § 50-1-1, to conduct surveillance of, interfere with the operation of,
8 or photographically or electronically record a state military installation, facility, aircraft,
9 military vehicle, or weapons system, without obtaining prior written consent from the
10 military installation or facility commander.
11 A violation of this section is a Class 6 felony.
12 Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 50-15:
13 If a law enforcement officer suspects that a drone, small unmanned aircraft, or
14 small unmanned aircraft system, as defined in § 50-1-1, is engaged in any of the activities
15 prohibited in §§ 50-15-3 to 50-15-5.1, inclusive, and section 1 of this Act, the officer may
16 take reasonable and necessary mitigation measures to protect public safety. A law
17 enforcement officer may intercept or disable the drone or small unmanned aircraft by:
18 (1) Jamming;
19 (2) Hacking; or
20 (3) Physical capture.
21 No law enforcement officer may use a firearm to disable a drone or small unmanned
22 aircraft.
23 A law enforcement officer may act pursuant to this section only if there is
24 reasonable suspicion that a drone or small unmanned aircraft is an imminent threat to
25 public safety, or is otherwise in violation of state or federal law.
Underscores indicate new language.
Overstrikes indicate deleted language.
