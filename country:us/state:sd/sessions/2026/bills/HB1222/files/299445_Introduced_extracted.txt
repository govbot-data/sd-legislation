26.642.15 101st Legislative Session 1222
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1222
Introduced by: Representative Lems
1 An Act to prohibit members of the Board of Economic Development from having any
2 interest in moneys from or approved by the Board of Economic Development.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 1-16G:
5 A member of the Board of Economic Development may not have an ownership
6 interest in, or serve on the board of, any entity receiving an award, disbursement,
7 expenditure, or grant of public moneys from or approved by the Board of Economic
8 Development.
