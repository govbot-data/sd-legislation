1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION urging the people of the state of South Dakota to
2 return to the Lord Most High, the Almighty and Uncreate, and plead for His
3 mercy upon the state.

5 who worked to build up our nation trusted in the all-powerful and most merciful hand of

11 loved by God, and that each human person has been created in the image and likeness of
12 God; and
