26.1034.11 101st Legislative Session SC813
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Commemoration 813
Introduced by: Senators Reed and Crabtree and Representatives Heermann, DeGroot, Reisch, and Walburg
1 A LEGISLATIVE COMMEMORATION celebrating Solventum's expansion of its
2 Brookings, South Dakota, manufacturing facility.
3 WHEREAS, Solventum is a global healthcare innovation company dedicated to advancing
4 medical technologies, improving patient outcomes, and strengthening healthcare delivery
5 systems; and
6 WHEREAS, Solventum maintains a significant operational presence in Brookings, South
7 Dakota, and has completed a $220 million expansion of its Brookings facility, representing a
8 substantial investment in the local community and the state of South Dakota; and
9 WHEREAS, the expansion included the construction of an approximately 274,000-square-
10 foot building addition, which incorporates sixteen new loading docks, enhancing the
11 company's operational and distribution capabilities; and
12 WHEREAS, Solventum's expansion project in Brookings demonstrates a commitment to
13 the local economy and a confidence in South Dakota's prosperous business environment;
14 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT COMMEMORATED, by the One Hundred First Legislature of the
15 State of South Dakota, that on April 2, 2026, Solventum will officially open its expanded
16 facility in Brookings, South Dakota, and continue its commitment to creating innovative
17 products and providing services that transform patient experiences and positively impact
18 millions of lives.
