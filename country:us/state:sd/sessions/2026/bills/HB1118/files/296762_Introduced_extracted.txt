26.574.9 101st Legislative Session 1118
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1118
Introduced by: Representative Kolbeck (Jack)
1 An Act to make an appropriation for the design and construction of an addition to
2 the existing swine wean-to-finish barn at South Dakota State University and
3 to declare an emergency.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
5 Section 1. The Board of Regents may contract for the design, construction, furnishing, and
6 equipping of a wean-to-finish barn addition at South Dakota State University, including
7 planning, site preparation, construction, completion, furnishing, equipping, and maintaining
8 of, together with any heating, air conditioning, plumbing, water, sewer, electrical, sidewalks,
9 parking, landscaping, and envelope systems, architectural and engineering services, and any
10 other services or actions as may be required for an estimated cost of $1,720,000.
11 Section 2. There is appropriated to the Board of Regents, for the purposes authorized in this
12 Act, the sum of $1,720,000, in other fund expenditure authority for donated moneys, together
13 with any additional sums received pursuant to section 4 of this Act and permitted adjustments
14 pursuant to section 3 of this Act.
15 Section 3. The cost estimate contained in this Act is stated in terms of 2026 values. The
16 Board of Regents may adjust the cost estimate to reflect inflation, as measured by the Building
17 Cost Index and reported by the Engineering News Record, and additional expenditures
18 required to comply with regulations adopted after the effective date of this Act, or additional
19 sums received pursuant to section 4 of this Act. Any adjustment to the construction cost
20 estimate for the project may not exceed one hundred twenty-five percent of the estimated
21 project construction cost stated in section 1 of this Act.
22 Section 4. The Board of Regents may accept, transfer, and expend any moneys obtained for
23 the project authorized in this Act from federal sources, donations, or any other external
24 sources, all of which comprise a special fund for the benefited project. All moneys deposited
