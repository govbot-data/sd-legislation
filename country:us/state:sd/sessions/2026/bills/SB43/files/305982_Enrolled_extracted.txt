Senate Bill 43
ENROLLED
AN ACT
ENTITLED An Act to address search and seizure provisions applicable to digital
currency.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
Section 1. That § 23A-35-14 be AMENDED:
23A-35-14. This chapter does not modify any inconsistent provision of this code
regulating search, seizure, and the issuance and execution of search warrants in
circumstances for which special provision is made.
For purposes of this chapter, "property" includes documents, books, papers, digital
currency, and any other tangible objects.
Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 23A-35:
For purposes of this chapter, "digital currency" means a digital representation of
value recorded on a cryptographically secured distributed ledger or other medium of
exchange, including blockchain, or similar technology.
