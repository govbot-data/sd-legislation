26.457.13 101st Legislative Session 43
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 43
Introduced by: The Chair of the Committee on Judiciary at the request of the Attorney General
1 An Act to address search and seizure provisions applicable to digital currency.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That § 23A-35-14 be AMENDED:
4 23A-35-14. This chapter does not modify any inconsistent provision of this code
5 regulating search, seizure, and the issuance and execution of search warrants in
6 circumstances for which special provision is made.
7 The term For purposes of this chapter, "property" as used in this chapter includes
8 documents, books, papers, digital currency, and any other tangible objects.
9 Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 23A-35:
10 For purposes of this chapter, "digital currency" means a digital representation of
11 value recorded on a cryptographically secured distributed ledger or other medium of
12 exchange, including blockchain, or similar technology.
Underscores indicate new language.
