26.1049.9 101st Legislative Session SC826
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Commemoration 826
Introduced by: Senators Foster and Perry and Representative Muckey
1 A LEGISLATIVE COMMEMORATION celebrating the Wall High School Eagles as the
2 2025 Class 9A State Football Champions.
3 WHEREAS, the Wall High School Eagles completed a record-setting, undefeated 2025
4 season, in which the 12-0 team scored 656 total points, established a new South Dakota Class
5 9A single-season scoring record, and commanded a 60-6 victory over the Howard Tigers to
6 capture the South Dakota High School Activities Association Class 9A State Football
7 Championship; and
8 WHEREAS, Wall's explosive, record-setting offense overwhelmed their championship
9 opponent with 478 total yards, a 45-yard touchdown run by Kean Hofer, quarterback Tanner
10 Volmer's four touchdown passes and two touchdown runs, and 211 rushing yards and three
11 touchdowns by senior running back Jace Mohr, who was named the Joe Robbie Most Valuable
12 Player and earned distinction as the Outstanding Back; and
13 WHEREAS, each of the team's impressive linemen and other defensive players also played
14 an indispensable role in the championship game by controlling the line of scrimmage and
15 limiting Howard to just 78 yards of total offense; and
16 WHEREAS, Head Coach Lex Heathershaw and Assistant Coaches Garrett Bryan, Preston
17 Eisenbraun, Chris Bessette, and Ryan Dinger, along with the athletic staff, school
18 administrators, and the entire Wall community, demonstrated exceptional leadership,
19 preparation, and support, fostering a culture of accountability, resilience, discipline, and
20 sportsmanship on and off the field;
21 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT COMMEMORATED, by the One Hundred First Legislature of the
22 State of South Dakota, that the Wall High School Eagles are the 2025 Class 9A State Football
23 Champions, and are commended for their extraordinary season, championship
24 accomplishment, and the pride they have brought to their school and community.
