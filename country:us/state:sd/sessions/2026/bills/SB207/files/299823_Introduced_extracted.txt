Introduced by: Senator Voita
1 An Act to require a vote to approve the creation of certain tax increment financing
2 districts.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 11-9-5 be AMENDED:
5 11-9-5. To establish create a district, the governing body must adopt a resolution
6 that:
7 (1) Describes the boundaries of a the district with sufficient definiteness to identify
8 with ordinary and reasonable certainty the territory included. The boundaries may
9 not split a whole unit of property that is being used for a single purpose;
10 (2) Creates the district on Specifies a given date for the creation of a district;
11 (3) Includes a finding that the assessed value of the taxable property in the district
12 plus the tax increment base of all other existing districts does not exceed ten
13 percent of the total assessed value of all taxable property in the political
14 subdivision; and
15 (4) Assigns a name to the district for identification purposes. The first district created
16 in each political subdivision must be known as "Tax Increment Financing District
17 Number One, City (or Town, or County) of __________." Each subsequently
18 created district must be assigned the next consecutive number.
19 A resolution adopted under this section is subject to a referendum under the
20 provisions of section 2 of this Act
21 Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 11-9:
22 If the estimated project costs of a proposed district are greater than fifteen million
23 dollars, the governing body creating the district must call a special election to determine
24 the question of the establishment of the district. Except as provided below, the election
