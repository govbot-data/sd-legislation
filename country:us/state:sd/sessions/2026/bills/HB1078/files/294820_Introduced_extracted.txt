7 24:43:11:02. General requirements for high school graduation. The units

13 (c) One unit credit of literature that must include, including one-half unit
14 credit of American literature;
15 (2) Three units or more credits of social studies that, which must include the
16 following:
17 (a) One unit credit of U.S. history; and
18 (b) One-half unit credit of U.S. government;
19 (3) Three units or more credits of mathematics that, which must include one unit
20 of algebra I;
21 (4) Three units or more credits of science that, which must include one unit of
22 biology;
