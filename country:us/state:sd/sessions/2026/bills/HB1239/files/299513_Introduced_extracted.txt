1 An Act to require that the Department of Education provide for the compensation of
2 a teacher employed by a school district.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 13-10-2 be AMENDED:
5 13-10-2. The school board shall have the power to Except as otherwise provided
6 in section 2 of this Act, the board of a school district may employ any personnel deemed
7 necessary by the board for the functioning of the school and to may define the duties and
8 fix the compensation of each employee.
9 Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 13-10:
10 Notwithstanding the provisions of § 13-10-2, the board of a school district may not
11 employ a number of teachers in excess of the district's target teacher ratio factor, as
12 established in § 13-13-10.1.
13 The Department of Education shall, with moneys appropriated in the general
14 appropriation bill, pay the salary and benefits of each teacher employed by a school
15 district.
16 The South Dakota Board of Education Standards shall promulgate rules, pursuant
17 to chapter 1-26, establishing a salary schedule and benefits package for teachers. The
18 salary schedule must begin with the target teacher salary, established in § 13-13-10.1,
19 and must provide for increases to a teacher's salary that reflect:
20 (1) The demand for the teacher's area of certification;
21 (2) The highest degree and additional credit hours held by the teacher; and
22 (3) The teacher's years of experience.
23 The benefits package of each teacher employed by a school district must equal at
24 least twenty-nine percent of the teacher's salary.
