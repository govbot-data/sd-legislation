26.279.11 101st Legislative Session 1064
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1064
Introduced by: Representative Shubeck
1 An Act to provide for the sale of producer-raised meat and meat food products
2 directly to consumers pending legalization under federal law.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 39-5:
5 Except as prohibited by federal law and subject to section 2 of this Act, and
6 notwithstanding any other provision of this chapter, a producer may sell meat or meat
7 food products from cattle, sheep, swine, or goats, which were raised by the producer and
8 slaughtered on the premises of the producer or at a custom exempt plant, if:
9 (1) The meat or meat food product is produced from an animal that was raised,
10 slaughtered, and processed in this state;
11 (2) The meat or meat food product is sold directly to an end consumer in this state;
12 and
13 (3) A label is affixed to the meat or meat food product packaging at the time of sale
14 stating that:
15 (a) The meat or meat food product has not been inspected and is not regulated;
16 and
17 (b) The consumer agrees not to sell, donate, or commercially redistribute the
18 meat or meat food product.
19 Section 2. Section 1 of this Act is effective on the date that the attorney general certifies to
20 code counsel that the sale of any meat or meat food product, in accordance with section 1 of
21 this Act, is legalized under federal law. Legalization under federal law consists of either the
22 passage of a federal law that allows direct‑to‑consumer sales of uninspected meat or meat
23 food products or the issuance of a final decision, by a federal court with jurisdiction over this
24 state, which declares that the federal prohibition on direct‑to‑consumer sales is
25 unconstitutional or otherwise invalid.
