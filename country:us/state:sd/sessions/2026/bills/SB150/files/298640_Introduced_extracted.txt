26.447.16 101st Legislative Session 150
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 150
Introduced by: Senator Larson
1 An Act to exempt one motor vehicle of a debtor from being taken by legal process.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That § 43-45-4 be AMENDED:
4 43-45-4. In addition to the any property provided for in §§ 43-45-2 and 43-45-3,
5 the debtor, if the head of a family, may, each debtor, personally, or by an agent or
6 attorney, may select as exempt property, pursuant to § 43-45-1, from all other of the
7 debtor's other personal property, that is not absolutely exempt, goods, chattels,:
8 (1) Chattels, goods, merchandise, money, or other personal property not to exceed
9 exceeding, in the aggregate seven:
10 (a) Seven thousand dollars in value; and, if not the head of a family, property
11 as aforesaid of the value of five, if the debtor is the head of a family; or
12 (b) Five thousand dollars, which is also in value, if the debtor is not the head
13 of a family; and
14 (2) One motor vehicle, as defined in § 32-5-1, not exceeding five thousand dollars
15 over any security interest on the motor vehicle.
16 The property selected pursuant to this section exempt, and which shall must be
17 chosen and appraised as provided by law.
