1 An Act to prohibit the use of automatic tabulating equipment and electronic ballot
2 marking systems.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 12-1:
5 The State Board of Elections may not authorize the use of any automatic tabulating
6 equipment or electronic ballot marking system.
7 The governing body of a political subdivision having supervision of an election may
8 not adopt, implement, or use any automatic tabulating or electronic ballot marking system
9 in an election.

16 independent candidates occupy a separate column under the heading, independent
17 candidates. In precincts using automatic tabulating systems, the ballot information,
