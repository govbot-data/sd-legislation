2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That § 34-26-67 be AMENDED:
4 34-26-67. (a) The Department of Public Safety shall establish or contract for the
5 establishment of a donor registry.
6 (b) The department, in partnership with the federally-designated organ
7 procurement organization and any nonprofit tissue or eye donation agency which that
8 holds agreements with hospitals in South Dakota, shall compile, operate, maintain, and
9 modify the registry as necessary. The registry shall must include pertinent information on
10 all South Dakotans who have registered to donate organs, tissues, and eyes, and all
11 relevant information regarding a donor's making amendment to, or revocation of, an
12 anatomical gift in accordance with the Revised Uniform Anatomical Gift Act ( §§ 34-26-48
13 to 34-26-72, inclusive).
14 (c) A donor registry must:
15 (1) Allow a donor, or other person authorized under by § 34-26-51, to include on the
16 donor registry a statement or symbol that the donor has made, amended, or
17 revoked an anatomical gift;
18 (2) Allow a donor or an individual, or other person authorized by § 34-26-51, online
19 access to register:
20 (A) Register as a donor, to amend an;
21 (B) Amend the individual's existing donor status, or remove an;
22 (C) Remove the individual's existing donor registration seven days a
23 week on a twenty-four hour basis; and
24 (D) Verify the individual's donor registration status;
25 (3) Be accessible electronically to the federally designated organ procurement
26 organization and any nonprofit tissue or eye donation agency which that holds
