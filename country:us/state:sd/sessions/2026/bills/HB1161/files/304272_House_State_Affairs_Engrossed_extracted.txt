1 An Act to amend provisions related to changing rooms, sleeping quarters, and
2 restrooms.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 20-13-59 be AMENDED:
5 20-13-59. Except as otherwise provided in this section and §§ 13-32-22 and 13-
6 32-23, the state or a political subdivision of this state must designate, as being for the
7 exclusive use of females or males, any multi-occupancy changing room, restroom, or
8 sleeping quarters in:
9 (1) A building or facility owned by the state or by a political subdivision of this state;
10 or
11 (2) A space leased to or occupied by the state or by a political subdivision of this state.
12 This section does not apply to any room used exclusively by firefighters or law
13 enforcement personnel acting in an official capacity.
14 Section 2. That § 20-13-61 be AMENDED:
15 20-13-61. The prohibitions set forth in § 20-13-60 do not apply to:
16 (1) An individual who accompanies a child under the age of ten, if the individual is the
17 child's parent or lawful chaperone;
18 (2) An individual who accompanies another individual requiring assistance because of
19 age or a disability;
20 (3) A law enforcement officer, fire official, firefighter, or other state or county
21 employee of the state or a political subdivision who is acting in an official capacity;
22 (4) An individual providing emergency medical assistance;
23 (5) An individual providing custodial, maintenance, or inspection services, provided the
24 changing room or restroom is unoccupied; or
25 (6) An individual providing services or rendering aid:
