26.617.17 101st Legislative Session 208
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 208
Introduced by: Senator Hulse
1 An Act to require the award of certain disbursements to prevailing owners and
2 taxpayers in appeals of property assessments.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 10-11-45.1 be AMENDED:
5 10-11-45.1. The circuit court may award disbursements, including reasonable
6 attorneys' attorney fees, in an action brought to circuit court pursuant to this chapter by
7 any appellant relative to the assessment of property, if the appellant does not prevail in
8 its the appeal of the property assessment.
9 If an aggrieved owner or taxpayer prevails in the appeal, the court must award
10 disbursements and reasonable attorney fees to the owner or taxpayer.
11 Section 2. That § 10-11-78 be AMENDED:
12 10-11-78. On motion, the Supreme Court may award reasonable attorneys' fees
13 in In an action brought to the Supreme Court of South Dakota, pursuant to this chapter,
14 against any appellant relative to the assessment of property, the court, upon motion:
15 (1) May award reasonable attorney fees, if the appellant does not prevail in its the
16 appeal of the property assessment. The motion shall be accompanied by counsel's;
17 or
18 (2) Must award reasonable attorney fees to any aggrieved owner or taxpayer that
19 prevails in the appeal.
20 The attorney for the moving party shall file a verified, itemized statement of costs
21 incurred, and legal services rendered, with the motion under this section.
