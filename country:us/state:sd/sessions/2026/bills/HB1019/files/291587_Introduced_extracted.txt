1 An Act to remove knowledge and intent from certain actions involving pesticide
2 laws.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 38-20A-13 be AMENDED:
5 38-20A-13. If the secretary determines, based on the preponderance of evidence,
6 that a registered pesticide or its labeling does not comply with the provisions of this
7 chapter or rules promulgated under this chapter, the secretary may cancel the registration
8 of that pesticide, subject to a hearing under the provisions of chapter 1-26.
9 Section 2. That § 38-20A-26 be AMENDED:
10 38-20A-26. No person may knowingly or willfully distribute, sell, or offer for sale
11 within this state any pesticide which that is adulterated or misbranded, or any device
12 which that is misbranded.
13 Section 3. That § 38-20A-27 be AMENDED:
14 38-20A-27. No person may knowingly or willfully distribute, sell, or offer for sale
15 within this state any:
16 (1) Any pesticide which that is not registered under the provisions of §§ 38-20A-4 to
17 38-20A-13, inclusive; or any
18 (2) Any pesticide, if any of the claims made or any of the directions for the pesticide's
19 use differ in substance from the representations made in connection with its
20 registration; or any
21 (3) Any pesticide, if the composition of the pesticide differs from the composition
22 represented in its registration.
