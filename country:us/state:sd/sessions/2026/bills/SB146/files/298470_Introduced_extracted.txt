1 An Act to clarify the effect of the resignation of certain gubernatorial appointees.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That § 3-4-11 be AMENDED:
4 3-4-11. If any position on a board or commission whose members are appointed
5 by the Governor with the consent of the Senate becomes vacant pursuant to § 3-4-1, the
6 Governor may make an interim appointment to fill the vacancy, but any and the Senate
7 must act upon the appointment. The interim appointment expires when the Senate acts
8 upon the appointee's nomination.
9 If the vacancy is any position on a board or commission whose members are
10 appointed by the Governor with the consent of the Senate becomes vacant as the result
11 of a resignation, the person who has resigned remains must continue to serve on the
12 board or commission until the earlier of:
13 (1) The Governor appoints a appointing another person with the consent of the Senate
14 or makes;
15 (2) The Governor making an interim appointment to fill the vacancy or until, which
16 must be acted upon by the Senate; or
17 (3) Until a date set in writing by the person who resigned, whichever is sooner.
18 If a person's term on the board or commission has expired, the person remains on
19 the board or commission until the Governor appoints another person and the Senate
20 consents to the appointment of the Governor's nominee or until the Governor reappoints
21 the same person and the Senate acts upon consents to the renomination of the person
22 whose term has expired.
23 If a person's term on a board or commission has expired and the Governor does
24 not nominate a another person for the new term by the tenth legislative day after the
25 Legislature convenes, the person whose term has expired is deemed to be renominated
26 for the new term and the Senate may shall act on the nomination accordingly. If a person
