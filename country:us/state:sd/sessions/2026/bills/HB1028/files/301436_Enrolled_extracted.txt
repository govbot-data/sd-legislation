House Bill 1028
ENROLLED
AN ACT
ENTITLED An Act to establish a criminal background check requirement for
multistate licensure under the social work licensure compact.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
Section 1. That § 36-26-13 be AMENDED:
36-26-13. The board shall issue a license as a certified social worker, a social
worker, or a social work associate, or a multistate license as provided in § 36-26A-1.
Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 36-26:
An applicant for a multistate license, as provided in § 36-26A-1, shall submit to
the board a full set of the applicant's fingerprints in a form and manner prescribed by the
board. The board shall deliver the fingerprints to the Division of Criminal Investigation for
a state and federal criminal background check by the division and the Federal Bureau of
Investigation. The applicant shall sign a release of information to the board, and pay any
fees charged for the fingerprinting and the background check.
Upon completion of the background check, the division shall deliver to the board
the applicant's criminal history record information. The board shall consider this
information in determining whether to issue a multistate license to the applicant. The
board may not issue a multistate license to the applicant before receiving this information.
The board may not disseminate any information obtained under this section to a person
not employed by the board.
