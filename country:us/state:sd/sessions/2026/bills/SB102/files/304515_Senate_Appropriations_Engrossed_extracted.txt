4 42-7B-48. There is established within the state treasury the South Dakota Gaming

12 (1) Forty percent of the gaming tax collected shall be transferred to the tourism

16 litigation and enforcement of this chapter and chapter 42-7 and for grants as

21 municipal proceeds and shall be disbursed at least quarterly to the City of
