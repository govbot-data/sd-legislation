26.1019.9 101st Legislative Session SC808
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Commemoration 808
Introduced by: Senators Schoenfish, Deibert, and Perry and Representative Muckey
1 A LEGISLATIVE COMMEMORATION commending and honoring Richard Schlosser, a
2 member of the Frederick Area School District Board of Education, on his 25
3 years of school board service.
4 WHEREAS, the 2025-2026 school year marks Richard Schlosser's 25th year of school board
5 service and he was honored by the Association of School Boards of South Dakota with the
6 Distinguished Service Award; and
7 WHEREAS, Richard has displayed an exceptional commitment to school board work
8 throughout his tenure on the Frederick Area School Board; and
9 WHEREAS, Richard's years of service on his local school board is an exemplary display of
10 dedication to the students, staff members, and community of the Frederick Area School
11 District;
12 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT COMMEMORATED, by the One Hundred First Legislature of the
13 State of South Dakota, that Richard Schlosser of Frederick, South Dakota, is commended on
14 his past, present, and future efforts to enhance educational opportunities for the K-12
15 students of this state.
