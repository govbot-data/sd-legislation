26.655.10 101st Legislative Session 1115
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1115
Introduced by: Representative Auch
1 An Act to revise a provision related to unauthorized possession of a controlled drug
2 or substance.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 22-42-5 be AMENDED:
5 22-42-5. No person may knowingly possess a controlled drug or substance unless
6 the substance was obtained directly or pursuant to a valid prescription or order from a
7 practitioner, while acting in the course of the practitioner's professional practice or except
8 as otherwise authorized by chapter 34-20B. A charge for unauthorized possession of
9 controlled substance when absorbed into the human body as set forth in subdivision 22-
10 42-1(1) shall only be charged under the provisions of § 22-42-5.1. A violation of this
11 section for a substance in Schedules I or II is a Class 5 felony. A violation of this section
12 for a substance in Schedule III and IV is a Class 6 felony.
