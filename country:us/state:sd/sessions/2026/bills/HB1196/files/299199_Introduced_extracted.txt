12 (1) Has been registered, or has been required to register, as a federal lobbyist within
13 the five years immediately prior to being appointed a delegate;
14 (2) Has been a federal employee or contractor at any time within the ten years
15 immediately prior to being appointed a delegate;
16 (3) Has held an elected or appointed federal office at any time within the ten years
17 immediately prior to being appointed a delegate;
18 (4) Has been convicted of a crime involving moral turpitude, as defined in § 22-1-2, in
19 any jurisdiction;
20 (6) Has been convicted of a felony in any jurisdiction within the ten years immediately
21 prior to being appointed a delegate; or
22 (7) Holds a statewide office.

24 enlisted as a member of the armed forces, as defined in § 36-1-2.
