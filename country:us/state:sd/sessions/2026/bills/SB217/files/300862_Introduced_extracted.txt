26.999.20 101st Legislative Session 217
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 217
Introduced by: Senator Otten
1 An Act to make an appropriation for a fire management officer position within the
2 Department of Public Safety.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. There is appropriated $750,000 from the general fund, to the Department of Public
5 Safety, for a fire management officer position to support Division of Wildland Fire operations
6 east of the Missouri River.
7 Section 2. The secretary of the Department of Public Safety shall approve vouchers and the
8 state auditor shall draw warrants to pay expenditures authorized in this Act.
9 Section 3. Any amounts appropriated in this Act not lawfully expended or obligated shall
10 revert in accordance with the procedures prescribed in chapter 4-8.
11 Section 4. This Act is effective beginning June 30, 2026.
