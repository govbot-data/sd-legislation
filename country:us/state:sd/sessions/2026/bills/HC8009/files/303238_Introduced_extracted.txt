26.1016.9 101st Legislative Session HC8009
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Commemoration 8009
Introduced by: Representatives Greenfield, Muckey, and Van Diepen and Senators Deibert and Perry
1 A LEGISLATIVE COMMEMORATION commending and honoring Brad Olson, a
2 member of the Aberdeen School District Board of Education, on his twenty-
3 five years of school board service.
4 WHEREAS, the 2025-26 school year marks Brad Olson’s twenty-fifth year of school board
5 service, and the Associated School Boards of South Dakota honored him with the
6 Distinguished Service Award; and
7 WHEREAS, Brad has displayed an exceptional commitment to school board work
8 throughout his tenure on the school board; and
9 WHEREAS, Brad’s years of service on the Aberdeen School District Board provide an
10 exemplary display of dedication to the students, staff members, and the community of the
11 Aberdeen School District;
12 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT COMMEMORATED, by the One Hundred First Legislature of the
13 State of South Dakota, that Brad Olson, of Aberdeen, South Dakota, deserves honor and
14 respect for his continuing efforts to enhance the educational opportunities for K-12 students
15 in this state.
