House Commemoration 8009
Introduced by: Representatives Greenfield, Muckey, Novstrup, and Van Diepen and Senators Crabtree, Deibert,
Duhamel, and Perry
A LEGISLATIVE COMMEMORATION commending and honoring Brad Olson, a
member of the Aberdeen School District Board of Education, on his twenty-
five years of school board service.
WHEREAS, the 2025-26 school year marks Brad Olson’s twenty-fifth year of school board
service, and the Associated School Boards of South Dakota honored him with the
Distinguished Service Award; and
WHEREAS, Brad has displayed an exceptional commitment to school board work
throughout his tenure on the school board; and
WHEREAS, Brad’s years of service on the Aberdeen School District Board provide an
exemplary display of dedication to the students, staff members, and the community of the
Aberdeen School District;
NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT COMMEMORATED, by the One Hundred First Legislature of the
State of South Dakota, that Brad Olson, of Aberdeen, South Dakota, deserves honor and
respect for his continuing efforts to enhance the educational opportunities for K-12 students
in this state.
