2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 37
Introduced by: The Chair of the Senate Committee on Appropriations at the request of the Department of Agriculture
and Natural Resources
1 An Act to make appropriations for water and environmental purposes and to declare
2 an emergency.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. There is appropriated $750,000 in other fund expenditure authority, from the
5 South Dakota water and environment fund, to the Board of Water and Natural Resources,
6 for the purpose of providing a grant to local project sponsors for a feasibility-level study,
7 system startup, and administration of the Dakota Mainstem regional water system study.
8 Moneys must be provided according to terms and conditions established by the Board of
9 Water and Natural Resources.
10 Section 2. There is appropriated $500,000, in other fund expenditure authority, from the
11 South Dakota water and environment fund, to the Board of Water and Natural Resources,
12 for the purpose of providing a grant to the Department of Agriculture and Natural
13 Resources for hydrology and water management studies to assess, model, and quantify
14 the state’s surface water and groundwater resources. The activities to be funded include
15 the development of a publicly accessible high-resolution map to delineate wetlands
16 throughout the state.
17 Notwithstanding § 46A-1-61, the board may provide the grant for up to one hundred
18 percent of the nonfederal share of expenditures for hydrology and water management
19 studies.
20 Moneys must be provided according to terms and conditions established by the Board of
21 Water and Natural Resources.
22 Section 3. There is appropriated $175,000, in other fund expenditure authority, from the
23 South Dakota water and environment fund, to the Board of Water and Natural Resources,
