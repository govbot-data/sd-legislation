26.1015.11 101st Legislative Session HC8008
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Commemoration 8008
Introduced by: Representative Overweg
1 A LEGISLATIVE COMMEMORATION honoring Ashlyn Koupal of Wagner for being
2 named to the 2026 McDonald's All-American Girls Basketball Team.
3 WHEREAS, Ashlyn Koupal, a senior at Wagner High School, has achieved national
4 recognition by being selected to the prestigious McDonald's All-American Girls Basketball
5 Team, one of the highest honors in high school basketball, recognizing the top players in the
6 nation who demonstrate exceptional skill, leadership, and sportsmanship; and
7 WHEREAS, Ashlyn Koupal becomes only the second South Dakotan ever selected to
8 participate in the McDonald's All-American Game and the first female athlete from South
9 Dakota to earn this distinction, which marks a historic achievement both for her community
10 and the state; and
11 WHEREAS, throughout her career at Wagner High School, Ashlyn has demonstrated
12 outstanding athletic achievement, dedication, perseverance, and leadership in addition to
13 serving as a role model to her teammates and young athletes across South Dakota; and
14 WHEREAS, Ashlyn Koupal has committed to the University of Nebraska to continue her
15 academic and athletic careers, where she will play for Head Coach Amy Williams, of South
16 Dakota, and both Ashlyn and Coach Williams will demonstrate at the collegiate level the values
17 and work ethic of this state; and
18 WHEREAS, Ashlyn's accomplishment brings great pride to the Wagner community and to
19 the entire State of South Dakota;
20 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT COMMEMORATED, by the One Hundred First Legislature of the
21 State of South Dakota, that Ashlyn Koupal is recognized for her outstanding achievement in
22 being named to the 2026 McDonald's All-American Girls Basketball Team.
