26.242.22 101st Legislative Session 1125
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1125
Introduced by: Representative Jorgenson
1 An Act to create a taskforce to study the impact of artificial intelligence systems on
2 the state.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. There is established a taskforce to study the continued technological development
5 and impact of artificial intelligence systems on the state. The taskforce shall consist of:
6 (1) The following members, appointed by the Executive Board of the Legislative
7 Research Council:
8 (a) Two members of the House of Representatives;
9 (b) Two members of the Senate;
10 (c) One member representing the health care industry;
11 (d) One member representing the banking and finance industry;
12 (e) One member representing the retail industry;
13 (f) One member representing the manufacturing industry;
14 (g) One member representing the technology industry;
15 (h) One individual employed by a school district as a teacher;
16 (i) One individual employed by a school district as an administrator;
17 (j) The auditor of a county with a population of fifteen thousand residents or
18 more;
19 (k) The auditor of a county with a population that is less than fifteen thousand
20 residents;
21 (l) An official or employee of a municipality with a population of twenty
22 thousand residents or more; and
23 (m) An official or employee of a municipality with a population of fewer than
24 twenty thousand residents; and
25 (2) One member appointed by the Governor;
26 (3) One member appointed by the chief justice of the Supreme Court; and

1 (4) One member appointed by the Board of Regents.

10 to the executive board.
11 Section 2. This Act is effective beginning January 1, 2027.
