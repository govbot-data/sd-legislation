26.1018.9 101st Legislative Session HC8011
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Commemoration 8011
Introduced by: Representatives Greenfield, Muckey, and Van Diepen and Senators Deibert and Perry
1 A LEGISLATIVE COMMEMORATION commending and honoring Greg Blue, a member
2 of the Iroquois School District Board of Education, on his thirty-five years of
3 school board service.
4 WHEREAS, the 2025-26 school year marks Greg Blue’s thirty-fifth year of school board
5 service, and the Associated School Boards of South Dakota honored him with the
6 Distinguished Service Award; and
7 WHEREAS, Greg has displayed an exceptional commitment to school board work
8 throughout his tenure on his local school board; and
9 WHEREAS, Greg’s years of service on the Iroquois School District Board of Education
10 provide an exemplary display of dedication to the students, staff members, and community
11 of the Iroquois School District;
12 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT COMMEMORATED, by the One Hundred First Legislature of the
13 State of South Dakota, that Greg Blue, of Cavour, South Dakota, deserves honor and respect
14 for his continuing efforts to enhance the educational opportunities for K-12 students in this
15 state.
