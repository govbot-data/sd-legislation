26.578.11 101st Legislative Session 1041
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1041
Introduced by: The Chair of the House Committee on Appropriations at the request of the Department of the Military
1 An Act to authorize the expansion and renovation of the Sturgis Readiness Center,
2 to make an appropriation therefor, and to declare an emergency.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. The Department of the Military may contract for the construction, completion,
5 furnishing, equipping, and maintenance of the expansion and renovation of the Sturgis
6 Readiness Center, including heating, plumbing, water, sewer, electric facilities, architectural
7 and engineering services, and any other services or actions necessary for this purpose.
8 Section 2. There is appropriated $2,350,000 from the general fund and $8,000,000 in federal
9 fund expenditure authority, to the Department of the Military, for the purposes authorized in
10 section 1 of this Act.
11 Section 3. The administration of the design and construction of the project authorized in this
12 Act shall be under the general charge and supervision of the Bureau of Human Resources and
13 Administration as provided in chapter 5-14.
14 Section 4. The adjutant general of the Department of the Military shall approve vouchers and
15 the state auditor shall draw warrants to pay expenditures authorized in this Act.
16 Section 5. Any amounts appropriated in this Act not lawfully expended or obligated by June
17 30, 2031, shall revert in accordance with the procedures prescribed in chapter 4-8.
18 Section 6. Whereas, this Act is necessary for the support of the state government and its
19 existing public institutions, an emergency is hereby declared to exist, and this Act shall be in
20 full force and effect from and after its passage and approval.
