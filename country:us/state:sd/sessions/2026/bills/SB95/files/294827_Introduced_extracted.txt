26.353.13 101st Legislative Session 95
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 95
Introduced by: Senator Nelson
1 An Act to require that a medical cannabis cardholder provide a card or card number
2 upon request of a law enforcement officer.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 34-20G-2 be AMENDED:
5 34-20G-2. A cardholder is not subject to arrest, prosecution, or penalty of any
6 kind, or denial of any right or privilege, including any civil penalty or disciplinary action by
7 a court or occupational or professional licensing board or bureau, and may not be denied
8 any right or privilege for:
9 (l) The medical use of cannabis in accordance with this chapter, if the provided that:
10 (a) The cardholder does not possess more than the allowable amount of
11 cannabis, and if any; and
12 (b) Any cannabis plant possessed by the cardholder is either cultivated in an
13 enclosed, locked facility, or is being transported;
14 (2) Reimbursement by a registered qualifying patient to the patient's registered
15 designated caregiver for direct costs incurred by the registered designated
16 caregiver for assisting with the registered qualifying patient's medical use of
17 cannabis;
18 (3) Transferring the cannabis to a testing facility;
19 (4) Compensating a dispensary or a testing facility for goods or services provided;
20 (5) Selling, transferring, or delivering cannabis seeds produced by the a cardholder to
21 a cultivation facility or dispensary; or
22 (6) Offering or providing cannabis to a cardholder for a registered qualifying patient's
23 medical use, to a nonresident cardholder, or to a dispensary if, provided that
24 nothing of value is transferred in return, and the person cardholder giving the
25 cannabis does not knowingly cause the recipient to possess more than the
26 allowable amount of cannabis.
Underscores indicate new language.
Overstrikes indicate deleted language.
