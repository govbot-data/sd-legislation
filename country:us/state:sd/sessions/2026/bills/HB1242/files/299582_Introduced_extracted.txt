26.861.9 101st Legislative Session 1242
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1242
Introduced by: Representative Jensen (Phil)
1 An Act to require emergency personnel to use flares or emergency reflective
2 triangles to mark stopped emergency vehicles in some circumstances.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 32-31-3 be AMENDED:
5 32-31-3. The exemptions granted in subdivisions 32-31-2(2) and (3) to an
6 authorized emergency vehicle apply only if the vehicle is making use of audible or visual
7 signals meeting the requirements of law. However, the
8 The exemption granted in subdivision 32-31-2(1) to an authorized emergency
9 vehicle applies only if the driver of the emergency vehicle is making use of:
10 (1) Using visual signals meeting the requirements of law; and
11 (2) Has placed, as soon as practicable after stopping, three flares or emergency
12 reflective triangles on the roadway, in the manner and as required by section 2 of
13 this Act.
14 Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 32-31:
15 The driver of an emergency vehicle that is stopped to respond to an emergency
16 must mark the location by placing three flares or emergency reflective triangles on the
17 roadway if:
18 (1) Visibility is reduced due to:
19 (a) Weather;
20 (b) Terrain;
21 (c) Time of day;
22 (2) Road conditions at the time of the emergency reduce a driver's ability to slow or
23 stop a motor vehicle; or
24 (3) The nature of the emergency requires emergency vehicles to be at the location for
25 an extended period of time.
Underscores indicate new language.
Overstrikes indicate deleted language.
