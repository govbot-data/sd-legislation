26.768.9 101st Legislative Session 1129
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1129
Introduced by: Representative Rice
1 An Act to provide for the carrying of a concealed pistol by certain school district
2 employees.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 13-32-7 be AMENDED:
5 13-32-7. Any person individual who intentionally carries, possesses, stores,
6 keeps, leaves, places, or puts into the possession of another person, any dangerous
7 weapon, firearm, or air gun, whether or not the firearm or air gun is designed, adapted,
8 used, or intended to be used primarily for imitative or noisemaking purposes, on or in any
9 public elementary or secondary school premises, vehicle, or building, or on or in any
10 premises, vehicle, or building used or leased for public elementary or secondary school
11 functions, whether or not any person is endangered by any action under this section, is
12 guilty of a Class 1 misdemeanor.
13 This section does not apply to:
14 (1) A law enforcement officer;
15 (2) An individual who has completed a school sentinel training course, in accordance
16 with chapter 13-64;
17 (3) An individual who:
18 (a) Is twenty-one years of age or older;
19 (b) Holds an enhanced permit to carry a concealed pistol, issued in accordance
20 with chapter 23-7; and
21 (c) Has written permission from the principal of the school or other person who
22 has general control and supervision of the building or grounds;
23 (4) An individual who:
24 (a) Is employed by the school district;
25 (b) Is twenty-one years of age or older;
