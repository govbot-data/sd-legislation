26.647.14 101st Legislative Session HC8002
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Commemoration 8002
Introduced by: Representatives Aylward, Bahmuller, Baxter, Emery, Jensen (Phil), Jordan, Moore, Muckey, Pourier,
Schaefbauer, and Sjaarda and Senators Perry and Zikmund
1 A LEGISLATIVE COMMEMORATION celebrating the Harrisburg Tigers as the 2025
2 Class AA state gymnastics champions.
3 WHEREAS, the Harrisburg Tigers gymnastics team displayed dominance throughout the
4 2025 season, placing first at the Eastern South Dakota Conference and the Metro Conference
5 meets; and
6 WHEREAS, the Harrisburg Tigers gymnastics team achieved a historic milestone by
7 winning their first Class AA Championship, and set a new Class AA record with a team score
8 of 152.983; and
9 WHEREAS, outstanding performances in the individual competitions led to first place
10 finishes for Katelyn Maeschen in the Class AA all-around and floor individual competitions,
11 Emery Myron in the Class AA beam individual competition, and Isabelle Reiners in the Class
12 AA vault individual competition; and
13 WHEREAS, the team's success was driven by the dedicated coaching of head coach, Callie
14 Aylward, who was named the Gymnastics Coach of the Year by the South Dakota High School
15 Coaches Association, and assistant coach, Abby Christiansen;
16 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT COMMEMORATED, by the One Hundred First Legislature of the
17 State of South Dakota, that the Harrisburg Tigers women's gymnastics team is the 2025 Class
18 AA gymnastics state champions.
