26.336.14 101st Legislative Session 1090
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1090
Introduced by: Representative Fitzgerald
1 An Act to require publication of notices of gubernatorial appointments and
2 reappointments.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 3-4:
5 The governor's office shall publish, in the next regularly scheduled issue of the
6 South Dakota Register, notice of any appointment or reappointment of a nominee to, or
7 the filling of any vacancy of, a board, commission, council, or any other state entity over
8 which the Governor has a constitutional or statutory power of appointment.
