26.910.9 101st Legislative Session 1256
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1256
Introduced by: Representative Goodwin
1 An Act to require the promulgation of rules related to accessing the capitol complex
2 and providing electronic card access to capitol access pass holders.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 5-15-34 be AMENDED:
5 5-15-34. The commissioner of human resources and administration the Bureau of
6 Human Resources and Administration may promulgate such rules, pursuant to chapter 1-
7 26 as may be necessary to promote the, regarding the capitol complex and grounds, which
8 are necessary to:
9 (1) Promote health, safety, and general welfare, to prohibit;
10 (2) Prohibit public intoxication, disturbances, and disorderly assemblies, to keep;
11 (3) Keep the peace, and to declare what; and
12 (4) Declare that which constitutes a nuisance within the buildings of the capitol
13 complex and the capitol grounds.
14 These rules may include the regulation of hours of general public accessibility to
15 buildings within the capitol complex and the regulation of obstruction, speed limits, and
16 parking on the streets and alleys within the capitol grounds.
17 Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 5-15:
18 The commissioner of the Bureau of Human Resources and Administration shall:
19 (1) Promulgate rules, pursuant to chapter 1-26, regulating general public accessibility
20 to the buildings within the capitol complex; and
21 (2) Permit the holder of a capitol access pass to access all outside entrances into the
22 Capitol complex, which are accessible by electronic access card.
