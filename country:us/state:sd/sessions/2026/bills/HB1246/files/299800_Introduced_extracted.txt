26.944.15 101st Legislative Session 1246
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1246
Introduced by: Representative Hughes
1 An Act to prohibit nondisclosure of certain agreements related to data centers.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 1-27:
4 A state agency or political subdivision may not enter into an agreement with a non-
5 governmental entity, private person, or public-private business concerning the
6 construction, development, or location of a data center, which requires confidentiality or
7 nondisclosure. An agreement under this section is a public record as defined in § 1-27-
8 1.1.
9 For purposes of this section, "data center" means any facility that is used as a
10 centralized repository for the dissemination, management, processing, and storage of
11 electronic data and information.
