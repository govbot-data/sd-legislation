26.819.9 101st Legislative Session SCR603
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Concurrent Resolution 603
Introduced by: Senator Karr
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION supporting the secretary of the United States
2 Department of Agriculture in establishing a working group of nutrition
3 experts to determine national standards for foods and beverages purchased
4 with supplemental nutrition assistance program benefits.
5 WHEREAS, the supplemental nutrition assistance program (SNAP) is a federal program
6 that provides food assistance to millions of Americans, including South Dakota residents, to
7 help ensure that families have access to food; and
8 WHEREAS, research shows that diet quality is directly linked to health outcomes, and
9 there is a need for clear national guidance on nutritious foods and beverages that can be
10 purchased with SNAP benefits; and
11 WHEREAS, it is in the public interest to ensure that SNAP benefits are used responsibly to
12 support sound nutrition and healthy families; and
13 WHEREAS, nutrition standards for SNAP purchases should be informed by the latest
14 science, ensure equal access to healthy foods, and support public health objectives; and
15 WHEREAS, updated national dietary guidance, including a newly released food pyramid,
16 reflects current nutrition science and provides an opportunity to align SNAP purchasing
17 standards with widely accepted recommendations for healthy eating; and
18 WHEREAS, a federal mandate for nutritious foods in SNAP is a federal matter that requires
19 an act of Congress; and federal action is necessary to ensure consistency nationwide, avoid
20 unnecessary state administrative costs, and avoid a reversal of policy in a future federal
21 administration; and
22 WHEREAS, the secretary of the United States Department of Agriculture has been directed
23 through H.R. Rep. No. 119‑172, 119th Cong. (2025) to establish a working group of nutrition
