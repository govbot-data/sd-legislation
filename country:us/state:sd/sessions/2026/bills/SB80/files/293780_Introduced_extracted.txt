26.610.9 101st Legislative Session 80
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 80
Introduced by: Senator Vilhauer
1 An Act to prohibit the sale of delta-9 tetrahydrocannabinol products to individuals
2 under the age of twenty-one.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 34-20B-117 be AMENDED:
5 34-20B-117. The following actions are unlawful:
6 (1) To knowingly sell or distribute a product intended for human consumption
7 containing delta-8 tetrahydrocannabinol, delta-9 tetrahydrocannabinol, THC-O
8 acetate, or hexahydrocannabinol to a person under the age of twenty-one;
9 (2) The purchase or attempt to purchase, the receipt or attempt to receive, the
10 possession, or the consumption of, a product intended for human consumption
11 containing delta-8 tetrahydrocannabinol, delta-9 tetrahydrocannabinol, THC-O
12 acetate, or hexahydrocannabinol to a person under the age of twenty-one; and
13 (3) To purchase a product intended for human consumption containing delta-8
14 tetrahydrocannabinol, delta-9 tetrahydrocannabinol, THC-O acetate, or
15 hexahydrocannabinol on behalf of, or to give a product intended for human
16 consumption containing delta-8 tetrahydrocannabinol, delta-9
17 tetrahydrocannabinol, THC-O acetate, or hexahydrocannabinol to, any person
18 under the age of twenty-one, unless the purchaser is a parent or guardian of the
19 person under the age of twenty-one.
20 A violation of this section is a Class 2 misdemeanor.
