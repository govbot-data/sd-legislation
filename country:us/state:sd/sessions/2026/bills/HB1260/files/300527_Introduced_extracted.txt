26.229.13 101st Legislative Session 1260
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1260
Introduced by: The Committee on Local Government
1 An Act to authorize the establishment of municipal property tax rebate programs.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 10-13:
4 The governing body of a municipality may, by ordinance, establish a program to
5 provide a property tax rebate to an owner of an owner-occupied single-family dwelling
6 situated within the municipality. Only taxes levied by the municipality may be rebated
7 under the program.
8 If a municipality establishes a program to provide a property tax rebate pursuant
9 to this section, the municipality must issue a rebate to any applicant who meets the criteria
10 provided in the ordinance.
