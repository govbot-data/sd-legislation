2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1104
Introduced by: Representative Mulder
1 An Act to revise the application requirements for special license plates and parking
2 permits for individuals with disabilities.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 32-5-76 be AMENDED:
5 32-5-76. An owner of a motor vehicle, who is a resident of this state, who has
6 complied with all the laws of this state in regards to the registration of a motor vehicle,
7 and who operates or directs the operation of the vehicle, may submit to the county
8 treasurer an application containing a physician's certificate, on a form approved by the
9 secretary, containing a certification signed by any physician, physician assistant,
10 chiropractor, physical therapist, or certified nurse practitioner, licensed under title 36,
11 stating that the applicant is a person an individual with a substantial physical disability
12 that makes it impossible or causes substantial hardship to walk. The certification must be
13 within the practitioner's applicable scope of practice.
14 An owner of a motor vehicle, who is a resident of this state, who has complied with
15 all the laws of this state in regards to the registration of a motor vehicle, and who is the
16 parent or legal guardian of a dependant dependent with a substantial physical disability
17 and operates the motor vehicle for the purpose of transporting the dependent, may submit
18 to the county treasurer an application, containing a physician's certificate on a form
19 approved by the secretary, containing a certification signed by any physician, physician
20 assistant, chiropractor, physical therapist, or certified nurse practitioner, licensed under
21 title 36, stating that the dependent has a substantial physical disability that makes it
22 impossible or causes substantial hardship to walk. The certification must be within the
23 practitioner's applicable scope of practice.
24 The secretary shall promulgate a rule, pursuant to chapter 1-26, defining a person
25 an individual with a substantial physical disability.
