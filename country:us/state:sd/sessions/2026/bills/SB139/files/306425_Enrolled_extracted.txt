2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 139
ENROLLED
AN ACT
ENTITLED An Act to revise a provision related to the transportation of an inmate
upon discharge from a correctional facility.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
Section 1. That § 24-5-3 be AMENDED:
24-5-3. Upon discharge of an inmate from a correctional facility, whether by
parole, suspended sentence, or final discharge, a correctional facility official must provide
the inmate with the following, if not already provided:
(1) Suitable clothing;
(2) A sum of money to be determined by the secretary of corrections; and
(3) Transportation to one of the following locations:
(a) The county where the inmate resided at the time of the offense that resulted
in imprisonment;
(b) The county where the inmate received sentence; or
(c) A location equivalent in distance to either subsection (a) or (b), whichever
is lesser in mileage.
