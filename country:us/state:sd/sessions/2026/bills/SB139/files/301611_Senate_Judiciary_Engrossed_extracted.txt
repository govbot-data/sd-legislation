1 An Act to revise a provision related to the transportation of an inmate upon
2 discharge from a correctional facility.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 24-5-3 be AMENDED:
5 24-5-3. If not already provided, Upon discharge of an inmate from a correctional
6 facility, whether by parole, suspended sentence, or final discharge, a correctional facility
7 official shall must provide every the inmate with suitable the following, if not already
8 provided:
9 (1) Suitable clothing, a;
10 (2) A sum of money to be determined by the secretary of corrections, and
11 transportation; and
12 (3) Transportation to the place one of the following locations:
13 (a) The county where the inmate resided at the time of the offense that resulted
14 in imprisonment;
15 (b) The county where the inmate received sentence; or an
16 (c) A location equivalent in distance upon discharge from a correctional facility,
17 whether by parole, suspended sentence, or final discharge to either
18 subsection (a) or (b), whichever is lesser in mileage.
