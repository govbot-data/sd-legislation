AN ACT
ENTITLED An Act to clarify when certification of values is transmitted to the county.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
Section 1. That § 10-11-52 be AMENDED:
10-11-52. Failure on the part of the Department of Revenue to transmit the
certificate required by § 10-11-51, on or before the fourth Monday of August, does not in
any way invalidate the assessment or tax levied, if the certificate is transmitted to the
county auditor within a reasonable time.
