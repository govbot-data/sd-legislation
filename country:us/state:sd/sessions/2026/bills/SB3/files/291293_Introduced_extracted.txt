26.34.21 101st Legislative Session 3
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 3
Introduced by: Senator Rohl
1 An Act to limit the types of residential improvements for which counties,
2 municipalities, and townships may require a permit.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 11-10:
5 Notwithstanding any authority granted to a county, municipality, or township to
6 prescribe building codes or standards or to issue building permits, a county, municipality,
7 or township may not require a building permit for:
8 (1) The repair or substantially similar replacement of any of the following existing
9 exterior parts of an owner-occupied residential structure:
10 (a) Door;
11 (b) Downspout;
12 (c) Fencing panel or post;
13 (d) Gutter;
14 (e) Roof flashing, shingles, shakes, or tiles;
15 (f) Siding; and
16 (g) Window; or
17 (2) Any interior alteration, improvement, or renovation of an owner-occupied
18 residential structure, which does not affect the structure's:
19 (a) Air conditioning, electric, heating, plumbing, or ventilation system in a
20 manner requiring any load-bearing modification;
21 (b) Foundation;
22 (c) Load-bearing walls;
23 (d) Roof frame; or
24 (e) Support beams.
