26.34.23 101st Legislative Session 3
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 3
HOUSE LOCAL GOVERNMENT ENGROSSED
Introduced by: Senator Rohl
1 An Act to limit the types of residential improvements for which counties,
2 municipalities, and townships may require a permit.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 11-10:
5 Notwithstanding any authority granted to a county, municipality, or township to
6 prescribe building codes or standards or to issue building permits, a county, municipality,
7 or township may not require a building permit for the repair or substantially similar
8 replacement of any of the following existing exterior parts of an owner-occupied residential
9 structure:
10 (1) Door, if the dimensions remain the same;
11 (2) Downspout;
12 (3) Fencing panel or post;
13 (4) Gutter;
14 (5) Siding, if nonstructural; and
15 (6) Window, if the dimensions remain the same.
16 Nothing in this section relieves a homeowner from compliance with any other
17 applicable building code or ordinance.
18 A county, municipality, or township may require building permits for repairs
19 pursuant to this section or substantially similar replacements for exterior parts of any
20 designated historical property as authorized under chapter 1-19B.
Underscores indicate new language.
