26.587.11 101st Legislative Session 1183
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1183
Introduced by: Representative Healy
1 An Act to revise qualifications for immunity from prosecution following a drug-
2 related overdose.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 34-20A-111 be AMENDED:
5 34-20A-111. A person who experiences a drug-related overdose and is in need of
6 medical assistance may not be arrested, charged, or prosecuted for any misdemeanor or
7 felony offense of possession, inhalation, ingestion, or otherwise taking into the body any
8 controlled drug or substance if that person or another person contacts law enforcement
9 or emergency medical services and reports that he or she is in need of requests medical
10 assistance as the result because of a drug-related overdose. A person qualifies for the
11 immunities provided in this section only if the evidence for the charge or prosecution was
12 obtained as a result of the drug-related overdose and the need for medical assistance.
