26.625.16 101st Legislative Session 1179
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1179
SENATE JUDICIARY ENGROSSED
Introduced by: Representative Mortenson
1 An Act to modify provisions related to the reimbursement fee for preparation of a
2 garnishment disclosure.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 21-18-9 be AMENDED:
5 21-18-9. If a garnishee summons, affidavit, and garnishment disclosure is served,
6 upon receipt by the plaintiff of the garnishee's garnishment disclosure of liability provided
7 for in § 21-18-27, the garnishee shall must be paid the sum of fifteen forty dollars to
8 reimburse the garnishee for the expense of preparing the garnishment disclosure, which
9 sum shall must be taxed as a part of the plaintiff's costs. If the garnishee is not paid, the
10 garnishment proceeding is void. The return of service of the garnishee summons shall
11 must include a statement that the sum was paid. The garnishment disclosure shall must
12 be returned to the plaintiff and filed with the court.
