26.625.15 101st Legislative Session 1179
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1179
Introduced by: Representative Mortenson
1 An Act to increase the reimbursement fee for preparation of a garnishment
2 disclosure.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 21-18-9 be AMENDED:
5 21-18-9. If a garnishee summons, affidavit, and garnishment disclosure is served,
6 the garnishee shall must be paid the sum of fifteen fifty dollars to reimburse the garnishee
7 for the expense of preparing the garnishment disclosure, which sum shall must be taxed
8 as a part of the plaintiff's costs. If the garnishee is not paid, the garnishment proceeding
9 is void. The return of service of the garnishee summons shall must include a statement
10 that the sum was paid. The garnishment disclosure shall must be returned to the plaintiff
11 and filed with the court.
