26.973.9 101st Legislative Session 1294
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1294
Introduced by: Representative Andera
1 An Act to prohibit geoengineering in this state.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 34A-1:
4 No person may engage in geoengineering in this state.
5 A violation of this section is a Class 6 felony.
6 For purposes of this section, "geoengineering" means the intentional, large-scale
7 intervention in the earth’s climate system to counteract climate change by:
8 (1) Removing carbon dioxide from the atmosphere; or
9 (2) Reflecting sunlight away from earth.
