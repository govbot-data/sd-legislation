1 An Act to create the homeowner property tax reduction fund, and to transfer
2 moneys to the homeowner property tax reduction fund.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 10-13:
5 There is created in the state treasury the homeowner property tax reduction fund.
6 The Department of Revenue shall administer the fund. The purpose of the fund is
7 to provide property tax relief for owner-occupied single-family dwellings. Moneys in the
8 fund must be used to reduce the local effort for general education levies on owner-
9 occupied single-family dwellings.
10 Interest on moneys credited to the fund must remain in the fund. Expenditures
11 from the fund must be budgeted through the general appropriation bill.
12 Moneys in the fund may not be transferred to the general fund.
13 Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 10-13:
14 The treasurer shall deposit in the homeowner property tax reduction fund
15 established in section 1 of this Act, the applicable percent of taxes collected on or after
16 August 1, 2027, pursuant to:
17 (1) Chapter 10-45;
18 (2) Chapter 10-46;
19 (3) Chapter 10-46E; and
20 (4) Chapter 10-58.
21 For purposes of this section, "applicable percent" means three-tenths percent
22 divided by the tax rate provided for the taxes referenced in subdivisions (1) to (4),
23 inclusive, of this section, as applicable.
