26.937.13 101st Legislative Session SC807
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Commemoration 807
Introduced by: Senator Nelson and Representative Auch
1 A LEGISLATIVE COMMEMORATION recognizing and honoring custodial staff across
2 the State of South Dakota for their dedication and service.
3 WHEREAS, custodial staff are the unsung heroes of public and private facilities, often being
4 the first to arrive and the last to leave, and dedicating countless hours day in and day out to
5 ensuring safe, clean, and welcoming environments for all; and
6 WHEREAS, custodial staff play a vital role in protecting public health, particularly in
7 healthcare settings, where their diligent cleaning and disinfecting efforts serve as the first line
8 of defense against the spread of viruses and bacteria; and
9 WHEREAS, custodial staff contribute to the success of workplaces by maintaining
10 organized, sanitary, and attractive environments that promote productivity and create
11 positive impressions on customers and visitors alike; and
12 WHEREAS, in schools, businesses, and communities, custodial staff are essential to
13 creating environments conducive to learning, often safeguarding student and staff health by
14 routinely disinfecting high-touch areas such as foyers, meeting rooms, classrooms, and
15 restrooms;
16 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT COMMEMORATED, by the One Hundred First Legislature of the
17 State of South Dakota, that recognition be extended to all custodial staff in this state for their
18 hard work, professionalism, and dedication to maintaining safe, clean, and welcoming
19 environments in our communities.
