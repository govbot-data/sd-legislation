26.628.10 101st Legislative Session 198
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 198
Introduced by: Senator Karr
1 An Act to restrict the use of a cell phone by a student during instructional time.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 13-32:
4 Except as otherwise provided in this section, a student may not use a cell phone
5 during the school day, as set by the board of the school district pursuant to § 13-26-1.
6 A student may use a cell phone during the school day if:
7 (1) The use of a cell phone is required or permitted as part of an approved medical or
8 educational accommodation; or
9 (2) The use of a cell phone is required during an emergency affecting the health,
10 safety, or well-being of a student or staff member.
11 The board of each school district shall adopt a policy that sets forth disciplinary
12 actions for student noncompliance.
