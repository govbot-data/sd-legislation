26.890.21 101st Legislative Session 1277
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1277
Introduced by: Representative Novstrup
1 An Act to require that campgrounds and campground additions, alterations, and
2 modifications comply with National Fire Protection Association standards.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 34-18:
5 Any existing or newly constructed campground, or a permanent addition,
6 alteration, or modification to an existing structure in a campground, which requires notice
7 to the department, as set forth in § 34-18-23, must comply with the National Fire
8 Protection Association 1194, Standard for Recreational Vehicle Parks and Campgrounds,
9 2026 Edition.
10 This section does not apply to a campground owned or operated by this state or a
11 political subdivision of this state.
12 Section 2. This Act is effective beginning July 1, 2027.
