26.644.15 101st Legislative Session 1305
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1305
Introduced by: Representative Schaefbauer
1 An Act to prohibit the dissemination of location information about a public officer
2 and provide a penalty and civil cause of action therefor.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 22-11:
5 No person may publicly disseminate the employment, home, or school address or
6 location of a current or former public officer, or the officer's family or household member:
7 (1) Without the consent of the public officer; and
8 (2) With the intent to:
9 (a) Harass, intimidate, or threaten the public officer or the officer's family or
10 household member; or
11 (b) Otherwise cause the public officer or the officer's family or household
12 member to reasonably fear for the officer's safety or the safety of the
13 officer's family or household member.
14 A violation of this section is a Class 6 felony.
15 Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 20-9:
16 In addition to the criminal penalty provided in section 1 of this Act, and any other
17 civil or criminal remedy available under law, there is a civil cause of action for the
18 dissemination of location information about a current or former public officer. The public
19 officer may recover punitive, special, and general damages, including damages for
20 emotional distress, and reasonable attorney fees and costs.
21 An action may be maintained under this section whether or not a person alleged
22 to have violated section 1 of this Act is charged or convicted of the alleged crime.
