26.58.11 101st Legislative Session 6
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 6
Introduced by: Senator Rohl
1 An Act to reduce the duration of an individual's reemployment benefits.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That § 61-6-8 be AMENDED:
4 61-6-8. Unless the provisions of §§ 61-6-49 to 61-6-64, inclusive, apply, an
5 individual's maximum benefit amount is an amount equal to one-third of the individual's
6 total base period wages in covered employment not to exceed twenty-six twelve times the
7 individual's weekly benefit amount. If that amount is not a multiple of one dollar, it is
8 lowered to the next lower multiple of one dollar.
9 Trade readjustment payments may allow an individual to receive benefits in excess
10 of twenty-six twelve weeks if the individual is in training approved by the secretary under
11 the Trade Act of 1974, as amended by section 2501 of P.L. 97-35--August 13, 1981, and
12 then only as long as necessary to complete the training.
13 If the benefit year of an individual ends within an extended benefit period, the
14 number of weeks of extended benefits that the individual would, but for this section, be
15 entitled to in that extended benefit period is reduced by the number of weeks for which
16 the individual received any amounts as trade readjustment allowances within that benefit
17 year, multiplied by the individual's weekly benefit amount for extended benefits.
