26.806.9 101st Legislative Session 1124
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1124
Introduced by: Representative Schaefbauer
1 An Act to establish the crime of trespass upon a place of worship, provide a penalty
2 therefor, and declare an emergency.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 22-35:
5 No person may enter or remain on the premises of any place of worship, or within
6 one thousand feet of any place of worship, with the intent to disrupt worship services at
7 the place of worship, menace or harass congregants or employees of the place of worship,
8 or for the purpose of political intimidation of or the incitement of fear of violence in those
9 attending the place of worship. A violation of this section is a Class 5 felony.
10 For purposes of this section, "place of worship" means a structure where people
11 regularly assemble for worship, ceremonies, rituals, and education relating to a particular
12 form or religious belief, and which a reasonable person would conclude is a place of
13 worship by reason of design, signs, or architectural or other features.
14 Section 2. Whereas, this Act is necessary for the immediate preservation of the public peace,
15 health, or safety, an emergency is hereby declared to exist, and this Act shall be in full force
16 and effect from and after its passage and approval.
