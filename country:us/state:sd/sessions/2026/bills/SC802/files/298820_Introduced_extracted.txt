26.670.9 101st Legislative Session SC802
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Commemoration 802
Introduced by: Senators Schoenfish, Crabtree, Deibert, Larson, Nelson, Perry, Pischke, Reed, and Zikmund and
Representatives Bahmuller, Emery, Goodwin, Heermann, Kull, Moore, Muckey, Peterson (Drew), and Stevens
1 A LEGISLATIVE COMMEMORATION celebrating and honoring Tabor Czech Days
2 annual festivities on the occasion of its seventy-seventh anniversary.
3 WHEREAS, Czech immigrants have enriched the culture of South Dakota with their
4 exceptional achievements in traditional music, literature, and art, and are known for their
5 love of agriculture, mechanical arts, and democracy, which they strive to preserve for future
6 generations; and
7 WHEREAS, this year's Czech Days Royalty are Queen Tarynn Torsney, Princess Olivia
8 Pechous, and Prince Miles Mayer; and
9 WHEREAS, Czech Days offers a variety of exhibits, attractions, events, and entertainment,
10 including the Czech Heritage Museum; Blachnik Museum; St. Wenceslaus Catholic Church;
11 programs for adults and children in Sokol Park; live Czech music in Beseda Hall; a variety of
12 Czech foods, including kolaches; kolache baking and dumpling making demonstrations; adult
13 and youth parades; a craft fair; a pedal tractor pull; a Bohemian tractor pull; a Tabor Bluebirds
14 amateur baseball game; and the Rich Schild Memorial Fireworks Display to start the
15 celebration on the evening of June 18;
16 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT COMMEMORATED, by the One Hundred First Legislature of the
17 State of South Dakota, that this is the year of the seventy-seventh Tabor Czech Days, which
18 will be held from June 18 through 20, 2026.
