5 Section 1. That § 1-44-4 be AMENDED:
6 1-44-4. There is created the Transportation Commission within the Department
7 of Transportation. The Transportation Commission consists of nine members. The
8 Governor shall appoint the members. All members are full voting members of the
9 commission. Not all members of the commission may be from the same political party. No
10 two members may, at the same time, be a resident residents of the same county. At least
11 two members shall have experience in the use or operation of rail service. At least two
12 members shall have experience in aeronautics.
