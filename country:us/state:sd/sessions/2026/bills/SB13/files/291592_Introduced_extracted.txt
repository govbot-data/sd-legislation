26.185.9 101st Legislative Session 13
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 13
Introduced by: Senator Reed
1 An Act to clarify application of the seventy-two hour notice requirement to website
2 posting.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 1-25-1.3 be AMENDED:
5 1-25-1.3. The state shall provide public notice of a regular meeting by posting a
6 copy of the proposed agenda at:
7 (1) At the principal office of the board, commission, or department holding the
8 meeting; and
9 (2) On a state website designated by the commissioner of the Bureau of Finance and
10 Management.
11 The proposed agenda shall must include the date, time, and location of the
12 meeting, and be visible, readable, and accessible to the public. The agenda shall must be
13 posted as required in this section at least seventy-two hours before the meeting is
14 scheduled to start, according to the agenda. The seventy-two hours does not include
15 Saturday, Sunday, or any legal holidays. The notice shall also be posted on a state
16 website, designated by the commissioner of the Bureau of Finance and Management
17 holiday.
18 For any special or rescheduled meeting, the information in the notice shall must be
19 delivered in person, or by mail, by email, or by telephone, to members of the local news
20 media who have requested notice. For any special or rescheduled meeting, and the state
21 shall also comply with the must provide public notice provisions of as required in this
22 section for a regular meeting to the extent that circumstances permit.
23 A violation of this section is a Class 2 misdemeanor.
