AN ACT
ENTITLED An Act to revise the daily meal allowance for wildland fire employees and
fire suppression forces in certain circumstances.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 3-9:
Notwithstanding §§ 3-9-2 to 3-9-2.2, inclusive, the Department of Public Safety
shall establish a maximum daily meal allowance for wildland fire employees and fire
suppression forces authorized by §§ 34-35-20, 41-20A-8, 41-20A-10, and 41-20A-12,
while performing duties on any uncontrolled fire within the Black Hills Forest Fire Protection
District, or outside the District if the Governor has declared an emergency pursuant to
§ 34-48A-5 or 41-20A-8. The amount may not exceed the current United States General
Services Administration per diem rate for South Dakota. The department shall publish the
maximum daily meal allowance to be effective as of October first each year. The daily
meal allowance applies only to meals that are ordered and distributed in a manner
approved by the department.
