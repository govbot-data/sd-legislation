26.842.9 101st Legislative Session 1151
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1151
Introduced by: Representative Heinemann
1 An Act to ban kratom and kratom products and to provide a penalty therefor.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 34-20B:
4 A person who sells, distributes, purchases, consumes, or possesses kratom, a
5 kratom product, or a product containing synthetic mitragynine or 7-hydroxymitragynine,
6 is guilty of a Class 2 misdemeanor.
7 Section 2. That § 34-20B-115 be REPEALED.
8 Any of the following actions are unlawful:
9 (1) To knowingly sell or distribute a kratom product to a person under the age of
10 twenty-one;
11 (2) The purchase or attempt to purchase, the receipt or attempt to receive, the
12 possession, or the consumption of a kratom product by a person under the age of
13 twenty-one; or
14 (3) To purchase a kratom product on behalf of, or to give a kratom product to, any
15 person under the age of twenty-one, unless the purchaser is a parent or guardian
16 of the person under the age of twenty-one.
17 A violation of this section is a Class 2 misdemeanor.
18 Section 3. That § 34-20B-115.1 be REPEALED.
19 No person may prepare, sell, or distribute a kratom product that:
20 (1) Contains a level of 7-hydroxymitragynine in the alkaloid fraction that is greater
21 than two percent of the alkaloid composition of the product;
22 (2) Contains synthetic mitragynine, synthetic 7-hydroxymitragynine, or any other
23 synthetic alkaloid or synthetically derived compound from the Mitragyna speciosa
24 plant;
