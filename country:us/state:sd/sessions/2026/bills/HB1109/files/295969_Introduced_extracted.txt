26.56.9 101st Legislative Session 1109
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1109
Introduced by: Representative Stevens
1 An Act to allow for the expungement of records pertinent to dismissed protection
2 order cases.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 22-19A:
5 If the court dismisses a petition filed pursuant to § 22-19A-8, either initially or
6 after a hearing held pursuant to § 22-19A-10, the court may then grant the expungement,
7 as defined by § 23A-3-26, of all records pertinent to the court file, including any on file
8 with the Department of Social Services.
9 Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 25-10:
10 If the court dismisses a petition filed pursuant to § 25-10-3, either initially or after
11 a hearing held pursuant to § 25-10-4, the court may then grant the expungement, as
12 defined by § 23A-3-26, of all records pertinent to the court file, including any on file with
13 the Department of Social Services.
Underscores indicate new language.
Overstrikes indicate deleted language.
