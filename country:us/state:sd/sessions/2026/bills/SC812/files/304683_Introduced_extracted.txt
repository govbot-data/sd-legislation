26.1010.17 101st Legislative Session SC812
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Commemoration 812
Introduced by: Senators Zikmund, Hulse, and Perry and Representative Muckey
1 A LEGISLATIVE COMMEMORATION to acknowledge Glenn Muller's distinguished
2 career and congratulate him on his achievements.
3 WHEREAS, Glenn Muller, of Davis, South Dakota, has dedicated his life to the agriculture
4 industry in South Dakota, having grown up on a swine operation, earned a degree in
5 agricultural education from South Dakota State University, and served the pork industry in
6 numerous roles within the state; and
7 WHEREAS, throughout his career, Mr. Muller has witnessed and contributed to significant
8 changes in the industry, including overseeing the expansion of swine operations, advancing
9 the SDSU Swine Unit, and helping cultivate a more positive public perception of pork
10 production in South Dakota; and
11 WHEREAS, Mr. Muller's distinguished career and steadfast commitment to supporting
12 producers, advocating policy, and educating the public about modern agriculture have earned
13 Mr. Muller the South Dakota Corn Growers Association Lifetime Achievement Award; and
14 WHEREAS, Mr. Muller's current role as Executive Director of the South Dakota Pork
15 Producers Council has him overseeing work that ranges from educating future industry
16 members to promoting pork at local and state events;
17 WHEREAS, Mr. Muller was named the 2025 Agribusiness Citizen of the Year by the Greater
18 Sioux Falls Chamber of Commerce and the 2025 Governor's Ag Ambassador, among other
19 awards he has received;
20 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT COMMEMORATED, by the One Hundred First Legislature of the
21 State of South Dakota, that Glenn Muller is congratulated on his exemplary career and
22 numerous accomplishments in supporting South Dakota's agriculture industry.
