26.838.10 101st Legislative Session 1136
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1136
Introduced by: Representative Derby
1 An Act to make an appropriation for the development of South Dakota.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. There is appropriated $101 from the general fund to the state, for purposes of
4 developing South Dakota.
5 Section 2. The state shall approve vouchers and the state auditor shall draw warrants to pay
6 expenditures authorized by this Act.
7 Section 3. Any amounts appropriated in this Act not lawfully expended or obligated shall
8 revert in accordance with the procedures prescribed in chapter 4-8.
9 Section 4. This Act is effective June 30, 2026.
