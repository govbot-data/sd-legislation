26.582.29 101st Legislative Session 1274
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1274
HOUSE STATE AFFAIRS ENGROSSED
Introduced by: Representative Hughes
1 An Act to prohibit the dispensing, distribution, sale, or advertisement of certain
2 articles or things for purposes of an unlawful abortion and provide a criminal
3 and civil penalty therefor.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
5 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 22-17:
6 No person may knowingly dispense, distribute, sell, or advertise any of the
7 following for purposes of an unlawful abortion pursuant to § 22-17-5.1:
8 (1) An article or thing designed, adapted, or intended for producing an abortion; or
9 (2) An article, instrument, substance, drug, medicine, or thing that is advertised or
10 described in a manner calculated to lead another to use or apply it for producing
11 an abortion.
12 A violation of this section is a Class 6 felony.
13 Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 22-17:
14 The attorney general may recover a civil penalty against a person for violating
15 section 1 of this Act. The amount of the civil penalty may not exceed ten thousand dollars
16 for each violation. The clerk of court shall forward any civil penalty collected under this
17 section to the state treasurer, for deposit in the life protection subfund, as established
18 within the extraordinary litigation fund pursuant to § 1-33-8.11.
19 The attorney general may recover attorney fees, costs, and any other award the
20 court determines is appropriate.
21 Section 3. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 22-17:
22 If the attorney general has reason to believe that a person is engaging in, has
23 engaged in, or is about to engage in a violation of section 1 of this Act, the attorney
