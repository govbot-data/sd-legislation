26.809.36 101st Legislative Session 1254
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1254
HOUSE ENGROSSED
Introduced by: Representative Heinemann
1 An Act to exempt any soil amendment used exclusively for agricultural purposes
2 from sales tax.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 10-45:
5 There are specifically exempted from the provisions of this chapter and from the
6 computation of the amount of tax imposed by it, the gross receipts from the sale of any
7 soil amendment, as defined in § 38-19A-1, provided five hundred pounds or more of any
8 one of these products is sold in a single sale to be used exclusively for agricultural
9 purposes.
