26.397.9 101st Legislative Session 10
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 10
Introduced by: The Chair of the Committee on Transportation at the request of the Department of Public Safety
1 An Act to revise a provision related to the adoption of federal motor carrier
2 regulations.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 49-28A-3 be AMENDED:
5 49-28A-3. The state hereby adopts Title 49 of the Code of Federal Regulations,
6 subtitle B, chapter I, subchapter A, part 107 (subparts F and G only) and subchapter C,
7 parts 171 to 180, inclusive, as amended through January 1, 2025 2026, and Title 49 of
8 the Code of Federal Regulations, subtitle B, chapter III, subchapter B, part 387 and parts
9 390 to 397, inclusive, as amended through January 1, 2025 2026, with the following
10 modifications:
11 (1) All references to interstate operations must also include intrastate operations
12 except that drivers and motor carriers operating intrastate vehicles and
13 combinations of vehicles with two axles or less or with a gross vehicle weight rating
14 of not more than twenty-six thousand pounds that are not used to transport
15 hazardous materials requiring placarding under part 177, or designed to transport
16 more than fifteen passengers, including the driver, are not subject to parts 390 to
17 397, inclusive;
18 (2) For the purposes of 49 C.F.R. § 391.11(b)(1), a driver must be at least twenty-one
19 years old if engaged in interstate commerce, or transporting hazardous material of
20 a type or quantity requiring placarding under part 177, or operating a vehicle
21 designed to transport more than fifteen passengers, including the driver. All other
22 drivers must be at least eighteen years old;
23 (3) Unless required by an employer to be medically certified under Title 49 of the Code
24 of Federal Regulations, intrastate drivers are exempt from the physical
25 requirements of 49 C.F.R. § 391.41.
