26.1060.11 101st Legislative Session SC824
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Commemoration 824
Introduced by: Senator Jensen (Kevin)
1 A LEGISLATIVE COMMEMORATION honoring the 2025-2026 Canton C-Hawks boys
2 wrestling team.
3 WHEREAS, the Canton C-Hawk boys wrestling team has a longstanding history of
4 excellence; and
5 WHEREAS, the Canton C-Hawk boys wrestling team won the Region 2B tournament in
6 February 2026; and
7 WHEREAS, the Canton C-Hawk boys wrestling team went on to win the State B wrestling
8 championship for the ninth consecutive year; and
9 WHEREAS, the 2026 C-Hawk state qualifiers were Bode Walker (106 pounds), Cael
10 Vanderbroek (113 pounds), Blaise Bentz (120 pounds), Levi Tuntland (126 pounds), Shane
11 Sehr (144 pounds), Camdyn Ockenga (150 pounds), Conner Giedd (157 pounds), Wyatt
12 Tuntland (165 pounds), Tasyn Ockenga (175 pounds), Aidan Kranz (190 pounds), Chase
13 Adam (215 pounds), and Gunnar Swenson (285 pounds); and
14 WHEREAS, the C-Hawk boys wrestling team was led by coaches Jeremy Swenson, Parker
15 Swanson, Kyle Seibel, Brandon Sehr, Mike Walker and Zach Bartels;
16 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT COMMEMORATED, by the One Hundred First Legislature of the
17 State of South Dakota, that through dedication, many hours of practice, and the support of
18 parents, friends, and the Canton community, the Canton C-Hawk boys are the 2026 State B
19 Wrestling Tournament Champions.
