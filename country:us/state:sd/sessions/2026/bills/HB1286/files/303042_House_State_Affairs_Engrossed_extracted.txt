3 Section 3. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 61-5:
4 If an applicant for an award from the employer's investment in South Dakota's
5 future fund is a business, the applicant must submit a business plan to the Governor's
6 Office of Economic Development before the Governor may award the applicant any
7 moneys from the fund. The applicant's business plan must include:
8 (1) The goods or services to be offered by the applicant;
9 (2) A description of:
10 (a) The types of jobs required to offer the goods or services;
11 (b) The educational requirement for each type of job; and
12 (c) The compensation package to be offered to individuals employed to perform
13 each type of job;
14 (3) The accounting practices employed by the business; and
15 (4) Other private or public funding received by the applicant.
16 Section 4. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 61-5:
17 Moneys from the employer's investment in South Dakota's future fund may be
18 awarded only to reimburse the actual cost of the research or economic development
19 project.
20 Before any moneys may be expended from the fund, the applicant shall sign an
21 agreement with the Governor's Office of Economic Development. If the award from the
22 fund exceeds one million dollars, the applicant must provide an itemized invoice of all
23 expenditures made by the applicant required in furtherance of the project and
24 documentation demonstrating proof of payment before any moneys may be expended
25 from the fund.
26 Any agreement executed pursuant to this section is open to public inspection and
27 must be posted on the state's website.
28 Section 5. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 61-5:
29 Before the Governor may approve the award of moneys from the employer's
30 investment in South Dakota's future fund, the Governor's Office of Economic Development
31 shall provide a detailed recommendation for the award to the Governor. Upon receiving
