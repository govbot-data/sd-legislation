2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1187
Introduced by: Representative Fitzgerald
1 An Act to add coaches to the list of mandatory reporters of child abuse or neglect.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That § 26-8A-3 be AMENDED:
4 26-8A-3. Any physician, dentist, doctor of osteopathy, chiropractor, optometrist,
5 emergency medical technician, paramedic, mental health professional or counselor,
6 podiatrist, psychologist, religious healing practitioner, social worker, hospital intern or
7 resident, parole or court services officer, law enforcement officer, coach, teacher, school
8 counselor, school official, nurse, licensed or registered child welfare provider, employee
9 or volunteer of a domestic abuse shelter, employee or volunteer of a child advocacy
10 organization or child welfare service provider, chemical dependency counselor, coroner,
11 dental hygienist, or any safety-sensitive position as defined in § 3-6C-1, who has
12 reasonable cause to suspect that a child under the age of eighteen has been abused or
13 neglected, as defined in § 26-8A-2, shall report that information in accordance with §§ 26-
14 8A-6, 26-8A-7, and 26-8A-8. Any person who intentionally fails to make the required
15 report is guilty of a Class 1 misdemeanor. Any person who knows or has reason to suspect
16 that a child has been abused or neglected as defined in § 26-8A-2 may report that
17 information as provided in § 26-8A-8.
