26.962.11 101st Legislative Session 1262
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1262
Introduced by: Representative Greenfield
1 An Act to make an appropriation for the construction of a juvenile corrections center
2 in Brown County and to declare an emergency.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. There is appropriated $2,000,000 from the general fund, to the Department of
5 Corrections, to contract for the construction, completion, and equipping of a regional juvenile
6 corrections center in Brown County, including heating, plumbing, water, sewer, electric
7 facilities, architectural and engineering services, and other services or actions as may be
8 required.
9 Section 2. The administration of the design and construction of the project authorized in this
10 Act is under the general charge and supervision of the Bureau of Human Resources and
11 Administration, as provided in chapter 5-14.
12 Section 3. The secretary of the Department of Corrections shall approve vouchers and the
13 state auditor shall draw warrants to pay expenditures authorized in this Act.
14 Section 4. Any amounts appropriated in this Act not lawfully expended or obligated shall
15 revert in accordance with the procedures prescribed in chapter 4-8.
16 Section 5. Whereas, this Act is necessary for the support of the state government and its
17 existing public institutions, an emergency is hereby declared to exist, and this Act shall be in
18 full force and effect from and after its passage and approval.
