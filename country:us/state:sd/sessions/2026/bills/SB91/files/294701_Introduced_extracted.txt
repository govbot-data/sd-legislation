26.443.9 101st Legislative Session 91
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 91
Introduced by: Senator Nelson
1 An Act to clarify the procedure for petitioning a board of county commissioners for
2 a change to the comprehensive plan or zoning ordinances.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 11-2-28.2 be AMENDED:
5 11-2-28.2. Following receipt of any A petition as provided in pursuant to § 11-2-
6 28 or 11-2-28.1, the board must be filed with the county auditor, who shall present the
7 petition to the board at its next regular or special meeting. Within forty-five days of the
8 presentation, the commission shall hold a public hearing on the petition, subject to the
9 requirements of § 11-2-19, and take action upon the petitioned request. Within forty-five
10 days of receipt by the board of any such petition, the county planning commission shall
11 consider the requested action and § 11-2-29, and make a recommendation thereon on
12 the petitioned request to the board.
13 Upon receipt of the commission's recommendation, the board must hold a public
14 hearing, subject to the requirements of § 11-2-19, and take action upon the petitioned
15 request, pursuant to § 11-2-30.
