26.443.11 101st Legislative Session 91
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 91
ENROLLED
AN ACT
ENTITLED An Act to clarify the procedure for petitioning a board of county
commissioners for a change to the comprehensive plan or zoning ordinances.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
Section 1. That § 11-2-28.2 be AMENDED:
11-2-28.2. A petition pursuant to § 11-2-28 or 11-2-28.1 must be filed with the
office of the county auditor. The county auditor shall forward the petition to the
commission, or a person designated by the commission, for consideration at its next
regular or special meeting and provide a copy to the board, or a person designated by the
board. Within forty-five days of the presentation, the commission shall hold a public
hearing on the petition, subject to the requirements of § 11-2-29, and make a
recommendation on the petitioned request to the board.
Upon receipt of the commission's recommendation, the board must hold a public
hearing, subject to the requirements of § 11-2-19, and take action upon the petitioned
request, pursuant to § 11-2-30.
