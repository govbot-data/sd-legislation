26.443.10 101st Legislative Session 91
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 91
SENATE LOCAL GOVERNMENT ENGROSSED
Introduced by: Senator Nelson
1 An Act to clarify the procedure for petitioning a board of county commissioners for
2 a change to the comprehensive plan or zoning ordinances.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 11-2-28.2 be AMENDED:
5 11-2-28.2. Following receipt of any A petition as provided in pursuant to § 11-2-
6 28 or 11-2-28.1, the board must be filed with the office of the county auditor. The county
7 auditor shall forward the petition to the commission, or a person designated by the
8 commission, for consideration at its next regular or special meeting and provide a copy to
9 the board, or a person designated by the board. Within forty-five days of the presentation,
10 the commission shall hold a public hearing on the petition, subject to the requirements of
11 § 11-2-19, and take action upon the petitioned request. Within forty-five days of receipt
12 by the board of any such petition, the county planning commission shall consider the
13 requested action and § 11-2-29, and make a recommendation thereon on the petitioned
14 request to the board.
15 Upon receipt of the commission's recommendation, the board must hold a public
16 hearing, subject to the requirements of § 11-2-19, and take action upon the petitioned
17 request, pursuant to § 11-2-30.
