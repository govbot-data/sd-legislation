26.645.25 101st Legislative Session 181
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 181
Introduced by: Senator Carley
1 An Act to cause the repeal of the medical cannabis chapter upon the federal re-
2 scheduling of cannabis.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That chapter 34-20G be REPEALED.
5 Section 2. Section 1 of this Act is effective ninety days after the attorney general notifies the
6 code counsel and the Department of Health that cannabis has been reclassified as a schedule
7 III drug by the United States Food and Drug Administration, pursuant to 21 U.S.C. § 812 (July
8 16, 2025).
9 Section 3. After receiving notification by the attorney general, as provided for in section 2 of
10 this Act, the Department of Health shall notify all cardholders and medical cannabis
11 establishments of the re-scheduling and of the date on which the repeal of the medical
12 cannabis chapter is effective.
