26.579.11 101st Legislative Session 1042
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1042
Introduced by: The Chair of the House Committee on Appropriations at the request of the Department of the Military
1 An Act to authorize the construction of a National Guard vehicle maintenance shop
2 in Sturgis, to make an appropriation therefor, and to declare an emergency.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. The Department of the Military may contract for the construction, completion,
5 furnishing, and equipping, including heating, plumbing, water, sewer, electric facilities,
6 architectural and engineering services, and any other services or actions as may be required
7 to construct and maintain a National Guard vehicle maintenance shop in Sturgis.
8 Section 2. There is appropriated $30,000,000 in federal fund expenditure authority, to the
9 Department of the Military, for the purposes authorized in section 1 of this Act.
10 Section 3. The administration of the design and construction of the project authorized in this
11 Act shall be under the general charge and supervision of the Bureau of Human Resources and
12 Administration as provided in chapter 5-14.
13 Section 4. The adjutant general of the Department of the Military shall approve vouchers and
14 the state auditor shall draw warrants to pay expenditures authorized in section 1 of this Act.
15 Section 5. Any amounts appropriated in this Act not lawfully expended or obligated by June
16 30, 2031, shall revert in accordance with the procedures prescribed in chapter 4-8.
17 Section 6. Whereas, this Act is necessary for the support of the state government and its
18 existing public institutions, an emergency is hereby declared to exist, and this Act shall be in
19 full force and effect from and after its passage and approval.
