26.757.9 101st Legislative Session 130
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 130
Introduced by: Senator Duhamel
1 An Act to make an appropriation for roadway and related infrastructure
2 improvements required because of construction activity at Ellsworth Air Force
3 Base and to declare an emergency.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
5 Section 1. There is appropriated $8,000,000 from the general fund to the South Dakota
6 Ellsworth Development Authority, for the purpose of funding public roadway and related
7 infrastructure improvements required because of construction activity occurring on Ellsworth
8 Air Force Base.
9 Section 2. The moneys appropriated in section 1 of this Act must be used for public roadway
10 and related infrastructure improvements necessary to support, mitigate, and address impacts
11 from construction activity occurring on Ellsworth Air Force Base.
12 Eligible uses include: roadway reconstruction, rehabilitation, widening, capacity
13 improvements, traffic safety enhancements and mitigation measures, engineering, design,
14 traffic studies, construction oversight, inspection, and industry-standard roadway analysis.
15 Section 3. On or before July thirty-first, the authority shall submit a report to the Governor's
16 Office of Economic Development, the Legislative Research Council, and the Department of
17 Legislative Audit listing, for the preceding fiscal year, each award recipient, the amount of
18 each award, and the purpose of each award.
19 Section 4. The South Dakota Ellsworth Development Authority may not spend more than
20 three percent of the total appropriation on administrative expenses.
21 Section 5. The executive director of the South Dakota Ellsworth Development Authority shall
22 approve vouchers and the state auditor shall draw warrants to pay expenditures authorized
23 in this Act.
