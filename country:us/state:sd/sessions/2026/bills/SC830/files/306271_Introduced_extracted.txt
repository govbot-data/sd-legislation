26.1055.11 101st Legislative Session SC830
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Commemoration 830
Introduced by: Senators Foster and Perry and Representative Muckey
1 A LEGISLATIVE COMMEMORATION honoring Dave Bonde for exemplary service to
2 South Dakota.
3 WHEREAS, Dave Bonde, as executive director of the Fort Pierre Development Corporation,
4 has supported local businesses, attracted new investment, and promoted sustainable
5 development throughout central South Dakota, demonstrating a deep commitment to the
6 economic growth and vitality of the region; and
7 WHEREAS, Mr. Bonde has served with distinction as sergeant-at-arms of the South Dakota
8 Senate, providing dedicated support and service to the legislative process; and
9 WHEREAS, under Mr. Bonde's guidance, the Fort Pierre Development Corporation has
10 facilitated community events, business expansions, ribbon-cuttings, and tours that showcase
11 Fort Pierre's potential; and
12 WHEREAS, Mr. Bonde's tireless efforts have reflected the best traditions of public service
13 and community leadership;
14 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT COMMEMORATED, by the One Hundred First Legislature of the
15 State of South Dakota, that Dave Bonde's life achievements helped shape the future of our
16 communities by empowering citizens with opportunities for growth and success.
