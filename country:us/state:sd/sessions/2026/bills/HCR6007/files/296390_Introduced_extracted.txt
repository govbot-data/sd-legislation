2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Concurrent Resolution
6007
Introduced by: Representative Jordan
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION encouraging honest economic development rooted in
2 the republican principles of limited government, deregulation, and organic
3 entrepreneurial growth.
4 WHEREAS, the free market economy is foundational to the United States of America; and
5 WHEREAS, the State of South Dakota thrives on free market principles, while emphasizing
6 individual liberty, exercising limited government, promoting personal responsibility,
7 protecting property rights, and keeping taxes low; and
8 WHEREAS, economic development that adheres to such principles benefits the well-being
9 and quality of life of South Dakotans; and
10 WHEREAS, the free market and the private sector, left alone, will best serve our economic
11 interests; and
12 WHEREAS, business owners left to freely evaluate South Dakota's principles, people, land,
13 and business climate will choose South Dakota without enticement from tax credits and
14 subsidies financed by South Dakotans; and
15 WHEREAS, businesses that choose to operate in this state need the government only to
16 uphold laws that protect the rights of individuals and organizations, limit taxes and
17 unnecessary regulations, and avoid competing with private enterprise; and

19 companies and jobs, but by deregulation, which will promote investment in communities;
20 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, by the House of Representatives of the One Hundred
21 First Legislature of the State of South Dakota, the Senate concurring therein, that legislative
