26.509.12 101st Legislative Session 1072
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1072
Introduced by: Representative Weisgram
1 An Act to provide an appropriation for a payment for state employee salaries.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. There is appropriated $8,454,195 from the general fund, $3,524,321 in federal
4 fund expenditure authority, and $8,678,462 in other fund expenditure authority, to the
5 Bureau of Finance and Management, for a payment to each state employee of one and a half
6 percent of the employee's current salary.
7 Section 2. The Bureau of Finance and Management shall distribute the amounts appropriated
8 in section 1 of this Act to each state agency and other state governmental entity, having at
9 least one state employee, to cover the requirements of section 3 of this Act.
10 Section 3. Starting July 1, 2026, each state employee employed as of January 1, 2026, shall
11 be paid at the beginning of each month one twelfth of one and a half percent of the employee's
12 annualized January 1, 2026 salary. If a state employee is no longer employed with the state
13 as of the payment date, no payment may be made to the employee.
14 Section 4. The commissioner of the Bureau of Finance and Management shall approve
15 vouchers and the state auditor shall draw warrants to pay expenditures authorized in this Act.
16 Section 5. Any amounts appropriated in this Act not lawfully expended or obligated by June
17 30, 2027, shall revert in accordance with the procedures prescribed in chapter 4-8.
18 Section 6. This Act is effective beginning June 30, 2026.
