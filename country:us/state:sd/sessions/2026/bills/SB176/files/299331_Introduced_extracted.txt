26.915.13 101st Legislative Session 176
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 176
Introduced by: Senator Hulse
1 An Act to amend the period of time before which ballots and other election materials
2 may be destroyed following an election.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 9-13:
5 The returns from a municipal election must be certified by the precinct election
6 board in each polling place, and the ballots, properly sealed in ballot boxes, together with
7 the pollbooks, must be placed in the custody of the municipal finance officer.
8 The finance officer may destroy voted ballots, pollbooks, and all other election
9 material relating to a municipal election twenty-two months after the election at which the
10 ballots were voted.
11 Section 2. That § 12-20-31 be AMENDED:
12 12-20-31. The officer in charge of an election may destroy voted ballots and
13 pollbooks from a nonfederal election sixty days following the election at which such ballots
14 were voted. However, they may not be destroyed if any recount or contest of such election
15 is pending. The officerThe person in charge of an election may destroy voted ballots,

17 months after the election at which the ballots were voted.

19 other election material relating to a federal election twenty-two months after the election
