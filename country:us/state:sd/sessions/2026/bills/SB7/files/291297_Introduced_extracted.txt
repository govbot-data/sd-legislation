26.87.21 101st Legislative Session 7
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 7
Introduced by: Senator Rohl
1 An Act to modify the tax rate on gold severed in this state.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That § 10-39-43 be AMENDED:
4 10-39-43. For the privilege of severing precious metals in this state, there There
5 is imposed on each person severing gold in this state, a severance tax of four dollars per
6 ounce of one percent of the market value of gold severed by the person in this state.
7 For purposes of this section, "market value" means the average London Bullion
8 Market Association price of gold, over the calendar quarter in which the gold was severed.
