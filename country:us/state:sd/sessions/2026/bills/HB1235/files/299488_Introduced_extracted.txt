26.945.9 101st Legislative Session 1235
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1235
Introduced by: Representative Pourier
1 An Act to increase communication between agencies of this state and tribal
2 governments.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 1-54:
5 At least once each calendar year, the officers and employees of each state agency
6 shall complete a training provided by an individual selected by the governments of each
7 Indian tribe. The training must include:
8 (1) The legal status of each Indian tribe;
9 (2) The legal rights of the members of each Indian tribe; and
10 (3) Any social, economic, and cultural issues of concern to each Indian tribe.
11 Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 1-54:
12 At least once each year, the Governor, the heads of each state agency, and tribal
13 officials from each Indian tribe, shall meet to discuss:
14 (1) Tribal concerns with the administrative rules and agency policies that directly
15 impact tribal government or tribal populations;
16 (2) Other issues of concern to either the state or the tribes; and
17 (3) Potential solutions to the concerns raised in subdivision (1) or (2).
18 Section 3. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 1-54:
19 On or before August fifteenth of each year, a state agency shall report to the
20 Governor, detailing the activities of the state agency relating to tribal governments or
21 tribal populations for the previous fiscal year. The report must include:
22 (1) Any administrative rule or agency policy change that the agency has adopted
23 because of the meeting held pursuant to section 2 of this Act;
