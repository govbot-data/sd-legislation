1 An Act to revise certain required minimum distribution provisions of the South

4 Section 1. That § 3-13-60 be AMENDED:
5 3-13-60. If a participant dies died on or before December 31, 2021, and before
6 required distribution of the participant's benefits has begun commenced, the participant's
7 interest shall must be distributed as follows:
8 (1) If the participant has a surviving spouse, the surviving spouse shall must receive
9 benefits over the life of the surviving spouse beginning as soon as administratively
10 possible and effective the month after the participant's death; or
11 (2) If the participant has no surviving spouse, the participant's remaining interest shall
12 must be distributed in a lump sum payment to the designated beneficiary within
13 five years from the date of the participant's death.
