26.926.13 101st Legislative Session 1236
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1236
Introduced by: Representative Pourier
1 An Act to establish civil liability for furnishing alcohol to an obviously intoxicated
2 person.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 35-4-78 be AMENDED:
5 35-4-78. No licensee, or any employee or agent of a licensee, may sell, or serve
6 any alcoholic beverage to any person who is obviously intoxicated. A violation of this
7 section is a Class 1 misdemeanor.
8 However, no licensee is civilly liable to any injured person or the injured person's
9 estate for any injury suffered, including any action for wrongful death, or property damage
10 suffered because of the intoxication of any person due to the sale or consumption of any
11 alcoholic beverage in violation of the provisions of this section.
