26.317.20 101st Legislative Session 32
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 32
ENROLLED
AN ACT
ENTITLED An Act to amend and repeal provisions pertaining to the ballot
requirements for certain elections and to declare an emergency.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
Section 1. That § 12-5-8 be AMENDED:
12-5-8. The county auditor shall include on the official ballot for a primary election
the name of each candidate for precinct committeeman or precinct committeewoman, in
the manner prescribed by the State Board of Elections.
Section 2. That § 12-9-12 be AMENDED:
12-9-12. In a primary election at which candidates for judicial office are to be
nominated, the name of each candidate must be placed on the official primary election
ballot, as prescribed by the State Board of Elections, in the order designated by the
secretary of state, without any party designation.
Section 3. That § 12-9-13 be REPEALED.
Section 4. That § 12-13-11 be REPEALED.
Section 5. Whereas, this Act is necessary for the immediate preservation of the public peace,
health, or safety, an emergency is hereby declared to exist, and this Act shall be in full force
and effect from and after its passage and approval.
