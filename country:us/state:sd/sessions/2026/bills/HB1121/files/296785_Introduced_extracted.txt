26.590.15 101st Legislative Session 1121
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1121
Introduced by: Representative Hansen
1 An Act to expand the locations at which raw milk may be purchased for personal
2 use by a consumer.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 39-6-3 be AMENDED:
5 39-6-3. Raw milk for human consumption may be secured or purchased for
6 personal use by any consumer at:
7 (1) At the place or farm where the milk is produced and the milk may be delivered ;
8 (2) At a farmers market; and
9 (3) At a retail store that is owned by the producer of the raw milk and is not located
10 at the place or farm where the milk is produced.
11 The producer of the raw milk may deliver the milk directly to the consumer by the
12 producer. No raw milk for human consumption may be purchased at a farmers' market or
13 farmer owned retail store that is not located at the place or farm where the milk is
14 produced.
