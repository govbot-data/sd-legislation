26.1001.9 101st Legislative Session HCR6013
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Concurrent Resolution
6013
Introduced by: Representative Jensen (Phil)
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION encouraging the state's congressional delegation to
2 meet with the Legislature.
3 WHEREAS, the framers of the United States Constitution empowered state legislators to
4 be the guardians of liberty against abuses of power by the federal government; and
5 WHEREAS, the federal government, through improper and imprudent spending has
6 created a crushing national debt that now exceeds the national gross domestic product; and
7 WHEREAS, the federal government has overstepped the limits imposed by the United
8 States Constitution and has impinged upon the legitimate roles of each state through the
9 manipulative process of federal mandates; and
10 WHEREAS, the federal government has ceased to operate under a proper interpretation
11 of the United States Constitution;
12 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, by the House of Representatives of the One Hundred
13 First Legislature of the State of South Dakota, the Senate concurring therein, that the
14 Legislature encourages each member of the state's congressional delegation to annually meet
15 with the Legislature, sitting as a joint committee of the whole, for at least one hour, at a time
16 agreed to by the member, the speaker of the House of Representatives, and the president
17 pro tempore of the Senate, to discuss the state of the federal government and the work of
18 the member on behalf of the state and its residents.
