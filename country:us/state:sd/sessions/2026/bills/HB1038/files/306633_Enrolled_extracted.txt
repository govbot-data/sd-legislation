2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1038
ENROLLED
AN ACT
ENTITLED An Act to allow the Public Utilities Commission to assess actual costs to
data centers that are customers of public utilities.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 49-34A:
The commission may assess a customer of a public utility the actual costs
associated with processing a request for approval of a contract with deviations, or other
electric service agreement with a public utility, related to the provision of electric service
to a customer that is a data center with a peak demand of ten megawatts or greater.
The commission shall forward any moneys collected under this section to the state
treasurer, for deposit in the South Dakota Public Utilities Commission gross receipts tax
fund established in § 49-1A-2.
For the purposes of this section, "data center" means a centralized repository for
the primary purpose of storage, management, dissemination, and processing of electronic
data and information, including the mining of digital currency.
