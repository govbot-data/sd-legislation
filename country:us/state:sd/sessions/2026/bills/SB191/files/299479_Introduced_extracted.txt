2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 191
Introduced by: Senator Howard
1 An Act to remove the authorization to issue grants as part of a tax increment
2 financing district.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 11-9-1 be AMENDED:
5 11-9-1. Terms used in this chapter mean:
6 (1) "Department," the Department of Revenue;
7 (2) "District," a tax increment financing district;
8 (3) "Governing body," the board of trustees, the board of commissioners, the board of
9 county commissioners, or the common council of a municipality;
10 (4) "Grant," the transfer of money or property to a transferee for a governmental
11 purpose that is not a related party to or an agent of the political subdivision;
12 (5) "Planning commission," a planning commission created under chapters chapter 11-
13 2 or 11-6, a planning committee of a governing body of a political subdivision that
14 does not have a planning commission, or the governing body of a political
15 subdivision that does not have a planning commission or planning committee;
16 (6)(5) "Political subdivision," a municipality, as defined in § 11-6-1, or county of this
17 state;
18 (7)(6) "Project plan," the properly approved plan for the development or redevelopment
19 of a tax increment financing district including and all properly approved
20 amendments to the plan;
21 (8)(7) "Tax increment financing district," a contiguous geographic area within a political
22 subdivision defined and created by resolution of the governing body;
23 (9)(8) "Taxable property," all real and personal taxable property located in a tax
24 increment financing district; and
25 (10)(9) "Tax increment valuation," the total value of the tax increment financing district
26 minus the tax increment base as determined pursuant to § 11-9-19.
