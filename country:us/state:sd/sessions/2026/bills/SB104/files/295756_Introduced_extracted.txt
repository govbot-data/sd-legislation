26.420.11 101st Legislative Session 104
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 104
Introduced by: Senator Pischke
1 An Act to require the reimbursement of therapy using equine movement through
2 the state medicaid program.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 28-6:
5 The Department of Social Service shall, for a medical assistance recipient under
6 this chapter, reimburse therapy using equine movement provided by:
7 (1) A physical therapist licensed in accordance with chapter 36-10;
8 (2) An occupational therapist licensed in accordance with chapter 36-31; or
9 (3) A speech-language pathologist licensed in accordance with chapter 36-37.
10 For purposes of this section, "therapy using equine movement" means therapeutic
11 activities that leverage horse-human interactions to facilitate progression toward meeting
12 therapeutic goals.
13 Section 2. The Department of Social Services shall, on or before August 1, 2026, submit a
14 state plan amendment to the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services to effectuate the
15 coverage referenced in section 1 of this Act.
