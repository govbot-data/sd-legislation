26.35.22 101st Legislative Session 4
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 4
Introduced by: Senator Rohl
1 An Act to revise procedures for returning and withholding security deposits for
2 residential premises.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 43-32-24 be AMENDED:
5 43-32-24. Every lessor of residential premises shall, within two weeks twenty-one
6 days after the termination of the tenancy and receipt of the tenant's mailing address or
7 delivery instructions, return the security deposit described in § 43-32-6.1 to the tenant,
8 or furnish to the tenant, a written statement showing the specific reason for the
9 withholding of the deposit, or any portion thereof of the deposit. The lessor may withhold
10 from such the deposit only such the amounts as are reasonably necessary to remedy:
11 (1) Remedy tenant defaults in the payment of rent or of other:
12 (a) Rent; and
13 (b) Other funds due to the landlord pursuant to an agreement or to restore;
14 and
15 (2) Restore the premises to their condition at the commencement of the tenancy,
16 ordinary wear and tear excepted.
17 Within forty-five days after termination of the tenancy, upon request of the lessee,
18 the lessor shall must provide the lessee with an itemized accounting of any deposit
19 withheld.
20 Any lessor of residential premises who fails to comply with this section shall forfeit
21 forfeits all rights to withhold any portion of such the deposit.
22 The bad faith retention of a deposit, or any portion of a deposit, by a lessor of
23 residential premises, in violation of this section, including or the bad faith failure to provide
24 the written statement and or itemized accounting, as required by this section, shall subject
25 subjects the lessor to punitive damages not to exceed two hundred dollars.
