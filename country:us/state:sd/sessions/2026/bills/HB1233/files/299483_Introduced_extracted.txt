26.906.13 101st Legislative Session 1233
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1233
Introduced by: Representative Pourier
1 An Act to modify provisions for a tax collection agreement with an Indian tribe.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That § 10-12A-4 be AMENDED:
4 10-12A-4. The department may enter into tax collection agreements with any
5 Indian tribe under the provisions of this chapter and chapter 1-24. These agreements may
6 provide for the collection of any of the following state taxes and any tribal taxes imposed
7 by a tribe that are identical to the following state taxes:
8 (1) The retail sales and service tax imposed by chapter 10-45;
9 (2) The use tax imposed by chapter 10-46;
10 (3) The contractors' excise tax imposed by chapter 10-46A;
11 (4) The alternate contractors' excise tax imposed by chapter 10-46B;
12 (5) The cigarette tax imposed by chapter 10-50;
13 (6) The motor vehicle excise tax imposed by chapter 32-5B;
14 (7) The fuel excise tax imposed by chapter 10-47B;
15 (8) The wholesale tax on tobacco products imposed by chapter 10-50;
16 (9) The amusement device tax imposed by chapter 10-58;
17 (10) The gross receipts tax on visitor related businesses imposed by chapter 10-45D;
18 (11) The excise tax on farm machinery, attachment units, and irrigation equipment
19 imposed by chapter 10-46E;
20 (12) The sales tax on remote sellers imposed by chapter 10-64; and
21 (13) The tax on marketplace providers imposed by chapter 10-65.
22 The agreement may provide for the retention by the department of an agreed-
23 upon percentage of the gross revenue as an administrative fee.
