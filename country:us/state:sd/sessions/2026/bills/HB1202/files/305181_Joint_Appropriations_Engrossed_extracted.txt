26.824.20 101st Legislative Session 1202
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1202
JOINT APPROPRIATIONS ENGROSSED
Introduced by: Representative Roby
1 An Act to authorize the construction of a trades center on the campus of Lake Area
2 Technical College, to make an appropriation therefor, and to declare an
3 emergency.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
5 Section 1. There is appropriated $101 from the general fund, to the South Dakota Board of
6 Technical Education, to contract for the construction, completion, and equipping of a trades
7 center to house technical training laboratories, classrooms, and other student services on the
8 campus of Lake Area Technical College, including heating, plumbing, water, sewer, electric
9 facilities, architectural and engineering services, and other services or actions as may be
10 required.
11 Section 2. The moneys appropriated by this Act are contingent upon Lake Area Technical
12 College receiving from gifts, grants, donations, or any other source, an amount of money
13 equal to the amount required to complete the project described in section 1 of this Act, less
14 the moneys appropriated pursuant to section 1 of this Act. No bonds may be issued to pay
15 for the completion of the project.
16 Section 3. The administration of the design and construction of the project authorized in this
17 Act is under the general charge and supervision of the Bureau of Human Resources and
18 Administration as provided in chapter 5-14.
19 Section 4. The secretary of the Department of Education shall approve vouchers and the
20 state auditor shall draw warrants to pay expenditures authorized in this Act.
21 Section 5. Any amounts appropriated in this Act not lawfully expended or obligated shall
22 revert in accordance with the procedures prescribed in chapter 4-8.
