26.414.14 101st Legislative Session HCR6004
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Concurrent Resolution
6004
Introduced by: Representative Sjaarda
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION encouraging the use of physical cash in transactions.
2 WHEREAS, payment with physical cash prevents tracking and controlling purchases; and
3 WHEREAS, payment with physical cash saves businesses money on transaction fees; and
4 WHEREAS, payment with physical cash ensures more money stays in this state; and
5 WHEREAS, payment with physical cash ensures the unbanked can participate in the South
6 Dakota economy; and
7 WHEREAS, the use of physical cash in transactions makes this state more resilient in case
8 of a natural disaster or emergency situation;
9 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, by the House of Representatives of the One Hundred
10 First Legislature of the State of South Dakota, the Senate concurring therein, that the citizens
11 of this state are strongly encouraged to pay with physical cash whenever and wherever
12 possible; and
13 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that the citizens of this state are strongly encouraged to
14 support businesses that offer a discount for using physical cash; and
15 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that businesses in this state are strongly encouraged to offer
16 a discount for using physical cash in transactions.
