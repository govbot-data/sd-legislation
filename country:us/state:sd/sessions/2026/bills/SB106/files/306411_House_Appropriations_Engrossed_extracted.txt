26.434.10 101st Legislative Session 106
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 106
HOUSE APPROPRIATIONS ENGROSSED
Introduced by: Senator Sauder
1 An Act to modify the amount required to be set aside for extraordinary expenses
2 incurred in providing special education programs.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 13-37-40 be AMENDED:
5 13-37-40. Subject to the limitation limitations provided below and in § 13-37-42,
6 the secretary of the Department of Education shall, for school fiscal year 2014 and each
7 year thereafter beginning July 1, 2026, set aside four five million dollars of the state aid
8 to districts for special education appropriation for extraordinary expenses incurred in
9 providing special education programs or services to one or more children with disabilities,
10 with expenditures to be made as recommended by an oversight board and approved by
11 the secretary of the Department of Education provided in § 13-37-60.
12 Any funds not expended or obligated pursuant to this section shall are not be
13 subject to reversion pursuant to § 4-8-19. The total amount set aside for extraordinary
14 expenses each fiscal year plus the total amount not reverted from previous fiscal years
15 may, provided that the total amount reserved each year for extraordinary expenses does
16 not exceed five million five hundred thousand dollars.
17 The amount appropriated for extraordinary expenses shall be recalculated at the
18 same time as the amount of the allocations for disability levels as provided in § 13-37-
19 35.2.
