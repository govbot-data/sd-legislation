26.433.10 101st Legislative Session 122
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 122
Introduced by: Senator Perry
1 An Act to establish a duty to assist in an emergency and provide a penalty therefor.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 22-18:
4 A person at the scene of an emergency who knows that another person is exposed
5 to the risk of serious bodily injury or has suffered serious bodily injury shall, to the extent
6 that the person may do so without danger to self or others, provide assistance to the
7 person by obtaining or attempting to obtain aid from law enforcement or medical
8 personnel. A violation of this section is a Class 2 misdemeanor.
9 A person who, without compensation or expectation of compensation, provides
10 assistance in compliance with this section, is not liable for any civil damages as a result
11 of an act or omission by the person in providing the assistance, unless the person acts in
12 a willful, wanton, or reckless manner in providing the assistance. Immunity from liability
13 under this section does not apply to a person providing assistance in the regular course
14 of the person's employment and receiving or expecting to receive compensation for the
15 assistance.
