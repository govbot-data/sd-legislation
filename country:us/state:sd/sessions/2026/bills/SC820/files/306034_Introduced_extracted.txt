26.1050.9 101st Legislative Session SC820
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Commemoration 820
Introduced by: Senator Deibert
1 A LEGISLATIVE COMMEMORATION honoring Dr. Margaret Becker as the recipient of
2 the 2025 Copic Humanitarian Award.
3 WHEREAS, Dr. Margaret Becker, a board-certified family medicine physician, has provided
4 comprehensive care to patients of all ages at the Monument Health Spearfish Clinic and has,
5 for the past eight years, also served as the medical director of the Good Shepherd Free Clinic
6 in Spearfish; and
7 WHEREAS, through her work with the Good Shepherd Free Clinic, Dr. Becker has
8 addressed the health needs of countless individuals, who would otherwise have had to forego
9 medical attention; and
10 WHEREAS, for her unwavering dedication and selfless service, the South Dakota State
11 Medical Association presented Dr. Becker with the 2025 South Dakota Copic Humanitarian
12 Award; and
13 WHEREAS, Dr. Becker designated the Good Shepherd Free Clinic as the recipient of the
14 $10,000 Copic donation;
15 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT COMMEMORATED, by the One Hundred First Legislature of the
16 State of South Dakota, that Dr. Margaret Becker, the recipient of the 2025 Copic Humanitarian
17 Award, has expended innumerable hours providing volunteer medical services and helping to
18 improve the day-to-day lives of countless patients in western South Dakota.
