26.604.9 101st Legislative Session 1221
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1221
Introduced by: Representative Lems
1 An Act to establish a post-employment restriction for employees of the Governor's
2 Office of Economic Development and create a penalty therefor.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 1-16G:
5 Notwithstanding the provisions of § 53-9-8, an individual employed by the
6 Governor's Office of Economic Development may not, for a period of five years after
7 termination of employment with the office, accept compensation or employment from, or
8 enter into a contractual relationship with, any person that, during the individual's
9 employment with the Governor's Office of Economic Development, received, directly or
10 indirectly:
11 (1) Any grant, loan, moneys, or other financial assistance administered by the
12 Governor's Office of Economic Development; or
13 (2) Moneys from the employer's investment in South Dakota's future fund established
14 pursuant to § 61-5-29.1.
15 A violation of this section is a Class 1 misdemeanor.
