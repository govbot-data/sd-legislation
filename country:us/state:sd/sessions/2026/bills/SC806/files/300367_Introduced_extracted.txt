26.843.9 101st Legislative Session SC806
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Commemoration 806
Introduced by: Senators Schoenfish, Crabtree, Deibert, Hulse, Larson, Perry, Reed, Wipf, and Zikmund and
Representatives Greenfield, Bahmuller, Emery, Goodwin, Jordan, Kull, Moore, Muckey, Novstrup, and Reisch
1 A LEGISLATIVE COMMEMORATION commending and honoring the 2025-2026
2 Outstanding School Administrators of South Dakota: David Olson, Meade,
3 Middle School Principal; Kendra Becker, Redfield, Secondary School Principal;
4 Richie Hutchinson, Sioux Falls, Assistant Middle School Principal; Kay Bass,
5 Harrisburg, Assistant Secondary School Principal.
6 WHEREAS, these administrators have been elected by their peers for outstanding service
7 and dedication to education; and
8 WHEREAS, the combined years of service to the people of South Dakota and to the children
9 and youth of South Dakota of these four educational administrators is eighty-eight years; and
10 WHEREAS, each of these educators has demonstrated both a sincere interest in our
11 children as well as superior skills in their chosen field of educational administration;
12 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT COMMEMORATED, by the One Hundred First Legislature of the
13 State of South Dakota, that David Olson, Kendra Becker, Richie Hutchinson, and Kay Bass
14 are named as official representatives of the School Administrators of South Dakota and shall
15 continue their efforts to improve educational opportunities for the children of this state.
