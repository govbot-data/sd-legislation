15 Any legislative decision of a board of county commissioners is subject to the
16 referendum process. A legislative decision is one that enacts a permanent law or lays
17 down a rule of conduct or course of policy for the guidance of citizens or their officers. Any
18 matter of a permanent or general character is a legislative decision.
19 No administrative decision of a governing body is subject to the referendum
20 process, unless specifically authorized by this code. An administrative decision is one that
21 merely puts into execution a plan already adopted by the governing body itself or by the
22 Legislature. Supervision of a program is an administrative decision. Hiring, disciplining,
23 and setting the salaries of employees are administrative decisions.
