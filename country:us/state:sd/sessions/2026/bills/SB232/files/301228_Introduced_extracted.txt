26.970.13 101st Legislative Session 232
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 232
Introduced by: Senator Howard
1 An Act to impose a one-year moratorium on the construction or expansion of
2 hyperscale data centers.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, a moratorium is imposed
5 on any new construction or expansion of a hyperscale data center in this state. The
6 moratorium includes the expansion of any data center that is not currently a hyperscale
7 data center but, if expanded, would become a hyperscale data center.
8 The moratorium is in effect through June 30, 2027.
9 For purposes of this Act, "hyperscale data center" means a centralized repository:
10 (1) For the storage, management, dissemination, and processing of electronic data
11 and information; and
12 (2) Having a peak electrical demand of fifty megawatts or greater.
