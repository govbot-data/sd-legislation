26.759.10 101st Legislative Session 177
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 177
SENATE STATE AFFAIRS ENGROSSED
Introduced by: Senator Blanc
1 An Act to clarify the requirements for marking an optical scan ballot.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That § 12-18-16.1 be AMENDED:
4 12-18-16.1. If an optical scan ballot is used, the voter may use a pencil or other
5 marking instrument provided to mark the voter's choice on the ballot person in charge of
6 the election must:
7 (1) Provide a black ink pen for each in-person voter to mark the voter's choice on the
8 ballot; and
9 (2) Provide instructions to each voter who applies to vote by absentee ballot that the
10 voter use a black ink pen to mark the voter's choice on the ballot.
11 The person in charge of the election may not disqualify any ballot marked by a
12 writing instrument other than a black ink pen.
