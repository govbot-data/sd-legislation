ENROLLED
AN ACT
ENTITLED An Act to clarify the requirements for marking an optical scan ballot.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
Section 1. That § 12-18-16.1 be AMENDED:
12-18-16.1. If an optical scan ballot is used, the person in charge of the election
must:
(1) Provide a black ink pen for each in-person voter to mark the voter's choice on the
ballot; and
(2) Provide instructions to each voter who applies to vote by absentee ballot that the
voter use a black ink pen to mark the voter's choice on the ballot.
The person in charge of the election may not disqualify any ballot marked by a
writing instrument other than a black ink pen.
