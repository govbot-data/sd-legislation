4 Section 3. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 41-11:
5 Before an individual may salvage eggs from non-migratory game bird nests, which
6 have been or may be unintentionally destroyed during normal agricultural operations or
7 other lawful activities, the individual must obtain an avian special purpose salvage permit
8 from the Department of Game, Fish and Parks. The permit authorizes the individual to:
9 (1) Salvage eggs from non-migratory game bird nests;
10 (2) Incubate and hatch eggs from non-migratory game bird nests; and
11 (3) Band and release the birds at a developmental stage appropriate for survival.
12 The department shall develop and post a permit application on its website. A permit
13 under this section is valid until December thirty-first of each year.
14 Section 4. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 41-11:
15 The Game, Fish and Parks Commission shall, by rules promulgated in accordance
16 with chapter 1-26:
17 (1) Establish the criteria for determining the developmental stage at which and manner
18 in which hatched birds are to be released;
19 (2) Establish documentation requirements pertaining to the release of the birds; and
20 (3)_ Establish an annual fee for the permit, not in excess of ten dollars.
21 The department shall forward any moneys collected under this section to the state
22 treasurer for deposit in the department of game, fish and parks fund.
