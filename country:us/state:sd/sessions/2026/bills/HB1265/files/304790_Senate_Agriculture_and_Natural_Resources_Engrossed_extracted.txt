1 An Act to authorize the salvage of certain bird eggs.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That § 41-11-7 be AMENDED:
4 41-11-7. Except as permitted by statute, no person otherwise provided in this
5 section, an individual may not take or, have in possession, or intentionally break or destroy
6 any nest or the eggs of the kinds of birds, the taking or killing of which is at any time or
7 at all times prohibited. A violation of this section is a Class 2 misdemeanor.
8 Nothing in this section prohibits any egg salvage activity if:
9 (1) The individual holds a migratory bird special purpose salvage permit, issued by the
10 United States Fish and Wildlife Service, in accordance with 50 C.F.R § 21.95
11 (January 1, 2026); and
12 (2) The salvage activity does not involve the eggs of birds, the taking or killing of which
13 is at all times prohibited.
