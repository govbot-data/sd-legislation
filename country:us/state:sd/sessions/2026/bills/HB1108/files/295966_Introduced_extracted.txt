26.55.11 101st Legislative Session 1108
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1108
Introduced by: Representative Stevens
1 An Act to allow for the sealing of court files in dismissed protection order cases.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 22-19A:
4 If the court dismisses a petition filed pursuant to § 22-19A-8, either initially or
5 after a hearing held pursuant to § 22-19A-10, the court may then grant the immediate
6 sealing of the court file containing the petition.
7 Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 25-10:
8 If the court dismisses a petition filed pursuant to § 25-10-3, either initially or after
9 a hearing held pursuant to § 25-10-4, the court may then grant the immediate sealing of
10 the court file containing the petition.
