House Bill 1108
ENROLLED
AN ACT
ENTITLED An Act to require the sealing of court files upon dismissal or denial of a
petition for a protection order.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 22-19A:
If the court dismisses a petition filed pursuant to § 22-19A-8 without a hearing
pursuant to § 22-19A-10, the clerk of court must immediately seal the petition and any
supporting documents in the case.
Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 22-19A:
If the court denies a petition filed pursuant to § 22-19A-8 after a hearing pursuant
to § 22-19A-10, the court must seal the case upon a finding on the record that the petition
was filed with no basis in fact or law and was harassing, frivolous, or abusive in nature.
The court shall notify law enforcement of any sealing order issued pursuant to this
section. Upon notification, law enforcement must make any record of the petition
nonpublic.
Section 3. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 25-10:
If the court dismisses a petition filed pursuant to § 25-10-3 without a hearing held
pursuant to § 25-10-4, the clerk of court must immediately seal the petition and any
supporting documents in the case.
Section 4. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 25-10:
If the court denies a petition filed pursuant to § 25-10-3 without a hearing held
pursuant to § 25-10-4, the court must seal the case upon a finding on the record that the
