26.704.10 101st Legislative Session 1163
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1163
Introduced by: Representative Andera
1 An Act to prohibit certain persons from requiring genetic-based vaccinations.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That § 34-22-18 be AMENDED:
4 34-22-18. Any person Except as otherwise provided in this section, an individual
5 in the state reasonably suspected of having active tuberculosis, middle east respiratory
6 syndrome (MERS), severe acute respiratory syndrome (SARS), smallpox, or viral
7 hemorrhagic fevers, or any disease or condition which that is the subject of a declared
8 public health emergency pursuant to § 34-22-42, shall accept a necessary diagnosis or
9 treatment, or both. Any person individual who intentionally refuses to accept the diagnosis
10 or treatment, or both, or who fails to follow the reasonable and necessary directives of
11 the department issued for the protection of other persons individuals, is guilty of a Class
12 1 misdemeanor.
13 An individual may not be required to receive a genetic-based vaccination, as
14 defined in section 2 of this Act, for treatment of a disease or condition that is the subject
15 of a declared public health emergency.
16 Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to title 34:
17 For the purpose of sections 3 to 7, inclusive, of this Act, "genetic-based vaccination"
18 means a vaccination developed using messenger ribonucleic acid technology, modified
19 messenger ribonucleic technology, self-amplifying messenger ribonucleic technology,
20 deoxyribonucleic technology, or other technology that functions by utilizing, modifying, or
21 transferring genetic material to induce an immune response.
22 Section 3. That a NEW SECTION be added to title 34:
23 No public or private employer may:
