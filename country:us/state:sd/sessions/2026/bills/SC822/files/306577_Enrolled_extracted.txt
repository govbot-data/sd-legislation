Senate Commemoration 822
Introduced by: Senators Wipf, Crabtree, Davis, Duhamel, Howard, Hulse, Mehlhaff, Otten, Perry, Peterson (Sue),
Pischke, Reed, Rohl, Sauder, Schoenfish, and Voita and Representatives Emery, Fitzgerald, Muckey, Roby, and
Wittman
A LEGISLATIVE COMMEMORATION honoring TKO’s Custom Catering and Capitol Cafe
for outstanding service.
WHEREAS, the team of TKO’s Custom Catering and Capitol Cafe begins its work before
many legislators arrive and continues to provide delicious food through the long days of the
legislative session, thereby contributing to the efficiency, positive morale, and overall
experience of all those in the Capitol Building; and
WHEREAS, the TKO team's commitment to excellence in both food and service has earned
the recognition and appreciation of legislators, staff, and visitors; and
WHEREAS, the team's individualized attention in providing high-quality food service
ensures that all those within the Capitol Building are well nourished and can focus on serving
the people of South Dakota;
NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT COMMEMORATED, by the One Hundred First Legislature of the
State of South Dakota, that TKO’s Custom Catering and Capitol Cafe deserves honor and
recognition for its outstanding effort and dedication in providing delicious meals throughout
the legislative session.
