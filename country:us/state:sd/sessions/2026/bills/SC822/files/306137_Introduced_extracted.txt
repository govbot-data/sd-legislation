26.1032.11 101st Legislative Session SC822
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Commemoration 822
Introduced by: Senator Wipf
1 A LEGISLATIVE COMMEMORATION honoring TKO’s Custom Catering and Capitol Cafe
2 for outstanding service.
3 WHEREAS, the team of TKO’s Custom Catering and Capitol Café begins its work before
4 many legislators arrive and continues to provide delicious food through the long days of the
5 legislative session, thereby contributing to the efficiency, positive morale, and overall
6 experience of all those in the Capitol Building; and
7 WHEREAS, the TKO team's commitment to excellence in both food and service has earned
8 the recognition and appreciation of legislators, staff, and visitors; and
9 WHEREAS, the team's individualized attention in providing high-quality food service
10 ensures that all those within the Capitol Building are well nourished and can focus on serving
11 the people of South Dakota;
12 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT COMMEMORATED, by the One Hundred First Legislature of the
13 State of South Dakota, that TKO’s Custom Catering and Capitol Cafe deserves honor and
14 recognition for its outstanding effort and dedication in providing delicious meals throughout
15 the legislative session.
