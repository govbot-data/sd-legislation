26.1022.11 101st Legislative Session SC811
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Commemoration 811
Introduced by: Senators Schoenfish, Deibert, and Perry and Representative Muckey
1 A LEGISLATIVE COMMEMORATION commending and honoring Amber Vogt, a
2 member of the Lead-Deadwood School District Board of Education, on her
3 selection as the Associated School Boards of South Dakota Outstanding
4 School Board Member of South Dakota for 2025.
5 WHEREAS, Amber Vogt is deeply committed to her district and is regularly an outlet for
6 students, staff, and community members, which makes her decisions as a board member
7 very informed; and
8 WHEREAS, she is a strong advocate for increased opportunities for students in this state
9 and has been instrumental in the process of helping the district approve substantial salary
10 increases; and
11 WHEREAS, Ms. Vogt truly listens to staff on contract language requests and is very good
12 at building consensus on the board; and
13 WHEREAS, Ms. Vogt leads with her heart and is a smart and savvy board member who
14 gives a great deal to her school district;
15 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT COMMEMORATED, by the One Hundred First Legislature of the
16 State of South Dakota, that Amber Vogt of the Lead-Deadwood School District Board of
17 Education, is recognized for her selection as Outstanding School Board Member of 2025, and
18 for her efforts to seek enhancement of the educational opportunities for the K-12 students of
19 this state.
