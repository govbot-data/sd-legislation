1 An Act to authorize the possession and self-administration of diabetes medications
2 by a student on school property and at school-related events and activities.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 13-32-10 be AMENDED:
5 13-32-10. Terms used in this section and §§ 13-32-11 to 13-32-13, inclusive,
6 mean:
7 (1) "Medication," an inhaled bronchodilator or, auto-injectable epinephrine, or both
8 insulin, or other diabetes medication;
9 (2) "Parent," any person standing in parental relation;
10 (3) "Related equipment and supplies," medical equipment and supplies necessary for
11 a student's management and treatment of diabetes; and
12 (4) "School," any public or nonpublic school;
13 (4) "Self-administration of prescription medication," a student's discretionary use of
14 prescription asthma or anaphylaxis medication, or both.
15 Section 2. That § 13-32-11 be AMENDED:
16 13-32-11. Any student with asthma or anaphylaxis may possess and self-
17 administer prescription medication, and possess and use related equipment and supplies,
18 while on school property or at a school-related event or activity only if:
19 (1) The student has been diagnosed with asthma, anaphylaxis, or diabetes, by a
20 licensed health care provider;
21 (2) The prescription medication has been prescribed for that student as indicated by
22 the prescription label on the medication, or by a prescriber's school orders;
23 (2) The self-administration is done in compliance with the prescription or written
24 instructions from the student's physician or other licensed health care provider;
