1 An Act to authorize the possession and self-administration of nasal glucagon by a
2 student on school property and at school-related events and activities.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 13-32-10 be AMENDED:
5 13-32-10. Terms used in this section and §§ 13-32-11 to 13-32-13, inclusive,
6 mean:
7 (1) "Medication," an inhaled bronchodilator or, auto-injectable epinephrine, or both
8 nasal glucagon;
9 (2) "Parent," any person standing in parental relation; and
10 (3) "School," any public or nonpublic school;
11 (4) "Self-administration of prescription medication," a student's discretionary use of
12 prescription asthma or anaphylaxis medication, or both.
13 Section 2. That § 13-32-11 be AMENDED:
14 13-32-11. Any student with asthma or anaphylaxis may possess and self-
15 administer prescription medication while on school property or at a school-related event
16 or activity only if:
17 (1) The student has been diagnosed with asthma, anaphylaxis, or diabetes, by a
18 licensed health care provider;
19 (2) The prescription medication has been prescribed for that student as indicated by
20 the prescription label on the medication;
21 (2) The self-administration is done in compliance with the prescription or written
22 instructions from the student's physician or other licensed health care provider;
23 and
24 (3) A parent of the student The student's parent provides to the school:
