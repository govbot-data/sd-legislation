26.881.15 101st Legislative Session 1206
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1206
Introduced by: Representative Novstrup
1 An Act to clarify the use of public funds for the purpose of alternative instruction.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 13-13:
4 Any parent, guardian, or other person having control of a child who has a
5 notification of alternative instruction on file with the Department of Education or the school
6 district where the child is a resident, may not apply for or receive state public funds, as
7 defined by § 4-4-2, for the purpose of funding the child's education.
