26.291.9 101st Legislative Session 1015
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1015
Introduced by: The Chair of the Committee on Judiciary at the request of the Chief Justice
1 An Act to create a pilot program in the Unified Judicial System to develop a pretrial
2 release program.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. The Unified Judicial System shall conduct a pilot program to allow a committing
5 magistrate judge or circuit court judge to place a person charged with a misdemeanor or
6 felony offense under the supervision of a presentence court services officer. The committing
7 magistrate judge or circuit court judge may impose conditions of release pursuant to § 23A-
8 43-3.
9 Section 2. The Unified Judicial System shall report to the Legislature the results of the pilot
10 program on or before December 31, 2027. The report must include:
11 (1) Information on any pretrial screenings and assessments completed;
12 (2) The number of cases supervised;
13 (3) The number of cases in which the person supervised:
14 (a) Appeared for all scheduled court appearances;
15 (b) Was charged with a new criminal offense;
16 (c) Failed to appear at a court date and a warrant was issued by the court; or
17 (d) Did not have bond revoked by the court; and
18 (4) Costs associated with the program.
19 Section 3. The pilot program will expire on December 31, 2027.
