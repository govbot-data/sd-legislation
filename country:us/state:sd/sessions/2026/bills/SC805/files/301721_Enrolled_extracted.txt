Senate Commemoration 805
Introduced by: Senators Schoenfish, Crabtree, Deibert, Duhamel, Hulse, Otten, Perry, Reed, Vilhauer, Wipf, and
Zikmund and Representatives Greenfield, Aylward, Bahmuller, Emery, Goodwin, Heermann, Jordan, Kull, Moore,
Muckey, Novstrup, Pourier, Reisch, Rice, Roby, and Sjaarda
A LEGISLATIVE COMMEMORATION commending and honoring the 2024-2025
Outstanding School Administrators of South Dakota: Dr. Jeff Danielsen,
Watertown, School Superintendent; Bo Beck, Aberdeen, Middle School
Principal; Connie Alspach, Dupree, School Business Official; Jay Swatek, Tea,
Elementary School Principal; Kelly Messmer, Harding County, Secondary
School Principal; Sherri Nelson, Brandon Valley, Curriculum Leader; and
Monica Waltman, Douglas, Director of Special Education.
WHEREAS, these administrators have been elected by their peers for outstanding service and
dedication to education; and
WHEREAS, the combined years of service to the people of South Dakota and to the children
and youth of South Dakota of these seven educational administrators is one hundred ninety-seven
years; and
WHEREAS, each of these educators has demonstrated both a sincere interest in our children
as well as superior skills in their chosen field of educational administration;
NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT COMMEMORATED, by the One Hundred First Legislature of the State
of South Dakota, that Dr. Jeff Danielsen, Bo Beck, Connie Alspach, Jay Swatek, Kelly Messmer,
Sherri Nelson, and Monica Waltman are named as official representatives of the School
Administrators of South Dakota and shall continue their efforts to improve the educational
opportunities for the children of this state.
