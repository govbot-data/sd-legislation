1 Section 3. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 36-15:
2 If an applicant licensed by another state, district, territory, or foreign country holds
3 a cosmetology-related license that is not equivalent to a cosmetology, nail technology, or
4 esthetics license in this state, the commission must allow credit for relevant work
5 experience to apply toward the hours of education required for the cosmetology, nail
6 technician, or esthetics license, as applicable.
7 The commission shall credit two hours of work experience in the applicable field as
8 one hour of school education. Only work experience that occurred after the applicant was
9 initially licensed may be credited. The work experience hours may not be credited for more
10 than half of the hours of education required for the license.
11 The applicant shall submit documentation to the commission, in the form and
12 manner required by the commission, demonstrating the relevant work experience. The
13 commission may deny the issuance of a license, regardless of the amount of work
14 experience submitted by the applicant, if the commission determines that the applicant is
15 not competent for the applicable license.
16 If the applicant obtained the work experience more than five years before the date
17 of application, the commission may require the applicant to take a commission-approved
18 safety and infection control procedures course.
