26.732.21 101st Legislative Session 1180
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1180
Introduced by: Representative Mortenson
1 An Act to ensure the validity of non-compete agreements in the context of jointly
2 owned business entities.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 53-9:
5 In any governing document of a business entity, or as part of a contract for the
6 purchase, sale, or transfer of an ownership interest in the entity, the parties may agree
7 that an owner, after the transfer of the interest in the entity, may not engage, directly or
8 indirectly, in the same or similar type of business conducted by the entity during the
9 owner's period of ownership, within the specified geographic area in which the entity
10 conducts business, for a period not exceeding three years from the date of transfer.
