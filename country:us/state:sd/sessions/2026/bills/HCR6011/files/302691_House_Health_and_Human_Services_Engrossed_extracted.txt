26.886.10 101st Legislative Session HCR6011
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Concurrent Resolution
6011
HOUSE HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES ENGROSSED
Introduced by: Representative Garcia
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION encouraging the Executive Board of the Legislative
2 Research Council to form an interim Neurohealth Safety Committee to study
3 and assess the regulatory framework under which tobacco and vapor product
4 retail establishments operate.
5 WHEREAS, rules that are unclear in their intent and direction create compliance challenges
6 for tobacco and vapor product retailers; and
7 WHEREAS, the lack of clarity has the potential to affect a variety of regulatory areas,
8 including the payment of fees and the scheduling and conduct of compliance inspections; and
9 WHEREAS, a committee that brings interested parties into a conversation about rules and
10 compliance stands the best chance of clarifying the future obligations of all parties;
11 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, by the House of Representatives of the One Hundred
12 First Legislature of the State of South Dakota, the Senate concurring therein, that the
13 Executive Board of the Legislative Research Council be encouraged to form the interim
14 Neurohealth Safety Committee for the purpose of studying the regulatory framework under
15 which tobacco and vapor product retailers operate and to assess the extent of the need for
16 further regulatory action.
