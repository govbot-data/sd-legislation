26.209.9 101st Legislative Session 1152
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1152
Introduced by: Representative Greenfield
1 An Act to require that the secretary of the Department of Education apply to the
2 United States Department of Education for the authority to develop and
3 implement an innovative assessment system.
4 Section 1. The secretary of the South Dakota Department of Education shall apply to the
5 United States Department of Education, pursuant to 20 U.S.C. § 6364 (January 1, 2026), for
6 the authority to establish an innovative assessment system.
7 Section 2. Upon receipt of a notice indicating that the secretary's application has been
8 approved by the United States Department of Education, the secretary shall, in consultation
9 with school administrators, teachers, parents, and other education experts, develop and
10 implement an assessment model that:
11 (1) Meets the requirements for an innovative assessment system established in 20
12 U.S.C. § 6364 (January 1, 2026);
13 (2) Corresponds with the requirements for an academic achievement assessment
14 established in § 13-3-55;
15 (3) Accurately measures student growth in reading and mathematics;
16 (4) Aligns with evidence-based literacy practices founded upon the science of reading;
17 and
18 (5) Reinforces the mastery of foundational mathematics skills.
