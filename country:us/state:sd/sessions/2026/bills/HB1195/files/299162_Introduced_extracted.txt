26.781.9 101st Legislative Session 1195
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1195
Introduced by: Representative Emery
1 An Act to provide free admission to state parks and recreation areas for residents
2 enrolled as members of Indian tribes.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 41-17:
5 Any South Dakota resident who is an enrolled member of an Indian tribe may
6 obtain free admission to any South Dakota state park or recreation area. The qualifying
7 individual may also receive a fifty percent discount on any camping fee or associated
8 electrical service fee established pursuant to § 41-17-1.1 or 41-17-14.1.
9 Any individual who applies for and qualifies for free admission under the provisions
10 of this section must be issued a lifetime park entrance license by the Department of Game,
11 Fish and Parks. The Game, Fish and Parks Commission shall promulgate rules pursuant to
12 chapter 1-26 establishing criteria and procedures for obtaining free admission and reduced
13 fees pursuant to this section and for the issuance of a lifetime park entrance license.
