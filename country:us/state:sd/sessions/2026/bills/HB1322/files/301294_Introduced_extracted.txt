26.85.10 101st Legislative Session 1322
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1322
Introduced by: Representative Randolph
1 An Act to revise a provision related to the transportation of an inmate upon
2 discharge from a correctional facility.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 24-5-3 be AMENDED:
5 24-5-3. If not already provided, Upon discharge of an inmate from a correctional
6 facility, whether by parole, suspended sentence, or final discharge, a correctional facility
7 official shall must provide every the inmate with suitable:
8 (1) Suitable clothing, a;
9 (2) A sum of money to be determined by the secretary of corrections, and
10 transportation; and
11 (3) Transportation to the place one of the following locations, as chosen by the inmate:
12 (a) The county where the inmate resided at the time of the offense that resulted
13 in imprisonment;
14 (b) The county where the inmate received sentence; or an
15 (c) A location equivalent in distance upon discharge from a correctional facility,
16 whether by parole, suspended sentence, or final discharge to either
17 subsection (a) or (b), whichever is lesser in mileage.
