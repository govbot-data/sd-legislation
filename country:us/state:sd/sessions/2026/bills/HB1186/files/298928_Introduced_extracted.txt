26.796.15 101st Legislative Session 1186
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1186
Introduced by: Representative Jorgenson
1 An Act to require the approval of the county for the creation of a tax increment
2 financing district by a municipality.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 11-9:
5 No municipality may create a district unless the board of county commissioners of
6 each county in which the district is located, either in whole or in part, has consented to
7 the creation of the district by resolution.
