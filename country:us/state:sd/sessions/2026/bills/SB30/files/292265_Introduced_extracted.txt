26.307.15 101st Legislative Session 30
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 30
Introduced by: The Chair of the Committee on State Affairs at the request of the Secretary of State
1 An Act to amend the reasons for challenging an individual's eligibility to vote.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That § 12-4-59 be AMENDED:
4 12-4-59. An application for the correction of any precinct registration list or to
5 challenge the right to vote of any registered voter may be filed by the secretary of state,
6 the auditor of the county where the challenged voter is registered, or any other individual
7 who is registered as a voter in the same county where the challenged voter is registered,
8 not later than the ninetieth day before the day of a primary, runoff, special, or general
9 election. The application for correction filed pursuant to this section may only challenge
10 only whether a voter:
11 (1) Has voted or has registered to vote in another state; or
12 (2) Is a resident of this state, pursuant to § 12-1-4 as defined in § 12-1-4; or
13 (3) Is a United States citizen.
