119A 101st Legislative Session 119
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 119
AMENDME NT 119A
FOR THE INTRODUCED BILL
Introduced by: Senator Otten
1 An Act to make an appropriation to enhance the economic health of South
2 Dakotacreate the capitol restoration fund and to transfer moneys thereto.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated from the general fund the sum of $101 to the state,
5 for purposes of enhancing the economic health of South Dakota.
6 Section 2. The state shall approve vouchers and the state auditor shall draw warrants to pay
7 expenditures authorized by this Act.
8 Section 3. Any amounts appropriated in this Act not lawfully expended or obligated shall
9 revert in accordance with the procedures prescribed in chapter 4-8.
10 Section 4. This Act is effective June 30, 2026.
11 Section 1. The state treasurer shall transfer $25,000,000 from the general fund to the capitol
12 restoration fund, created in section 2 of this Act.
13 Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 5-15:
14 There is created the capitol restoration fund, to be administered by the Bureau of
15 Human Resources and Administration, for the purpose of restoring, renovating, and
16 improving the state capitol building.
17 Expenditures from the fund must be by special appropriation of the Legislature.
18 Any interest earned on moneys in the fund must remain in the fund.
19 Section 3. This act is effective June 29, 2026.
