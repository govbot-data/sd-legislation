26.833.14 101st Legislative Session 119
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 119
ENROLLED
AN ACT
ENTITLED An Act to create the capitol restoration fund and to transfer moneys
thereto.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
Section 1. The state treasurer shall transfer $1,100,000 from the public buildings fund,
created in § 5-15-29.2 to the capitol restoration fund, created in section 2 of this Act.
Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 5-15:
There is created the capitol restoration fund, to be administered by the Bureau of
Human Resources and Administration, for the purpose of restoring, renovating, and
improving the state capitol building.
Expenditures from the fund must be by special appropriation of the Legislature.
Any interest earned on moneys in the fund must remain in the fund.
Section 3. This Act is effective June 29, 2026.
