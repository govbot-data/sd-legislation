26.437.23 101st Legislative Session 1251
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1251
Introduced by: Representative Mulder
1 An Act to create the community-based providers methodology supplement fund,
2 create the target teacher salary supplement fund, and provide for the transfer
3 of certain unobligated cash balances to the fund.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
5 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 13-13:
6 There is created in the state treasury the target teacher salary supplement fund.
7 The fund consists of moneys specified for deposit in the fund pursuant to § 4-7-32. The
8 Department of Education shall administer the fund. The purpose of the fund is to enable
9 an increase to the target teacher salary, as set forth in § 13-13-10.1.
10 The department, as part of the annual budget submission, in accordance with § 4-
11 7-7, shall submit a budget request utilizing money from the fund to provide an increase
12 in the target teacher salary, as set forth in § 13-13-10.1.
13 Interest on moneys credited to the fund must remain in the fund.
14 Expenditures from the fund must be budgeted through the general appropriation
15 bill.
16 Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 28-22:
17 There is created in the state treasury the community-based providers methodology
18 supplement fund. The fund consists of moneys specified for deposit in the fund pursuant
19 to § 4-7-32. The Department of Human Services shall administer the fund. The purpose
20 of the fund is to enable an increase in the rates payable to the types of community-based
21 providers listed in § 28-22-6, in order for the rate methodology of each provider to be
22 closer to one hundred percent of the methodology established in chapter 28-22.
23 The department, as part of the annual budget submission, in accordance with § 4-
24 7-7, shall submit a budget request utilizing money from the fund to provide a uniform
