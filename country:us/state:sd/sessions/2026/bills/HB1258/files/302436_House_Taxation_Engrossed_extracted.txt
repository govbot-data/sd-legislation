26.146.30 101st Legislative Session 1258
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1258
HOUSE TAXATION ENGROSSED
Introduced by: The Committee on Local Government
1 An Act to require that a property tax bill provide access to a state website providing
2 property tax information.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 10-21-1.2 be AMENDED:
5 10-21-1.2. For purposes of chapters 10-21 to 10-26, inclusive, the following terms
6 mean:
7 (1) "Courthouse," the county courthouse, the administrative building where the county
8 office of treasurer is located, or a building where the board of county
9 commissioners meet;
10 (2) "QR code", a quick response code taking the form of a two-dimensional matrix
11 barcode intended to be digitally scanned; and
12 (3) "Treasurer," the county treasurer.
13 Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 10-21:
14 The property tax bill sent to each taxpayer pursuant to § 10-21-1.1 must include
15 a QR code and an internet address, connecting the taxpayer to a website maintained by
16 the Department of Revenue which provides supplementary information on property
17 taxation, and on property tax relief programs, in this state.
18 The tax bill must provide notice to the taxpayer that further tax information may
19 be found by using the QR code and the internet address.
Underscores indicate new language.
Overstrikes indicate deleted language.
