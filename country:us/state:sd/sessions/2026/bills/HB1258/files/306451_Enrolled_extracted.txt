2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1258
ENROLLED
AN ACT
ENTITLED An Act to require that a property tax bill provide access to a state website
providing property tax information.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
Section 1. That § 10-21-1.2 be AMENDED:
10-21-1.2. For purposes of chapters 10-21 to 10-26, inclusive, the following terms
mean:
(1) "Courthouse," the county courthouse, the administrative building where the county
office of treasurer is located, or a building where the board of county
commissioners meet;
(2) "QR code", a quick response code taking the form of a two-dimensional matrix
barcode intended to be digitally scanned; and
(3) "Treasurer," the county treasurer.
Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 10-21:
The property tax bill sent to each taxpayer pursuant to § 10-21-1.1 must include
a QR code and an internet address, connecting the taxpayer to a website maintained by
the Department of Revenue which provides supplementary information on property
taxation, and on property tax relief programs, in this state.
The tax bill must provide notice to the taxpayer that further tax information may
be found by using the QR code and the internet address.
