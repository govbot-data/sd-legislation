26.329.33 101st Legislative Session 143
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 143
Introduced by: Senator Carley
1 An Act to require that the Department of Health annually report on public health
2 effects of cannabis use.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 34-20G:
5 On or before March first of each year, the department shall provide a report to the
6 legislature. The report must include, for the preceding calendar year:
7 (1) The expenses incurred and revenues generated from the medical cannabis
8 program;
9 (2) The impact of medical cannabis on public safety, public health, and on behavioral
10 health services; and
11 (3) The number of:
12 (a) Applications received by the department for registry identification cards;
13 (b) Cannabis-related poisonings;
14 (c) Drug-related deaths in which a toxicology screen detected metabolites;
15 (d) Hospitalizations in this state related to cannabis use;
16 (e) Licensing violations determined by the department;
17 (f) Medical cannabis establishments by type;
18 (g) Registered qualifying patients by medical condition;
19 (h) Practitioners, by specialty, who have provided written certifications;
20 (i) Qualifying patients and designated caregivers;
21 (j) Qualifying patient demographics by age and sex; and
22 (k) Registry identification cards revoked.
