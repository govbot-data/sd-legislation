26.671.10 101st Legislative Session 1054
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1054
ENROLLED
AN ACT
ENTITLED An Act to repeal the creation and budgeting for the Digital Dakota
Network.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
Section 1. That § 1-26D-5 be AMENDED:
1-26D-5. The chief hearing examiner shall promulgate rules, pursuant to chapter
1-26, for the operation of the Office of Hearing Examiners and for the procedures to be
used with regard to hearing contested cases.
Section 2. That § 1-33-38 be AMENDED:
1-33-38. The head of the Bureau of Information and Telecommunications is the
commissioner of information and telecommunications.
The bureau is administered by the commissioner, who shall:
(1) Provide assistance, under the rules and regulations provided in this section, as
necessary for the performance of the official duties imposed upon the various
departments and divisions by this code;
(2) Employ staff and maintain facilities as necessary to operate a local government
services program that arranges or provides services for public corporations
pursuant to § 1-33-45;
(3) Create a telecommunications network that:
(a) Provides cost effective services for business, education, government, and
rural economic development; and
(b) Ensures network uses are consistent with the best interests of the state and
telecommunications network users; and
(4) Administer policies and promulgate rules, pursuant to chapter 1-26, related to the
management, programming, and use of the state information network.
