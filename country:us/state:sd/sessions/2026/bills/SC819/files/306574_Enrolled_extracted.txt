Senate Commemoration 819
Introduced by: Senators Jensen (Kevin), Crabtree, Duhamel, Perry, and Zikmund and Representatives Aylward,
Emery, Lems, Moore, Muckey, and Shubeck
A LEGISLATIVE COMMEMORATION honoring the 2025-2026 Canton C-Hawk girls
wrestling team.
WHEREAS, the Canton C-Hawk girls won the Region 2 regional tournament for the second
consecutive year in February 2026; and
WHEREAS, the Canton C-Hawk girls had thirteen disciplined and dedicated wrestlers qualify for
the state tournament; and
WHEREAS, the 2026 C-Hawk state qualifiers were Jewel Gannon (100 pounds), Charlee Ann
Winter (105 pounds), Avery Kranz (110 pounds), Nayeli Ortega (115 pounds), Brooke Warejcka
(120 pounds), Finley Evjen (125 pounds), Marin Rhode (130 pounds), Kenzie King (140 pounds),
Lakynn Qualseth (145 pounds), Alyssa McCracken (155 pounds), Sophia Larsen (170 pounds),
Natalia Long (190 pounds), and Kori Wallner (235 pounds); and
WHEREAS, each C-Hawk girl wrestling in the placement rounds won her match, and Brooke
Warejcka (120 pounds) and Finley Evjen (125 pounds) won state championships for the C-Hawks;
and
WHEREAS, the C-Hawk girls team lived up to the high expectations set by coaches Camron
Groenke, Jeremy Ask, Brandon Jarman, and Jason Bartels;
NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT COMMEMORATED, by the One Hundred First Legislature of the State
of South Dakota, that the Canton C-Hawk girls are great competitors, great leaders, and the 2026
State Wrestling Tournament champions.
