ENROLLED
AN ACT
ENTITLED An Act to revise a provision related to the venue of adoption proceedings.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
Section 1. That § 25-6-7 be AMENDED:
25-6-7. A petition for adoption may be filed in the circuit court for any of the
following counties:
(1) The county in which the adopting parent resides;
(2) The county in which the authorized agency having care, custody, or control,
whether legal or physical, of the child involved is located; or
(3) The county in which the child involved was born or resides.
