2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1197
Introduced by: Representative Hughes
1 An Act to revise a provision related to the venue of adoption proceedings.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That § 25-6-7 be AMENDED:
4 25-6-7. The circuit court for the county of the child's legal residence or of the
5 adopting parent's legal residence according to where petition is first filed shall have the
6 original jurisdictionA petition for adoption may be filed in the circuit court for any of the
7 following counties:
8 (1) The county in which the adopting parent resides;
9 (2) The county in which the authorized agency having care, custody, or control,
10 whether legal or physical, of the child involved is located; or
11 (3) The county in which the child involved was born or resides.
