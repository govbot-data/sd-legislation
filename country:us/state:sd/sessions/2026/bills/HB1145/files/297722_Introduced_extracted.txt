13 (2) Has had Have two years of experience under appropriate supervision in the field of
14 specialization in which the applicant will practice (e.g., psychotherapy, community
15 organization, or planning); and
16 (3) Has passed Pass an examination prepared by the board for this purpose, or meet
17 the additional professional experience requirements set forth in section 3 of this
18 Act; and
19 (4) Submit an application to the board, accompanied by the fee authorized in § 36-26-
20 19.
21 A person who obtains licensure as a certified social worker in private, independent
22 practice without passing the examination required by this section is not eligible to
