26.354.31 101st Legislative Session 1182
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1182
Introduced by: Representative Jorgenson
1 An Act to establish a data collection system pertaining to assisted reproductive
2 technology.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 1-43:
5 The Department of Health shall establish and maintain a data collection system for
6 the purpose of determining, on an annual basis:
7 (1) The number of embryos created through assisted reproductive technology;
8 (2) The number of embryos that are implanted through assisted reproductive
9 technology;
10 (3) The method of disposition used for embryos that are not implanted during assisted
11 reproduction, including:
12 (a) Cryopreservation at a facility in this state;
13 (b) Donation to another;
14 (c) Compassionate implantation into the uterus of the female for purpose of
15 natural disposition; or
16 (d) Other means;
17 (4) The number of embryos transferred out of this state for the purposes of:
18 (a) Cryopreservation;
19 (b) Donation to another; or
20 (c) Medical research;
21 (5) The number of disposed embryos that were:
22 (a) Accidentally destroyed; or
23 (b) Deliberately destroyed;
24 (6) The number of pregnancies resulting from assisted reproduction; and
25 (7) The number of live births resulting from assisted reproduction.
Underscores indicate new language.
Overstrikes indicate deleted language.

26.354.31 2 1182
1 Each entity that participates in assisted reproductive technology in this state shall
2 provide the data required by this section to the Department of Health at the time and in
3 the manner requested by the department.
4 The department shall compile the information and make the information publicly
5 available on its website on or before July first of each year.
