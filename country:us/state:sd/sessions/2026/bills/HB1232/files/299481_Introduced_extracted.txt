26.900.19 101st Legislative Session 1232
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1232
Introduced by: Representative Pourier
1 An Act to establish principles for a state agency to consider when formulating or
2 implementing a policy or rule that has direct tribal implications.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 1-54:
5 When formulating or implementing any policy or administrative rules that have
6 direct tribal implications, a state agency shall document its consideration of the following
7 principles:
8 (1) Commitment to cooperation and collaboration between the agency and the Indian
9 Tribes;
10 (2) Understanding of and respect for the Indian tribes as sovereign nations and the
11 government-to-government relationship between the state and each tribe;
12 (3) Regular and early communication with tribal officials;
13 (4) Accountability when addressing issues raised by tribal officials regarding the policy
14 or rules; and
15 (5) Preservation of the tribal-state relationship.
