26.900.20 101st Legislative Session 1232
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1232
HOUSE STATE AFFAIRS ENGROSSED
Introduced by: Representative Pourier
1 An Act to establish principles for a state agency to consider when formulating or
2 implementing a program that has the potential of affecting tribal members
3 on a reservation.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
5 Section 1. That § 1-54-5 be AMENDED:
6 1-54-5. It is the policy of the state to consult with a tribal government regarding
7 the conduct of state government programs which that have the potential of affecting tribal
8 members on the a reservation. In developing or administering any program that has the
9 potential of affecting tribal members on a reservation, the state recognizes the following
10 guiding principles:
11 (1) Commitment to cooperation and collaboration between the agency and each Indian
12 tribe;
13 (2) Understanding of and respect for the Indian tribes as sovereign nations and the
14 government-to-government relationship between the state and each tribe;
15 (3) Regular and early communication with tribal officials;
16 (4) Accountability when addressing issues raised by tribal officials regarding the
17 program; and
18 (5) Preservation of the tribal-state relationship.
19 This section may not be construed to confer any substantive rights on any party in
20 any litigation or otherwise.
