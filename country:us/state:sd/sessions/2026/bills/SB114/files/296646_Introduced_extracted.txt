26.265.20 101st Legislative Session 114
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 114
Introduced by: Senator Pischke
1 An Act to provide for the publication of ballot images and the cast vote record of
2 automatic tabulating equipment.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 12-17B-13 be AMENDED:
5 12-17B-13. The procedure for process of tabulating the votes by using the
6 automatic tabulating equipment shall must be under the direction of the person in charge
7 of the election, and shall must conform to the specifications and requirements of the
8 automatic tabulating equipment.
9 The results printed by the automatic tabulating equipment shall constitute are the
10 immediate unofficial returns. These returns shall be open to the public and any cast vote
11 record or ballot images collected by the automatic tabulating equipment are public records
12 under chapter 1-27.
13 The final returns results printed by the automatic tabulating equipment shall must
14 be presented to the canvassing board for review and certification as the official returns.
15 For purposes of this section, "ballot image" means a digital rendering or facsimile
16 of a paper ballot tabulated in the election.
17 For purposes of this section, "cast vote record" means an electronic record of how
18 the marks on a ballot are tabulated as a vote for a candidate or other ballot question.
19 Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 12-17B:
20 Notwithstanding the provisions of § 12-17B-13, the governing body of the
21 jurisdiction conducting the election may not release any cast vote record or collection of
22 ballot images if the cast vote record or any ballot image:
23 (1) Indicates the date, time, or order in which a ballot was cast;
24 (2) Contains any unredacted personal information that may be used to identify the
25 voter who cast the ballot;
