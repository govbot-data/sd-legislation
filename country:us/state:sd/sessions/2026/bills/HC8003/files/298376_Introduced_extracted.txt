26.698.9 101st Legislative Session HC8003
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Commemoration 8003
Introduced by: Representatives Hunt, Aylward, Jensen (Phil), Jordan, Kull, Moore, and Muckey and
Senators Marty, Deibert, Howard, and Perry
1 A LEGISLATIVE COMMEMORATION congratulating and honoring Joyce Waddell on
2 her retirement from the Bison School District.
3 WHEREAS, Joyce Waddell is retiring from the Bison School District after fifty-six years of
4 dedicated service as a teacher, counselor, and librarian; and
5 WHEREAS, Joyce Waddell has devoted a total of sixty-one years to the education of South
6 Dakota students, including four years in Spearfish and one year in Isabel, demonstrating an
7 extraordinary lifetime commitment to public education; and
8 WHEREAS, Joyce has served as a common and enduring thread across generations within
9 her community, having taught parents, the children and grandchildren of her earliest
10 students, thereby shaping the educational foundation of countless families; and
11 WHEREAS, her influence extended well beyond the classroom, as she remained a
12 meaningful presence in the lives of former students through milestones such as weddings,
13 baby showers, and baptisms, reflecting her deep connection to the people of this community;
14 and
15 WHEREAS, Joyce exemplified the highest ideals of the teaching profession by living the
16 motto, “I do this for the love of the kids,” demonstrating compassion, patience, and
17 unwavering dedication to every student she served;
18 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT COMMEMORATED, by the One Hundred First Legislature of the
19 State of South Dakota, that Joyce Waddell's commitment to both education and community
20 has left an indelible impact on the Bison School District and this state.
