1 An Act to modify requirements regarding nondomiciled commercial driver licenses.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That § 32-12A-13 be AMENDED:
4 32-12A-13. The department may issue a nondomiciled commercial learner's permit or
5 commercial driver license to:
6 (1) A person who is domiciled in a foreign jurisdiction whose commercial motor vehicle
7 testing and licensing standards, as determined by the administrator of the Federal
8 Motor Carrier Safety Administration, do not meet the testing standards established
9 in 49 C.F.R. Part 383 as amended through January 1, 2015;
10 (2) A person who is domiciled in a state whose commercial driver licensing program
11 has been decertified by the administrator of the Federal Motor Carrier Safety
12 Administration.
13 an applicant who meets the requirements set forth in 49 C.F.R. §§ 383 and 384 (January
14 1, 2026). In addition, the department must observe the following conditions applicable to
15 the issuance of a nondomiciled commercial learner's permit or commercial driver license
16 in this state:
17 (1) An applicant may only be:
18 (a) An individual who has lawful, employment-based nonimmigrant status in
19 the H-2A, H-2B, or E-2 visa category; or
20 (b) An individual domiciled in a United States territory, who holds any of the
21 same employment-based visas described in subsection (a);
22 (2) Any non-citizen applicant, except for a lawful permanent resident, must provide an
23 unexpired foreign passport and an unexpired form I-94 or I-94A that indicates a
24 specified type of employment-based nonimmigrant status at every issuance,
25 transfer, renewal, and upgrade action;

17 state. The holder of a nondomiciled commercial learner's permit or commercial driver

19 learner's permit or commercial driver license issued to a person domiciled in this state.
20 A nondomiciled commercial learner's permit or commercial driver license issued

24 without a skills or knowledge test if the license has been expired for a period less than
