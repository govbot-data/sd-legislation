26.188.10 101st Legislative Session 1066
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1066
Introduced by: Representative Fitzgerald
1 An Act to revise a provision related to theft by a contractor, subcontractor, or
2 supplier.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 44-9-13 be AMENDED:
5 44-9-13. Any contractor, subcontractor, or supplier on any improvement of real
6 estate, mines, or public utilities within the purview of this chapter who knowingly uses
7 more than five hundred dollars of the proceeds of any payment made to him on account
8 of such improvement by the owner of such real estate or person having the improvement
9 made, having received or been entrusted with the property of another for the purpose of
10 improvement of real estate, and with the intent to defraud, appropriates that property for
11 any other purpose other than the payment for labor, skill, or materials, and machinery
12 contributed to such improvement while any account for such labor, skill, material, or
13 machinery furnished for such improvement up to the time of such payment remains unpaid
14 and due and owing under the credit terms arranged, for improvement of the real estate
15 for which it was received, is guilty of theft of the proceeds of such payment. It is not a
16 violation of this section to withhold funds from a contractor, subcontractor, or supplier
17 pending the completion and final approval of his work or product. Any person guilty of
18 theft pursuant to this section is subject to the penalties under § 22-30A-17.
