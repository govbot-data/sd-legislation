26.188.11 101st Legislative Session 1066
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1066
ENROLLED
AN ACT
ENTITLED An Act to revise a provision related to theft by a contractor,
subcontractor, or supplier.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
Section 1. That § 44-9-13 be AMENDED:
44-9-13. Any contractor, subcontractor, or supplier on any improvement of real
estate within the purview of this chapter who, having received or been entrusted with the
property of another for the purpose of improvement of real estate, and with the intent to
defraud, appropriates that property for any purpose other than the payment for labor,
skill, or materials for improvement of the real estate for which it was received, is guilty of
theft. Any person guilty of theft pursuant to this section is subject to the penalties under
§ 22-30A-17.
