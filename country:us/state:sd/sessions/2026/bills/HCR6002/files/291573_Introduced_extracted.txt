6002
Introduced by: Representative Manhart
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION Honoring the life and achievements of the late Charlie
2 Kirk.
3 WHEREAS, Charlie Kirk was born on October 14, 1993, in Arlington Heights, Illinois, and
4 rose to prominence as a dedicated advocate for American values and the American way of
5 life; and
6 WHEREAS, at the age of 18, Charlie Kirk founded Turning Point USA, an organization
7 committed to promoting free markets, limited government, fiscal responsibility, and
8 constitutional liberties among young people on college campuses across the nation; and
9 WHEREAS, through Turning Point USA, Charlie Kirk inspired thousands of students and
10 fostered a new generation of young leaders and activists who engaged in civic discourse and
11 political participation; and
12 WHEREAS, Charlie Kirk served as a close ally and advisor to President Donald J. Trump,
13 contributing to national policy discussions and grassroots efforts that emphasized patriotism,
14 economic opportunity, and individual freedoms; and
15 WHEREAS, Charlie Kirk's influential voice reached millions through his radio show,
16 podcasts, books, and public speaking engagements, all of which he used to champion causes
17 such as border security, religious liberty, and the protection of Second Amendment rights;
18 and
19 WHEREAS, on September 10, 2025, Charlie Kirk was tragically assassinated while
20 addressing an audience at Utah Valley University, cutting short a life of profound impact at
21 the age of 31; and
