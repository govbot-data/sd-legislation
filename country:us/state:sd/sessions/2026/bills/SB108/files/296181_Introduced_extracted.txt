26.347.15 101st Legislative Session 108
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 108
Introduced by: Senator Blanc
1 An Act to require that all recorded votes of a legislator be displayed on the
2 Legislative Research Council website.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 2-7:
5 The Legislative Research Council shall establish and maintain on a webpage for
6 each legislator a record of each vote made by the legislator on each bill and resolution
7 voted on in that legislator's chamber and in each committee to which the legislator is
8 appointed during each session and special session of the Legislature convened after July
9 1, 2026. If the legislator is absent or excused from a vote, the legislator's absence or
10 excusal must be recorded on the webpage. The voting record for each legislator must be
11 prominently displayed and easily accessible to the public as a tab on the legislator's profile
12 page.
