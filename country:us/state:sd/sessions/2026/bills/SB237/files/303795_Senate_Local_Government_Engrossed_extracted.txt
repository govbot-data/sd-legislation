26.919.10 101st Legislative Session 237
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 237
SENATE LOCAL GOVERNMENT ENGROSSED
This bill has been extensively amended (hoghoused) and may no longer be
consistent with the original intention of the sponsor.
Introduced by: Senator Davis
1 An Act to require that counties provide written notice to township supervisors of
2 conditional use applications and appeals.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 11-2:
5 A county shall provide to each township supervisor who has an oath filed in the
6 office of the county auditor, pursuant to § 8-4-3, written notice of any application for the
7 conditional use of real property or any appeal filed pursuant to this chapter, for property
8 located in the township, at least ten days before the board of adjustment or other
9 approving authority holds a public hearing on the application or appeal.
