2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 237
ENROLLED
AN ACT
ENTITLED An Act to require that counties provide written notice to township
supervisors of conditional use applications and appeals.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 11-2:
A county shall provide to each township supervisor who has an oath filed in the
office of the county auditor, pursuant to § 8-4-3, written notice of any application for the
conditional use of real property or any appeal filed pursuant to this chapter, for property
located in the township, at least ten days before the board of adjustment or other
approving authority holds a public hearing on the application or appeal.
