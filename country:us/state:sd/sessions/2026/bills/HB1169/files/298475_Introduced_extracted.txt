26.708.9 101st Legislative Session 1169
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1169
Introduced by: Representative Garcia
1 An Act to classify medetomidine as a Schedule III controlled substance and
2 establish permissible uses.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 34-20B:
5 Any material, compound, mixture, or preparation that contains medetomidine is a
6 Schedule III controlled drug or substance, except in the following cases:
7 (1) Dispensing, prescribing, or administering, to an animal, a drug that contains
8 medetomidine and has been approved by the United States secretary of health and
9 human services under 21 U.S.C. § 360b (January 1, 2026);
10 (2) Dispensing, prescribing, or administering medetomidine to an animal, as
11 permissible under 21 U.S.C. § 360b(a)(4) (January 1, 2026);
12 (3) Manufacturing, distributing, or using medetomidine as an active pharmaceutical
13 ingredient for manufacturing an animal drug:
14 (a) Approved under 21 U.S.C. § 360b (January 1, 2026); or
15 (b) Issued an investigational use exemption under 21 U.S.C. § 360b(j)
16 (January 1, 2026);
17 (4) Manufacturing, distributing, or using a medetomidine bulk chemical for
18 pharmaceutical compounding by a licensed pharmacist or veterinarian; or
19 (5) Any other use approved or permissible under 21 U.S.C. §§ 301 to 399i, inclusive
20 (January 1, 2026).
