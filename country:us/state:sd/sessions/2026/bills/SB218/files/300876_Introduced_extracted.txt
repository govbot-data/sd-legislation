3 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to a NEW CHAPTER in title 13:
4 Terms used in this chapter mean:
5 (1) "Authorizing entity," the board of the school district in which an approved charter

7 (2) "Charter school," a public school that is established and operated under the terms

13 district, or the board of a school district.

20 kindergarten and grade twelve, inclusive;
21 (4) Follow the same operational term as provided for in § 13-26-2;
22 (5) Follow all applicable state and federal laws governing the provision of education to
23 children with disabilities;
24 (6) Provide for the measurement of student progress in a manner required by the
25 Department of Education;

25 for special education services, if the Department of Education is the authorizing entity of
26 the charter school. If the authorizing entity of a charter school is a school district, the
27 school district is the local education agency.
28 The charter school shall provide services to students as required by federal and
29 state law and the charter school contract.
