1 Section 3. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 42-7:
2 Only a person licensed as a multi-jurisdictional totalizator hub in this state may
3 operate an online betting platform or electronic application to offer online pari-mutuel
4 wagering on a horse or dog race.
5 A person with a physical presence in this state operating an online betting platform
6 or electronic application to offer online pari-mutuel wagering on a horse or dog race is
7 subject to the tax imposed pursuant to § 42-7-102 on multi-jurisdictional totalizator hubs.
8 A person without a physical presence in this state operating an online betting
9 platform or electronic application to offer online pari-mutuel wagering on a horse or dog
10 race is subject to the tax imposed pursuant to § 42-7-102 on multi-jurisdictional
11 totalizator hubs, except that the tax rate applies to the total contributed through the hub
12 by individuals in this state on online pari-mutuel wagering pools for horse or dog races.
