26.1021.11 101st Legislative Session SC810
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Commemoration 810
Introduced by: Senators Schoenfish, Deibert, Hulse, and Perry and Representative Muckey
1 A LEGISLATIVE COMMEMORATION commending and honoring the Hill City School
2 Board on its selection as the Associated School Boards of South Dakota
3 Outstanding School Board of South Dakota for 2025.
4 WHEREAS, the Hill City School Board has long been recognized for its efforts to promote
5 student achievement, to encourage teacher quality and retention, and to foster a culture of
6 positivity, all while being good stewards of the people's tax dollars; and
7 WHEREAS, the board approved the implementation of targeted interventions that resulted
8 in measurable academic gains at both the elementary and high school levels, while also being
9 successful at increasing support for problem areas, increasing and maintaining opportunities
10 for professional development, and cultivating a culture of positivity; and
11 WHEREAS, the Hill City School Board has received the Associated School Boards of South
12 Dakota Gold Level ALL Award for six consecutive years, which proves their commitment to
13 education and to the betterment of their board skills;
14 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT COMMEMORATED, by the One Hundred First Legislature of the
15 State of South Dakota, that the Hill City School Board is recognized for being named as the
16 Outstanding School Board of South Dakota and for its efforts to enhance the education of
17 students in the district.
