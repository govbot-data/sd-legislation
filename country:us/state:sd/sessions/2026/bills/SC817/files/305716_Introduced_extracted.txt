26.1040.9 101st Legislative Session SC817
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Commemoration 817
Introduced by: Senator Jensen (Kevin)
1 A LEGISLATIVE COMMEMORATION honoring the 2025-2026 Canton C-Hawk girls
2 wrestling team.
3 WHEREAS, the Canton C-Hawk girls won the Region 2 regional tournament for the second
4 consecutive year in February 2026; and
5 WHEREAS, the Canton C-Hawk girls had thirteen disciplined and dedicated wrestlers
6 qualify for the state tournament; and
7 WHEREAS, the 2026 C-Hawk state qualifiers were Jewel Gannon (100 pounds), Charlee
8 Ann Winter (105 pounds), Avery Kranz (110 pounds), Nayeli Ortega (115 pounds), Brooke
9 Warejcka (120 pounds), Finley Evjen (125 pounds), Marin Rhode (130 pounds), Kenzie King
10 (140 pounds), Lakynn Qualseth (145 pounds), Alyssa McCracken (155 pounds), Sophia Larsen
11 (170 pounds), Natalia Long (190 pounds), and Kori Wallner (235 pounds); and
12 WHEREAS, Brooke Warejcka (120 pounds) and Finley Evjen (125 pounds) won state
13 championships for the C-Hawks; and
14 WHEREAS, the C-Hawk girls team lived up to the high expectations set by coaches Camron
15 Groenke, Jeremy Ask, Brandon Jarman, and Jason Bartels;
16 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT COMMEMORATED, by the One Hundred First Legislature of the
17 State of South Dakota, that the Canton C-Hawk girls are great competitors, great leaders,
18 and the 2026 State B Wrestling Tournament champions.
