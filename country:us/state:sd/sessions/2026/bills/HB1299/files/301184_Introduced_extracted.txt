26.775.9 101st Legislative Session 1299
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1299
Introduced by: Representative Uhre‑Balk
1 An Act to permit the use of biological filtration systems in water recreational
2 facilities located in certain lodging establishments.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 34-18:
5 A lodging establishment that has fifteen or fewer sleeping rooms may apply to the
6 department for a permit to use a biological filtration system to filter water in the
7 establishment's water recreational facility. Only an establishment that has been issued a
8 permit under this section may use a biological filtration system in a water recreational
9 facility. A permit issued under this section is valid for one year.
10 A water recreational facility that uses a biological filtration system must otherwise
11 meet the minimum public health and safety requirements for a water recreational facility,
12 established by the department pursuant to § 34-18-22.
13 When applying for a permit, an establishment shall submit to the department a
14 document that demonstrates how the proposed system meets water quality outcomes
15 established by the department.
16 A biological filtration system must be inspected by the department every year. If a
17 lodging establishment fails an inspection, the department must revoke the permit. An
18 establishment that fails an inspection may not reapply for a permit under this section for
19 one year.
20 The department shall promulgate rules pursuant to chapter 1-26 to establish:
21 (1) Application requirements for a permit to use a biological filtration system;
22 (2) Procedures for reapplying for a permit following a failed inspection;
23 (3) Requirements for the routine maintenance of a biological filtration system by a
24 lodging establishment;
25 (4) The permit fee, which may not exceed fifty dollars; and
26 (5) Water quality testing procedures for biological filtration systems.
