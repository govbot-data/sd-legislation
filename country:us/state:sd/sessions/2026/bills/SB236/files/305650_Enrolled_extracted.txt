AN ACT
ENTITLED An Act to create the county and township infrastructure fund for the
accounting, safekeeping, and allocation of unobligated rural access
infrastructure fund moneys.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
Section 1. That § 31-34-3 be AMENDED:
31-34-3. Each county shall establish a rural access infrastructure fund for the
deposit of moneys received pursuant to this chapter. The board of county commissioners
may only distribute fund moneys for the following expenses:
(1) Engineering, hydrological studies, planning, materials, and other costs as
necessary to plan for and complete the projects;
(2) Construction, rehabilitation, or replacement of small structures located in
townships complying with the requirements of this chapter; and
(3) Construction, rehabilitation, or replacement of small structures described in a
county highway and bridge improvement plan, which are located on county
secondary highways.
The moneys may not be used on a no-maintenance road.
On June thirtieth of each third year, beginning in 2029, the county auditor shall
transfer any unexpended moneys, not contractually obligated and approved under this
chapter, to the state treasurer for deposit in the county and township infrastructure fund,
created in section 2 of this Act.
Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 32-11:
There is created in the state treasury the county and township infrastructure fund.
The fund consists of unobligated moneys transferred pursuant to § 31-34-3. Expenditures
from the fund must be budgeted through the general appropriation bill. The Department
of Revenue shall administer the fund. The purpose of the fund is to:

(1) Provide for the distribution of moneys to counties, in accordance with § 31-34-2;
and
(2) Provide for the accounting and safekeeping of unobligated or unspent moneys
