2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 173
Introduced by: Senator Hohn
1 An Act to extend the motor vehicle and motorcycle licensing fee available to a
2 veteran with a disability to the spouse of a veteran with a disability.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 32-5-157.1 be AMENDED:
5 32-5-157.1. A disabled veteran with a disability, or the spouse of a veteran with
6 a disability, may obtain standard issue county motor vehicle or motorcycle license plates
7 in lieu of disabled veteran plates if the disabled veteran by paying the fees according to
8 § 32-5-160, if the veteran with the disability meets the requirements listed in §§ 32-5-
9 156 and 32-5-157. A disabled veteran who obtains plates pursuant to this section shall
10 pay fees according to § 32-5-160.
