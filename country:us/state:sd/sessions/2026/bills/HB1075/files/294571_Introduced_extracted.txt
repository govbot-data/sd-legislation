26.149.20 101st Legislative Session 1075
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1075
Introduced by: Representative Novstrup
1 An Act to require notification of property owners prior to the establishment of a tax
2 increment financing district.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 11-9:
5 At least ten days prior to the adoption of a resolution to establish a district, the
6 governing body shall provide written notice to each owner of property located within the
7 boundaries of the district, as described in the resolution required by § 11-9-5.
8 The notice must state that the owner's property is located within the proposed
9 district, and the date on which the district is to be established.
