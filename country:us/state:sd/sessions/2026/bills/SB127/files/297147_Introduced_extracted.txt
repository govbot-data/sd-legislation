26.14.24 101st Legislative Session 127
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 127
Introduced by: Senator Howard
1 An Act to limit nuisances caused by data centers.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 21-10:
4 Terms used in sections 1 to 4, inclusive, of this Act mean:
5 (1) "Data center," a centralized repository for the dissemination, management,
6 processing, and storage of electronic data and information, including:
7 (a) Application, cloud, or web hosting;
8 (b) Cloud computing;
9 (c) Colocation services;
10 (d) Cryptocurrency mining;
11 (e) Media streaming support;
12 (f) Platform as a service;
13 (g) Software as a service; and
14 (h) Any other comparable digital service.; and
15 (2) "Residential area," any area designated or zoned for residential use pursuant to
16 local law or zoning ordinance.
17 Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 21-10:
18 Effective July 1, 2026, a facility housing a data center may not be established within
19 one mile of any residential area, but a county, municipality, or township may adopt more
20 restrictive data center siting requirements.
21 Section 3. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 21-10:
22 A data center may not exceed a continuous noise level of forty-five decibels,
23 measured according to federal or local environmental standards, at the nearest property
24 line of a residential area.
Underscores indicate new language.
Overstrikes indicate deleted language.

26.14.24 2 127
1 Section 4. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 21-10:
2 A data center in violation of section 2 or 3 of this Act is declared a nuisance. The
3 state's attorney or any affected citizen may maintain an action against the operator of the
4 data center, and against the owner of the building in which or ground upon which the
5 nuisance exists, to enjoin the nuisance.
