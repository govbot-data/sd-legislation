26.538.17 101st Legislative Session 74
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 74
HOUSE APPROPRIATIONS ENGROSSED
Introduced by: The Chair of the Senate Committee on Appropriations at the request of the Bureau of Finance and
Management
1 An Act to transfer moneys to the general fund from the budget reserve fund and the
2 general revenue replacement fund, and to declare an emergency.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. The state treasurer shall transfer $101 from the budget reserve fund to the general
5 fund.
6 Section 2. The state treasurer shall transfer $101 from the general revenue replacement
7 fund to the general fund.
8 Section 3. Whereas, this Act is necessary for the support of the state government and its
9 existing public institutions, an emergency is hereby declared to exist, and this Act shall be in
10 full force and effect from and after its passage and approval.
