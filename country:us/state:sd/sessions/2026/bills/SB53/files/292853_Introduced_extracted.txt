26.553.12 101st Legislative Session 53
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 53
Introduced by: The Chair of the Committee on Retirement Laws at the request of the South Dakota Retirement
System
1 An Act to ensure uniformity in member identification provisions governing the South
2 Dakota Retirement System.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 3-12C-602 be AMENDED:
5 3-12C-602. A member who has left covered employment may withdraw the sum
6 of the member's accumulated contributions upon application to the system and submission
7 of a copy of the member's current driver license or other picture identification card issued
8 by a government agency or tribe executive director, on a form approved by the system.
9 If the member is married, the member's spouse shall sign the application and provide a
10 copy of the spouse's current driver license or other picture identification card issued by a
11 government agency or tribe must sign the form.
12 A member who withdraws the member's accumulated contributions pursuant to
13 this section forfeits all credited service and benefits provided in this chapter.
14 The right to withdraw accumulated contributions ceases if the member returns to
15 covered employment with a participating unit.
16 Section 2. That § 3-12C-1208 be AMENDED:
17 3-12C-1208. No retirement benefit may be paid unless the system has received
18 a completed application for a retirement benefit, including the benefit option elected. The
19 application must be signed by the generational member, and the member shall provide a
20 copy of the member's current driver license or other picture identification card issued by
21 a government agency or tribe made to the executive director, on a form approved by the
22 system. If the member is married, the member's spouse must sign the application and
23 provide a copy of the spouse's current driver license or other picture identification card
24 issued by a government agency or tribe form.
Underscores indicate new language.
Overstrikes indicate deleted language.
