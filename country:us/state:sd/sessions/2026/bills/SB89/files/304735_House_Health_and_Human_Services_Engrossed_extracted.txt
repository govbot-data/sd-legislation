26.404.15 101st Legislative Session 89
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 89
HOUSE HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES ENGROSSED
Introduced by: Senator Reed
1 An Act to create a task force to study the provision of emergency medical services
2 as an essential service and the funding thereof.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. The Executive Board of the Legislative Research Council shall establish the
5 Emergency Medical Services Funding Task Force. The task force shall:
6 (1) Examine mechanisms to fund counties and municipalities for the provision of
7 emergency medical services as an essential service;
8 (2) Examine policies for ambulance service payments, including reimbursement
9 standards for out of network emergency medical services;
10 (3) Seek input from relevant stakeholders on the provision of emergency medical
11 services as an essential service;
12 (4) Provide to the executive board, no later than November 1, 2026, a report
13 containing findings and recommendations for legislative proposals related to the
14 funding of emergency medical services in this state; and
15 (5) Review planned expenditures of the Rural Health Transformation program and
16 evaluate opportunities to incorporate the funds into emergency medical services
17 funding strategies.
18 The task force shall meet no more than five times during the 2026 interim. The
19 task force is dissolved upon the delivery of the task force's report to the executive board.
20 Section 2. The Emergency Medical Services Funding Task Force consists of:
21 (1) The chairs of the health and human services committees of the House of
22 Representatives and the Senate;
23 (2) The chairs of the taxation committees of the House of Representatives and the
24 Senate;
