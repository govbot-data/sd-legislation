26.896.12 101st Legislative Session 1284
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1284
Introduced by: Representative Weisgram
1 An Act to provide that an alcoholic beverage may be carried and consumed in
2 enterprises connected to and in the same building as a bar or lounge.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 35-4:
5 An individual may carry and consume an alcoholic beverage purchased in a bar or
6 lounge, as described in § 42-7A-37.1, in any other connected enterprise, owned or
7 operated by the same person, within the same building.
