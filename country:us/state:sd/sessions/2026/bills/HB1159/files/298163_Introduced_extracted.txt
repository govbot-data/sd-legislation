26.756.9 101st Legislative Session 1159
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1159
Introduced by: Representative Goodwin
1 An Act to impose a moratorium on industrial dairy operations.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, a moratorium is imposed
4 on any new or expanding industrial dairy operation that is permitted as a concentrated
5 animal feeding operation under chapter 34A-2 and granted a conditional use permit by
6 any county in this state, if the number of animal units at the new or expanding operation
7 is to exceed seven thousand four hundred ninety-nine dairy cows.
8 Currently permitted and operating dairy operations may continue to operate at the
9 scale identified in any permit approved prior to July 1, 2026.
10 The purpose of the moratorium is to:
11 (1) Allow the Department of Agriculture and Natural Resources to undertake an
12 interdisciplinary process of data collection, review, consideration, and analysis of
13 the potential economic and environmental impacts of industrial dairy operations in
14 this state, and to make results available to the public; and
15 (2) Allow county governments time to update comprehensive land use plans and
16 ordinances to match the scale of industrial dairy operations by:
17 (a) Requiring road agreements between townships and industrial dairy
18 operations as part of conditional use permit approvals; and
19 (b) Implementing incremental, increasing setbacks proportional to the size of
20 an industrial dairy operation; and
21 (3) Allow the Department of Agriculture and Natural Resources to determine whether
22 it has adequate resources to determine the impact of industrial dairy operations on
23 the watersheds of this state.
24 For purposes of this section, "industrial dairy operation" means a dairy with more
25 than seven thousand four hundred ninety-nine head of dairy cows.
26 The moratorium is in effect through June 30, 2028.
