26.467.10 101st Legislative Session 1325
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1325
Introduced by: Representative Jordan
1 An Act to prohibit the Board of Regents and institutions under its control from
2 requiring certain students to reside in on-campus housing or purchase meal
3 plans.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
5 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 13-53:
6 Neither the Board of Regents nor any institution under its control may require that
7 a postsecondary student live in on-campus housing, or purchase a meal plan or meal
8 voucher, beyond the student's first year of attendance at an institution.
