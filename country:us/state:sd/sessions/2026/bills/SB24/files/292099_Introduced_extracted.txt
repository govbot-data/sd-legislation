26.378.15 101st Legislative Session 24
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 24
Introduced by: The Chair of the Committee on Transportation at the request of the Department of Revenue
1 An Act to create a solid background license plate for South Dakota.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 32-5:
4 A motor vehicle owner who is a resident of this state, has a South Dakota driver
5 license, and has complied with all laws of this state in relation to the registration of a
6 motor vehicle may receive a solid background license plate. The solid background license
7 plates may be issued only upon proof of payment of the current registration fees.
8 Solid background license plates may be used only on automobiles, pickup trucks,
9 or vans licensed pursuant to § 32-5-6; motorcycles licensed pursuant to § 32-5-9; and
10 pickup trucks licensed pursuant to § 32-5-6.3. Solid background license plates must be
11 displayed in accordance with § 32-5-98.
12 Annual renewal of solid background license plates must be in accordance with the
13 provisions of this chapter.
14 Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 32-5:
15 The fee for solid background license plates is one hundred dollars at the initial
16 issuance of the plates and one hundred dollars at the time of each annual registration
17 renewal.
18 Twenty-five dollars from each initial fee and each annual registration renewal fee
19 must be deposited into the license plate special revenue fund.
20 Seventy-five dollars from each initial fee and each annual registration renewal fee
21 must be deposited into the state highway fund.
22 Section 3. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 32-5:
23 The Department of Revenue shall design the solid background license plates in
24 accordance with § 32-5-86. The department shall create and offer:

3 Section 4. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 32-5:
4 An owner may choose to personalize a solid background license plate for an
5 additional initial fee of twenty-five dollars. The personalized plate message must comply
6 with the requirements for special personalized license plates in § 32-5-89.2. All fees
7 collected pursuant to this section must be credited to the license plate special revenue
8 fund.
9 Section 5. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 32-5:
10 The solid background license plates may be transferred and replaced in the same
11 manner as emblem specialty plates pursuant to §§ 32-5-171 to 32-5-173, inclusive.
