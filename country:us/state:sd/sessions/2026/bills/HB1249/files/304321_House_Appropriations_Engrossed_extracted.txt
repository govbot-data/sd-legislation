2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1249
HOUSE APPROPRIATIONS ENGROSSED
Introduced by: Representative Kassin
1 An Act to amend provisions pertaining to the School Finance Accountability Board,
2 and the process by which a recommendation of the board is approved.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That § 1-45-38 be AMENDED:
5 1-45-38. There is hereby created the School Finance Accountability Board within
6 the Department of Education. The board shall consist consists of five members appointed
7 by the Governor. The members shall serve a term of four years.
8 The board may recommend that the provisions of § 13-13-73.5 be waived for a
9 school district if the district can demonstrate that its lowest monthly general cash fund
10 cash balance percentage is the result of special circumstances.
11 The board may recommend that a penalty against a school district imposed under
12 § 13-13-73.9 be waived, in whole or in part, if the district can demonstrate that its failure
13 to comply with § 13-13-73.6 or 13-13-73.8 is due to special circumstances. The board
14 may recommend the Department of Education review the accreditation of a school district
15 that is found not to be complying with §§ 13-13-73.5, 13-13-73.6, or 13-13-73.8.
16 The School Finance Accountability Board shall promulgate rules pursuant to chapter
17 1-26 to establish the appeals process provided for in § 13-13-73.9, and to establish the
18 factors that may be considered in considering a waiver requested by a school district,
19 which shall include the impact of retirements.
20 The Joint Committee on Appropriations or the Interim Committee on Appropriations
21 shall review any waivers of § 13-13-73.5, 13-13-73.6, or 13-13-73.8 recommended by
22 the School Finance Accountability Board. For a waiver recommended by the board under
23 this section, the committee may provide any suggested change to the waiver. Not more
24 than thirty days following receipt of a suggested change from the committee, the board
25 may amend the recommended waiver in accordance with the suggested change and shall
26 resubmit the recommended waiver. The Joint Committee on Appropriations or the Interim
