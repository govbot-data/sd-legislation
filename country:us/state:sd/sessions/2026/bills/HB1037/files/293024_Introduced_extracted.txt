26.600.9 101st Legislative Session 1037
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1037
Introduced by: Representative Ismay
1 An Act to exempt active-duty members of the armed forces of the United States and
2 veterans from paying a resident fishing license fee.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 41-6:
5 A resident of this state who is an active-duty member of the armed of forces of the
6 United States or a veteran who was discharged from active service in the armed forces of
7 the United States under conditions other than dishonorable is exempt from paying any
8 resident fishing license fee required by the Game, Fish and Parks Commission, pursuant
9 to § 41-6-10.
