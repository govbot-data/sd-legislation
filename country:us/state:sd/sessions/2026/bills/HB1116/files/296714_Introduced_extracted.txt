26.421.9 101st Legislative Session 1116
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1116
Introduced by: Representative Shubeck
1 An Act to prohibit a video streaming service from transmitting certain advertising
2 during children's programming and provide a penalty therefor.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 37-24:
5 Terms used in section 2 of this Act mean:
6 (1) "Children's programming," any content designed for an audience of individuals
7 under the age of sixteen; and
8 (2) "Video streaming service," an entity that makes either of the following available
9 directly to the consumer through a distribution method utilizing internet protocol:
10 (a) Video programming considered comparable to programming provided by a
11 television broadcast station; or
12 (b) Video content the service makes available for users to view.
13 The term does not include a television broadcast station, cable operator, or other
14 multichannel video programming distributor, or an entity that serves video
15 programming or video content without commercial advertisements.
16 Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 37-24:
17 A video streaming service may not transmit during any children's programming
18 any commercial advertisement that discusses, promotes, or contains imagery related to:
19 (1) Abortion;
20 (2) Alcohol;
21 (3) Drugs or pharmaceutical products;
22 (4) Gambling;
23 (5) Gender identity;
24 (6) Nicotine products; or
25 (7) Sexual behavior.
Underscores indicate new language.
Overstrikes indicate deleted language.

26.421.9 2 1116
1 Section 3. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 37-24:
2 The attorney general may impose a civil penalty against a video streaming service
3 for violating section 2 of this Act. The amount of the civil penalty may not exceed five
4 thousand dollars. The attorney general shall forward any civil penalty collected under this
5 section to the state treasurer, for deposit in the general fund.
