26.840.9 101st Legislative Session 1279
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1279
Introduced by: Representative Mortenson
1 An Act to permit technical college employees to join the state health plan.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That § 3-6E-1 be AMENDED:
4 3-6E-1. The bureau may establish a benefits plan for plan-eligible employees of
5 the state and technical college employees, pursuant to section 2 of this Act. The plan may
6 either be self-funded or established as a group health insurance program. The plan may
7 provide for group health coverage against the financial cost of hospital, surgical,
8 pharmacy, and medical treatment and care, and any other coverage or benefits the human
9 resources commissioner determines is appropriate and desirable. The human resources
10 commissioner may include a flexible benefit plan which that allows an employee to choose
11 the employee's own benefits or levels of coverage.
12 Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 3-6E:
13 Notwithstanding any other sections of law, a technical college employee may join
14 the plan.
15 Except as otherwise provided in this section, a technical college employee who
16 participates in the plan is considered a plan-eligible employee under this chapter.
17 In lieu of the monthly state contribution described in § 3-6E-8, the LEA, as defined
18 in § 13-39A-1, that employs a technical college employee shall contribute to the system
19 an amount equivalent to the monthly contribution provided by the state for other plan-
20 eligible employees.
21 In lieu of a payroll deduction, as described under § 3-6E-9, a technical college
22 employee enrolled in the plan shall pay to the state treasurer any premium or contribution
23 required by the human resources commissioner, pursuant to § 3-6E-9.
24 A technical college or LEA, may not restrict a technical college employee from
25 joining the plan.
