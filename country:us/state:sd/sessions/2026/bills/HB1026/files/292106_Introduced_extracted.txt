26.264.26 101st Legislative Session 1026
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1026
Introduced by: Representative Roe
1 An Act to provide for the licensing of property data collectors and the registration
2 of property data collection companies and to provide a penalty therefor.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to a NEW CHAPTER in title 36:
5 Terms used in this chapter mean:
6 (1) "Department," the Department of Labor and Regulation;
7 (2) "Property data collection," the:
8 (a) Physical or virtual visual observation of a specified parcel of real estate and
9 the improvements located thereon, for use as part of a real estate-related
10 financial transaction; and
11 (b) Preparation of a property data collection report that is transmitted to a
12 property data collection company, and does not involve the practice of real
13 estate appraisal, as defined in § 36-21B-2;
14 (3) "Property data collection company," an entity that employs, contracts, retains, or
15 otherwise engages one or more property data collectors to perform a property data
16 collection and which is retained by a client to manage the process of having a
17 property data collection performed;
18 (4) "Property data collector," an individual licensed by the department to perform a
19 property data collection; and
20 (5) "Real estate-related financial transaction," any agreement involving:
21 (a) The sale, lease, purchase, auction, finance, exchange of, or investment in,
22 real estate, including interests in real estate and the financing thereof;
23 (b) The refinancing of real estate or interests in real estate; and
24 (c) The use of real estate or interests in real estate as security for a loan or
25 investment, including mortgage-backed securities.
26 Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to a NEW CHAPTER in title 36:
