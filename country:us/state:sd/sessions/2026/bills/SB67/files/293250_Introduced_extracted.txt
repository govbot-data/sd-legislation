26.395.13 101st Legislative Session 67
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 67
Introduced by: The Chair of the Senate Committee on Appropriations at the request of the Department of Public
Safety
1 An Act to make an appropriation for costs related to emergencies and disasters
2 impacting the state and to declare an emergency.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. There is appropriated $4,205,497 from the general fund, to the special emergency
5 and disaster special revenue fund, for costs related to any emergency or disaster, as defined
6 in § 34-48A-1, impacting the state.
7 Section 2. The secretary of the Department of Public Safety shall approve vouchers and the
8 state auditor shall draw warrants to pay expenditures authorized in this Act.
9 Section 3. Whereas, this Act is necessary for the support of the state government and its
10 existing public institutions, an emergency is hereby declared to exist, and this Act shall be in
11 full force and effect from and after its passage and approval.
