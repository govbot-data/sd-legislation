5 32-22-51.2. The unloading of livestock as defined by subdivision 39-5-6(11) in
6 § 39-5-6, is prohibited for the enforcement of violations of §§ 32-22-16 and 32-22-21.

8 overweight permit for the amount of the overload if the load cannot be adjusted to the
