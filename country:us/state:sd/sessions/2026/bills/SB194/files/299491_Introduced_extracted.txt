26.327.11 101st Legislative Session 194
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 194
Introduced by: Senator Carley
1 An Act to limit the potency of cannabis products sold at medical cannabis
2 dispensaries.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
4 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 34-20G:
5 A medical cannabis dispensary licensed under this chapter may not acquire,
6 cultivate, deliver, dispense, distribute, possess, produce, sell, store, supply, transfer, or
7 transport:
8 (1) Cannabis flower that exceeds thirty percent tetrahydrocannabinol;
9 (2) A liquid concentrate cannabis product that exceeds sixty percent
10 tetrahydrocannabinol;
11 (3) An edible cannabis product that exceeds five milligrams tetrahydrocannabinol per
12 serving;
13 (4) An edible cannabis product that exceeds one hundred milligrams
14 tetrahydrocannabinol per container; and
15 (5) A cannabis oil that exceeds five percent tetrahydrocannabinol.
Underscores indicate new language.
