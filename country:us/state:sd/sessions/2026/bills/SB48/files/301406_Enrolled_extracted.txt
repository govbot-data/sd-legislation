26.463.10 101st Legislative Session 48
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 48
ENROLLED
AN ACT
ENTITLED An Act to clarify that an official open meeting agenda must be posted
online at least seventy-two hours before the scheduled start of the meeting.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
Section 1. That § 1-25-1.3 be AMENDED:
1-25-1.3. The state shall provide public notice of a meeting, at least seventy-two
hours before the meeting is scheduled to start, not including Saturday, Sunday, or any
legal holiday, by posting a copy of the proposed agenda:
(1) At the principal office of the board, commission, or department holding the
meeting;
(2) That includes the date, time, and location of the meeting;
(3) That is visible, readable, and accessible to the public; and
(4) On a state website, designated by the commissioner of the Bureau of Finance and
Management.
For any special or rescheduled meeting, the information in the notice must be
delivered in person, or by mail, email, or telephone, to members of the local news media
who have requested notice. The state shall also comply with all other requirements of this
section, for a special or rescheduled meeting, as circumstances permit.
A violation of this section is a Class 2 misdemeanor.
