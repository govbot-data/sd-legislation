House Commemoration 8017
Introduced by: Representatives Aylward, Emery, Greenfield, Jordan, Moore, Muckey, Novstrup, and Roby and
Senators Duhamel and Perry
A LEGISLATIVE COMMEMORATION commending the Harrisburg Tigers on their 2026
Class AA state gymnastics championship.
WHEREAS, the Harrisburg Tigers gymnastics team displayed another year of supremacy,
again winning the Eastern South Dakota Conference and Metro Conference meets; and
WHEREAS, the Harrisburg Tigers gymnastics team's score of 151.4834 secured their
second Class AA state gymnastics title; and
WHEREAS, outstanding performances in the individual competitions led to first-place
finishes for Katelyn Maeschen in the Class AA all around, vault, bars, beam, and floor
competitions; a second-place finish for Lilly Gusso in the floor competition; and a third-place
finish in the beam competition for Emery Myron; and
WHEREAS, Ailee Johnson, Jersy Tryon, Hadley Moody, Jaycie Adams, Laela Vincent,
Kennedy Malloy, and Manager Miranda Fokken all made exceptional contributions to the team;
and
WHEREAS, the team's success was driven by the stalwart coaching of head coach Callie
Aylward and assistant coaches Abby Christiansen and Hailey Handevidt;
NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT COMMEMORATED, by the One Hundred First Legislature of the
State of South Dakota, that the Harrisburg Tigers gymnastics team is the 2026 Class AA state
gymnastics champion.
