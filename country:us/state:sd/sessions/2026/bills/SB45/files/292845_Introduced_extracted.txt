26.460.10 101st Legislative Session 45
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 45
Introduced by: The Chair of the Committee on Judiciary at the request of the Attorney General
1 An Act to revise a provision regulating delta-8 tetrahydrocannabinol, THC-O acetate,
2 and hexahydrocannabinol for persons under the age of twenty-one and to
3 provide a penalty therefor.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
5 Section 1. That § 34-20B-117 be AMENDED:
6 34-20B-117. The following actions are unlawful:
7 (1) To knowingly sell or distribute a product intended for human consumption
8 containing delta-8 tetrahydrocannabinol, delta-9 tetrahydrocannabinolic acid, THC-
9 O acetate, or hexahydrocannabinol to a person under the age of twenty-one;
10 (2) The purchase or attempt to purchase, the receipt or attempt to receive, the
11 possession, or the consumption of, a product intended for human consumption
12 containing delta-8 tetrahydrocannabinol, delta-9 tetrahydrocannabinolic acid, THC-
13 O acetate, or hexahydrocannabinol to by a person under the age of twenty-one;
14 and
15 (3) To purchase a product intended for human consumption containing delta-8
16 tetrahydrocannabinol, delta-9 tetrahydrocannabinolic acid, THC-O acetate, or
17 hexahydrocannabinol on behalf of, or to give a product intended for human
18 consumption containing delta-8 tetrahydrocannabinol, THC-O acetate, or
19 hexahydrocannabinol to, any person under the age of twenty-one, unless the
20 purchaser is a parent or guardian of the person under the age of twenty-one.
21 A violation of this section subdivision (1) is a Class 6 felony. A violation of
22 subdivision (2) or (3) is a Class 2 misdemeanor.
