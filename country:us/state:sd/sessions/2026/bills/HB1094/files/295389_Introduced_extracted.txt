26.189.11 101st Legislative Session 1094
2026 South Dakota Legislature
House Bill 1094
Introduced by: Representative Garcia
1 An Act to clarify that consent for the donation of ovum or sperm must be explicit.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That a NEW SECTION be added to chapter 34-26:
4 An individual's consent to be an organ donor does not constitute consent for the
5 donation or retrieval of the individual's ovum or sperm, unless the individual explicitly
6 consents to the donation or retrieval of ovum or sperm.
