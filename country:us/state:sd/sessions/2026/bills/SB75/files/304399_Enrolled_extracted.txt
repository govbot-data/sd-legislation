26.539.20 101st Legislative Session 75
2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 75
ENROLLED
AN ACT
ENTITLED An Act to amend the type of entities eligible to participate in the
cybersecurity services initiative.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
Section 1. That 2024 Session Laws, chapter 229, § 1 be AMENDED:
Section 1. There is hereby appropriated from the general fund the sum of
$7,000,000 to the Office of the Attorney General, for the purpose of creating a
cybersecurity services initiative in this state.
Section 2. That 2024 Session Laws, chapter 229, § 2 be AMENDED:
Section 2. The moneys appropriated in section 1 of this Act must be used
exclusively to expand and improve cybersecurity for counties, nonprofit entities providing
drinking water and sanitary sewer utilities, utilities operated by a political subdivision, and
municipalities in the State of South Dakota, via any infrastructure and technology that can
protect the information technology assets of this state, and for administrative costs
necessary for expending these dollars efficiently and effectively.
Section 3. That 2024 Session Laws, chapter 229, § 3 be AMENDED:
Section 3. Moneys must be disbursed according to the specific needs of the project
and in such a way that best advances the cybersecurity needs of the state.
