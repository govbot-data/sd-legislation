1 An Act to modify the administration of the South Dakota-Ireland trade fund.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That § 1-57-2 be AMENDED:
4 1-57-2. The South Dakota-Ireland Trade Commission consists of:
5 (1) Two members of the Senate, appointed by the president pro tempore of the
6 Senate;
7 (2) Two members of the House of Representatives, appointed by the speaker of the
8 House;
9 (3) A representative of a private sector association headquartered in this state and
10 focused on the promotion of international trade, appointed by the Executive Board
11 of the Legislative Research Council;
12 (4) An officer of the Executive Branch, appointed by the Governor;
13 (5) A representative of a South Dakota private industry, appointed by the president
14 pro tempore of the Senate; and
15 (6) A representative of a South Dakota private industry, appointed by the speaker of
16 the House of Representatives.
17 Each appointed member shall serve for a term of two years and may be reappointed
18 without limit. Each term begins upon appointment in an odd-numbered year and expires
19 on December thirty-first of the following even-numbered year. If a position becomes
20 vacant, the position must be filled, for the duration of the term, in the same manner as
21 the original appointment.
22 Section 2. That § 1-57-3 be AMENDED:
23 1-57-3. The Executive Board of the Legislative Research Council shall provide
24 administrative services to the South Dakota-Ireland Trade Commission.
