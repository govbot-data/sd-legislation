5 10-1-28.2. All lists of taxpayers, licensees, or applicants compiled by the

10 Section 2. That a NEW SECTION be added to a NEW CHAPTER in title 10:
11 Terms used in this chapter mean:
12 (1) "Licensee," a person licensed under the provisions of this chapter;
13 (2) "Nicotine product," a product intended for human consumption, containing any
14 form of the chemical nicotine, including any sale or complex, from any source,
15 nicotinic alkaloids, nicotine analogs, and any substance intended to mimic or
16 replicate the pharmacological effect of nicotine. The term includes components,
17 parts, or accessories. The term does not include drugs, devices, or combination
18 products, as defined in the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act, approved for
19 sale by the United States Food and Drug Administration. Any communication by,
20 or on behalf of, the manufacturer distributor, or retailer of a tobacco product that
21 indicates that the product contains a nicotine analog constitutes presumptive
22 evidence that the product contains a nicotine analog;
23 (3) "Retailer," a person engaged in the business of selling a nicotine product to an
24 individual for any purpose other than resale.
