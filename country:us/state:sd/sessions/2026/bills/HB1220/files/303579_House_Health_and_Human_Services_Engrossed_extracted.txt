4 10-1-28.2. All lists of taxpayers, licensees, or applicants compiled by the

8 extent necessary to carry out the official duties of the department.

21 form of the chemical nicotine, including any sale or complex, from any source,
22 nicotinic alkaloids, nicotine analogs, and any substance intended to mimic or
23 replicate the pharmacological effect of nicotine. The term includes components,
24 parts, or accessories. The term does not include drugs, devices, or combination

21 (4) Be current on the remittance of all applicable state and local taxes.

1 Section 7. That a NEW SECTION be added to a NEW CHAPTER in title 10:
2 A nicotine product distributor shall maintain, for at least three years, all
3 documentation detailing the manufacturer, address of the manufacturer, ingredients, and
4 sales, of all nicotine products. A nicotine product distributor shall submit to an inspection
5 of inventory, products, and documentation, and shall provide a copy of the documentation
6 to the department, upon request.
7 A violation of this section is a Class 2 misdemeanor.
8 Section 8. That a NEW SECTION be added to a NEW CHAPTER in title 10:
9 The secretary may revoke a nicotine product distributor license or the registration
10 of any nicotine product retailer for failure to comply with any provision of state law.
11 A person aggrieved by the revocation may, in writing, request a contested case
12 hearing before the secretary. The request must be received by the department within
13 fourteen days from the date the notice of revocation was mailed to the licensee by certified
14 mail. If a request for a hearing is not received by the department within the time
15 prescribed, the revocation becomes final. Any hearing must be conducted, and any appeal
16 must be taken, pursuant to the provisions of chapters 1-26 and 1-26D.
17 A person who sells nicotine products without a valid license or registration is guilty
18 of a Class 1 misdemeanor.
19 Section 9. That a NEW SECTION be added to a NEW CHAPTER in title 10:
20 A nicotine product retailer may buy or receive nicotine products only from a nicotine
21 product distributor. A violation of this section is a Class 2 misdemeanor.
22 Section 10. That a NEW SECTION be added to a NEW CHAPTER in title 10:
23 A person who is engaged in the business of selling or distributing nicotine products
24 may not ship or transport, or cause to be shipped or transported, nicotine products directly
25 to any consumer in this state. This section applies regardless of whether the person
26 engaged in the business of selling or distributing nicotine products is located within or
27 outside of this state.

8 Section 12. That a NEW SECTION be added to a NEW CHAPTER in title 10:

11 Section 13. That a NEW SECTION be added to a NEW CHAPTER in title 10:
12 Unless otherwise expressly provided, the penalties or remedies, or both, under
13 sections 11 and 12 of this Act, inclusive, are in addition to any other penalties and
14 remedies available under any other law of this state. Nothing in sections 11 and 12 of this
15 Act, inclusive, prohibits the collection of any applicable tax against a person receiving
16 nicotine products in violation of this chapter.
17 Section 14. That a NEW SECTION be added to a NEW CHAPTER in title 10:
18 There is created a nicotine enforcement fund. The Department of Revenue shall
19 administer the fund. The purpose of the fund is to defray expenses associated with the
20 enforcement of this chapter.
21 Moneys collected under this Act must be forwarded to the state treasurer to be
22 deposited into the fund. Interest on moneys credited to the fund must remain in the fund.
23 Expenditures from the fund must be budgeted through the general appropriation
24 bill.
