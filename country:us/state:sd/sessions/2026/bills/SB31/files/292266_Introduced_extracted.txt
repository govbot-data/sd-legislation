2026 South Dakota Legislature
Senate Bill 31
Introduced by: The Chair of the Committee on State Affairs at the request of the Secretary of State
1 An Act to permit the use of a confirmation letter to verify a voter's registration.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
3 Section 1. That § 12-4-19.1 be AMENDED:
4 12-4-19.1. The confirmation mailing notice shall be a double postcard stating that
5 the voter's registration may be canceled if the card is not returned. In addition, the card
6 shall state that if If the acknowledgement notice sent pursuant to § 12-4-5.3 is returned
7 to the county auditor as undeliverable, the auditor must send a confirmation mailing letter
8 or postcard that:
9 (1) States the voter's registration may be canceled if the letter or postcard is not
10 returned;
11 (2) Lists the voter's information, as it appears in the master registration file; and
12 (3) Provides the following instructions:
13 (a) If the information on the return letter or card is correct, the voter must sign
14 and return the letter or card within thirty days of the date of the postmark
15 on the letter or card, or the voter's registration will become inactive. The
16 card shall also state that if;
17 (b) If the information on the return letter or card is not correct, the voter shall
18 must send the correct information to update the voter's registration, within
19 thirty days of the date of the postmark on the letter or card, or the voter's
20 registration will become inactive.; and
21 (c) If the letter or card is returned indicating a new address in another county
22 in South Dakota or another state, the card shall serve serves as a
23 cancellation authorization.
24 The card shall also give information on reregistering if the voter has moved to
25 another county or state. The card shall give the information about the voter as it appears
26 in the registration records.
