1 An Act to require that an individual provide proof of citizenship when registering to
2 vote using the voter registration form prescribed by the State Board of

4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF SOUTH DAKOTA:
5 Section 1. That § 12-4-1 be AMENDED:
6 12-4-1. An individual who is a resident of this state, as defined in § 12-1-4, and
7 has or will have the qualifications of a voter prescribed by § 12-3-1 or 12-3-1.1 at the
8 next ensuing municipal, primary, general, or school district election, is entitled to be
9 registered as a voter in the election precinct in which the person maintains residenceAn
10 individual is entitled to register as a voter of this state if the individual:
11 (1) Is a citizen of the United States and has provided sufficient evidence, as set forth
12 in section 2 of this Act, to demonstrate the individual's citizenship;
13 (2) Is a resident, as defined in § 12-1-4, of the precinct in which the individual is
14 registering as a voter;
15 (3) On or before the next election, is at least eighteen years of age; and
16 (4) Is not otherwise disqualified from voting.
17 An individual eligible to vote may vote only in the election precinct where the
18 person maintains residence the individual is a resident.
19 An individual who is a resident of a place that is within the boundaries of this state,
20 which has been ceded to or acquired by the federal government, may not be denied the
21 right to vote in any election held in the precinct where the individual is a resident if the
22 individual is otherwise qualified to vote in the election.
